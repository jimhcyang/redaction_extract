1 JULY 1960

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC

II. ASIA-AFRICA

Sihanouk warns Cambodia will accept bloc military aid unless arms superiority of US-supplied neighbors is rectified.

Administrative stagnation reappearing in Burma.

III. THE WEST

Reported Cuban "military pact" with Czechoslovakia, even if only for training and supplies, would arouse anti-Cuban feeling in Latin America.

Austria to bring South Tirol dispute to UN.

LATE ITEM

Czech diplomat says USSR will break off Geneva nuclear talks "within the next few days."
