GENERAL

1. Former Nazi SS officers reportedly ready to serve in Egypt:

Comment: The reaction of the Nagib regime to this offer is not known. Extremist elements would be interested in any preparations which might make future Egyptian activities against Britain more effective. Should Anglo-Egyptian relations sharply deteriorate, even the more conservative members of the regime might approve acceptance of such an offer.

Approximately 70 German experts, not considered to have been Nazi sympathizers, are now employed by the Egyptian Army. They would be inclined not to cooperate with the SS group.
