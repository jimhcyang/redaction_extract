4. Soviet bloc delegate makes friendly overture to Israeli UN delegate:

Israeli UN delegate Rafael has told Ambassador Lodge that a member of the Soviet bloc has made overtures to the Israeli delegation that might lead to renewal of diplomatic relations between Israel and the USSR.

Comment: This may be the initial Soviet response to the publicly voiced Israeli hope for a resumption of relations.

EASTERN EUROPE

5. Orbit trade meeting in Switzerland reported:

Orbit countries are holding a meeting in Switzerland this week to coordinate procurement of strategic goods.

There is no evidence that this is connected with the meeting in Geneva of the UN Economic Commission for Europe.

Comment: Such a meeting at the time of the ECE sessions would indicate an attempt to coordinate approaches to Western European delegates as well as to commercial agents.
