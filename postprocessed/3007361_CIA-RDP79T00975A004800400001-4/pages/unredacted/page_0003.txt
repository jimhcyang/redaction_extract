17 DECEMBER 1959

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC
USSR-- Ambassador Thompson discusses Soviet attitudes on German question.
Poland asks US for emergency grain shipments.

II. ASIA-AFRICA
Indonesian "state-of-war" declaration is technicality continuing existing controls.
Laos-- Phoui forms new cabinet without participation by reformist CDNI.
Rhodesia-Nyasaland Federation seen headed for increasing racial tension.
Rival Nigerian leaders maneuvering for formation of coalition government.

III. THE WEST
De Gaulle reportedly considers US-French policy agreement on North Africa "impossible."
