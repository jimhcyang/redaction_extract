CENTRAL INTELLIGENCE BULLETIN

21 May 1958

DAILY BRIEF

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC

*Communist bloc meetings: The meetings in Moscow this week of top level satellite figures probably have been called to consider broad ideological, political, and economic issues bearing on the question of bloc unity. Authoritative pronouncements may be issued on closer bloc economic integration, Yugoslav heresies, countermeasures to West German nuclear rearmament, and bloc endorsement of Moscow's summit conference proposals.
