It claims to have recaptured the airfield on Morotai Island on 20 May, the next objective will be the port of Djailolo on Halmahera Island. In Djakarta, President Sukarno was reported first incredulous and then impressed by a conversation with American author Louis Fischer when the latter told him that Soviet Russia is the biggest capitalist state in the world today.
