10 JULY 1959

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC
Communist radar reflects another penetration of mainland by Chinese Nationalist aircraft.
Peiping offers more military aid to Indonesia.

II. ASIA-AFRICA
UAR moving to improve relations with other Arab states, except Iraq.
Cairo postpones issuance of unified currency for Egypt and Syria.
Comment on new Indonesian cabinet.
Ceylon--Bandaranaike survives 8 July confidence vote; government remains in precarious position.

III. THE WEST
Haiti fearful of rebel incursions; asks US protection.
Influential trade union urges radical disarmament plan for UK.
Finnish official endorses Khrushchev overture to Scandinavia in hope USSR will acquiesce in Finnish participation in Western trade area.

LATE ITEM
Comment on Ghana's recognition of Algerian rebel government.
