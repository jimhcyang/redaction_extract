2. ETHIOPIA MAKES OIL AGREEMENT WITH CZECHOSLOVAKIA

Comment

Communist exports of petroleum are prominent in Moscow's trade with underdeveloped countries. In the first six months of 1955, Ethiopian-Soviet bloc trade amounted only to about $100,000.

The Czechs have shown increasing economic interest in Ethiopia by their large trade mission and by their expensive exhibit at the Jubilee Fair in November.

Communist interest in Africa has increased recently as demonstrated by the Soviet Union's diplomatic representation in Libya and its request for similar privileges in Liberia. (Concurred in by ORR)
