8. French Parliament reported hostile to German NATO membership:

If the French National Assembly suspects that German accession to NATO is implied in French ratification of the European Defense Forces, the government will suffer a severe defeat when this question is debated in the near future, according to the US Embassy in Paris.

Comment: While the attitude of the French Foreign Ministry on this issue has changed completely in the past two years, popular opinion is still violently opposed to German participation in NATO. The government hopes to sidestep the question during the forthcoming foreign policy debates in parliament and thereby to gain time for a gradual conversion of public opinion.
