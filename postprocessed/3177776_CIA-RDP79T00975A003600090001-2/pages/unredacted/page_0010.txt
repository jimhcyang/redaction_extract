In Djakarta, a prominent politician who has served in a liaison capacity between Padang and the central government in the interest of working out a negotiated settlement says that a substantial part of the attacking forces is composed of Communist veterans armed by the central government. He regards this as evidence that Djakarta has no present intention of compromising with the dissidents.
