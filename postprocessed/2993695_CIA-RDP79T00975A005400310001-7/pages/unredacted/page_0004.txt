6 DECEMBER 1960

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC

USSR attempting to clear way in UN for top-level contacts with new US administration at special General Assembly meeting next spring. (1)

Moscow and Cambodia formally announce accord on number of foreign policy positions in joint communique. (2)

Chinese Communists encountering "tremendous difficulties" in industry. (3)

II. ASIA-AFRICA

UAR orders widespread press attack in Lebanon on policies of Iraqi Prime Minister Qasim; may foreshadow new effort to hasten Qasim's decline. (4)

Situation in Laos (5)

III. THE WEST

Brazilian President-elect probably will institute major foreign policy changes, especially in colonial matters and Latin American economic development. (6)

Guatemala—Army unrest persists and new coup may be attempted soon. (7)
