Meanwhile, fighting continues in southern Xieng Khouang Province, where government forces are attempting to retain control of the Tha Thom airstrip.

Tha Thom was under heavy attack and might have to be relinquished. Government control of Tha Thom is essential to block an enemy advance south to Pak Sane. Tha Thom is also a point of departure for any future drive to retake the Plaine des Jarres, which Kong Le - Pathet Lao forces have held since 1 January.

Soviet and North Vietnamese aircraft continued airlift operations into Laos through 24 January and are scheduled for flights on 25 January.
