Comment: Other reports indicate that during mid-October an extensive call-up of reserves, requisition of vehicles, and the use of schools and barracks resulted in considerable excitement and tension in the First Army District, which includes Sofia. The concurrent mobilization in the First and Second Army areas is in contrast to the phased mobilization test which was conducted in 1951.

There has been no recent evidence of unusual military activities in the other Satellites. Neither Orbit propaganda nor other evidence suggests a Soviet intention to launch hostilities in the Balkans in the near future.
