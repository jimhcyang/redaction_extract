8. India express concern over stoppage of Iranian oil:
Meanwhile, the US Embassy in India states that according to reliable reports the Indian Government will probably re-introduce gas rationing on 1 July as a result of the Iranian oil situation.

Comment: It is estimated that the loss of crude oil and refined products now exported from Iran could be made up in time from other areas by increasing crude production and by more fully utilizing available refining capacity. A stoppage in the flow of Iranian oil would cause considerable dislocation and hardship for the Anglo-Iranian Oil Company's previous customers, since severe transportation and financial problems would have to be solved.
