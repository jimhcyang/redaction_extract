USSR OFFERS TO DEVELOP YUGOSLAV COPPER MINE Russia has offered Yugoslavia $50,000,000 for developing the Majdanpek copper mining complex, the loan is offered at two percent for 25 years with the interest payable in crude copper, finished copper, or currencies beginning the sixth year. Comment Yugoslavia to date has declined to export copper ore to the Soviet bloc.
