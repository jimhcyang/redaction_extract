5 JUNE 1958

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC
Comments by Soviet officials on French situation indicate indecision about line to take toward De Gaulle.

II. ASIA-AFRICA
Lebanon - Government forces moving against rebels in Biqa Valley and Tripoli. Sarraj reported determined to keep up UAR intervention.
Major Indonesian political party (PNI) denounces local Communists as instrument of foreign power.
Egyptian forces in Sinai taking routine precautions possibly connected with Israeli activity.
Tunisian President expects difficulties in relations with De Gaulle government.

III. THE WEST
Coty's chief civilian adviser describes De Gaulle as solid though probably difficult NATO partner.
De Gaulle promises Algerian Moslems political equality.
Italy - Minority all - Christian Democratic government likely.
Venezuelan Communist leaders, en route to Moscow, claim strong influence in Venezuelan cabinet.
