As of 17 April, Morocco had three French-trained jet officer pilots. Six noncommissioned officer pilots were scheduled to have completed their jet training by mid-1960.

The prince may expect to base jet aircraft, when they are acquired, at the American-built Boulhaut air base, which was evacuated by American forces on 4 March 1960 under the US-Moroccan base agreement of 22 December 1959, or possibly at the Rabat-Sale airfield, which is to be evacuated by the French air force by the end of this year.
