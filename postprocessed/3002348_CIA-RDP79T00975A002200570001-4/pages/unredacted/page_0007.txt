On 29 October, however, Premier Nasr turned down a Soviet offer of a $600,000,000 loan for the construction of the proposed high dam at Aswan.

Nasr allegedly rejected the offer because it was conditioned on Egypt's making full use of Russian experts and technicians. Earlier, Egyptian officials had said the USSR had given the impression it was not interested in sending technicians and other personnel to Egypt unless requested.
