Cuba: The Castro government evidently plans to raise a complaint in the UN against the US if the US reduces Cuba's sugar quota. Foreign Minister Roa has asked the UAR and Yugoslav ambassadors for their governments' opinions on such a complaint, and probably will try to obtain support from the Asian-African neutralist and Soviet bloc countries with which Cuba has increasingly closer relations. The UAR ambassador in Havana has reported to Cairo that Castro hopes to visit the USSR, UAR, Yugoslavia, Italy, Ghana, and Guinea in June.
