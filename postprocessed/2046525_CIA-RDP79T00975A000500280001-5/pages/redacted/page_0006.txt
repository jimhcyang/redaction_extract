6. Reuters correspondent implicates British officials in Iran:

The Reuters correspondent currently under arrest for circulating false reports derogatory to Iran is being held incommunicado and has made "confessions" mentioning four members of the British Embassy staff as the sources of his information. The British believe that he will be given a "state trial" along Eastern European lines, probably before the parliamentary interpellation of Mossadeq scheduled for 21 January. Iranian Minister of Court Ala has stated that the correspondent, who was "being encouraged to talk," has made statements which reflected adversely on members of the British Embassy staff.

Comment: Those British Embassy officers mentioned in the "confessions," individuals with much knowledge and experience in Iran who have been concerned with political reporting, have been suspected by the Iranians of inciting the tribes to revolt.
