28 JUNE 1960

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC

1. THE COMMUNIST BLOC

USSR may have terminated Geneva talks to preserve pose as champion of disarmament.

Peiping broadcast gives what may be hint of Chinese efforts to lessen degree of public quarreling with Moscow; Moscow releases text of Bucharest meeting communique.

II. ASIA-AFRICA

Zanzibar leftists plan demonstration against US space-tracking installations.

Syrian discontent may provoke anti-Egyptian demonstrations.

III. THE WEST

Adenauer, with an eye to 1961 elections, orders ‘all-out attack’ on Social Democrats during foreign policy debate.

Austria is considering buying Polish MIG-17s.
