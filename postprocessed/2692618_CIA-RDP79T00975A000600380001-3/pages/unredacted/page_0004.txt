FAR EAST

3. Chinese again report civilian suffering in North Korea:
Comment: Civilian food shortages, already critical, are probably being accentuated by the traditional "spring hunger" --the period between consumption of the fall rice harvest and the maturing of the summer grains in June.

All indications, however, point to a continued adequate level of supply for both Chinese Communist and North Korean military units.

4. Chinese guard against plague threat from Korea:
The Chinese Communists are restricting the return from Korea of personnel without an inoculation record and a "no-plague" certificate.
