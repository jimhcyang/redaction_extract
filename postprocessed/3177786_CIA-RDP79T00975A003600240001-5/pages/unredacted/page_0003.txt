28 MARCH 1958

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC
USSR - Khrushchev now holds top position in both party and government.
Three Soviet submarines believed destined for Egypt enter Mediterranean.

II. ASIA-AFRICA
Indonesia - Sumatran dissidents expected to offer serious resistance only in mountains; more Soviet aid reaching central government.
Saudi Arabia - Relative power positions of Saud, Faysal remain unclear to high Saudi officials.
Tunisia - Bourguiba refuses Gaillard's demand for neutral commission to supervise Tunisian side of Algerian border.

III. THE WEST
France - Pineau bent on exploring "disengagement" possibilities despite opposition within own government.
West Germany - Debate on foreign policy, nuclear armament restores unity of Adenauer's party, leaves opposition disorganized.
