12 MARCH 1958

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC
New Zealand's prime minister says he will honor pre-election pledge to recognize Communist China.

II. ASIA-AFRICA
Flurry of troop movements in Arab states reflects heightened tension in Near East resulting from recent developments.
Members of Saudi royal family may exploit Nasir's charges against Saud in effort to modify King's pro-Western policy.
Sudan - Pro-Western Prime Minister Khalil's party wins largest single bloc of seats in parliamentary elections.

III. THE WEST
British foreign secretary believes United Arab Republic will be serious threat to Western interests, especially during next six months. Britain wishes to discuss with US ways of undermining the UAR.
