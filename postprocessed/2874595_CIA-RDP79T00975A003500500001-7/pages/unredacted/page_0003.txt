31 JANUARY 1958

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC
Soviet vessels continue to deliver arms to Egypt and Syria.
Peiping optimistic over continued economic expansion.

LATE ITEM
Probable ICBM launching at Soviet Missile Test Range.

II. ASIA-AFRICA
Cairo still uncertain over Egyptian-Syrian union.
Morocco seeks to break French-Spanish control of arms supply.
Pro-Western Libyan former prime minister may return to office.
Afghan King to visit Pakistan.
Burmese prime minister publicly rejects Marxism.
Indonesia - Dissidents' open demand for Djuanda resignation may be prelude to ultimatum.

III. THE WEST
UK still unable to reach agreement with Turkey over Cyprus.
