29 MAY 1959

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC

II. ASIA-AFRICA

Prime Minister Abboud seeks British rockets for possible use against army rebels.

Indonesia--Vote expected in a few days on return to 1945 constitution. If Parliament rejects it, army may impose it.

Kurdish leaders in Turkey meet in secret to establish unified policy.

Dalai Lama intends to make public statement that Tibetans now are fighting for independence; Nehru opposed to such a declaration.

III. THE WEST

Haitian President seriously ill; power struggle likely.

Continued violence expected in Argentina.
