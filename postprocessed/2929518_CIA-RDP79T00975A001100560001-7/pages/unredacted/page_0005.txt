6. Slim majority seen for De Gasperi in 7 June elections:

The American embassy in Rome still believes that Premier de Gasperi's center bloc of parties will win the 7 June elections by a "rather slim margin" and obtain a majority in both the Senate and the Chamber of Deputies. The embassy states that the current wave of pessimism may be partly ascribed to a government tactic to bring out a maximum vote as the only means to avoid defeat with ensuing political chaos.

Comment: Under the recently passed electoral law, the De Gasperi bloc, by winning a bare majority of the popular vote, would gain almost two thirds of the seats in the Chamber of Deputies. This law does not apply to the Senate, however, and a bare majority there would leave the government parties severely handicapped by rightist and leftist opposition.
