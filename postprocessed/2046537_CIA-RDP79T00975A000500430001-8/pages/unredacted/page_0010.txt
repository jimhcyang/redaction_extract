Comment: This phase of the Communist propaganda campaign is well timed to exploit the growing opposition in Western Europe to American demands for tighter east-west trade controls. The Italian Communists have also opened a press campaign supporting the Economic Conference, seeking to show that US pressure against trade with the Satellites hurts the economies of Western European countries.

French manufacturers are particularly vulnerable to the temptations of trade with the Soviet Orbit at this time when certain French exports are declining and the spectre of currency devaluation looms large.
