FAR EAST

6. Early North Korean offensive on eastern Korean front indicated:

Comment:
While there have been no other indications of a pending offensive on the eastern front, there has been increased patrol activity in the area. An offensive there would be consistent with the recent pattern of Chinese attacks against South Korean positions.

The North Korean IV Corps is believed to have moved recently from the west coast to the east coast area, which would place nearly all North Korean forces on that part of the front.
