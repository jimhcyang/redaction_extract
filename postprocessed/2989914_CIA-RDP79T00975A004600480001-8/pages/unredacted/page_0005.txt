Brazil: Riots protesting the shortage of basic foods and the continuing increase in the cost of living may erupt in the industrial Sao Paulo area. The illegal Communist party is probably ready to participate actively in any public disturbances. State police would have difficulty in controlling any large-scale riots and might have to summon army help. (Page 4)
