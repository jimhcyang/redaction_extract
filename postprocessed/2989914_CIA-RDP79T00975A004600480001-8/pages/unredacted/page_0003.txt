25 AUGUST 1959

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC

II. ASIA-AFRICA

Laos--French and British representatives in Laos now believe situation critical; Hammarskjold suggests mediation formula to Nehru.

Yemen--The Imam's purge continues; Cairo considering withdrawal of some experts in its training mission.

III. THE WEST

French schedule further reconnaissance flights over Libya.

Brazil--Riots over economic conditions a possibility in Sao Paulo.
