Congo: The Leopoldville conference of Congolese leaders which convened on 25 January at Kasavubu's request has as its announced purpose the preparation of a later conference of all Congolese leaders. It has been boycotted by Lumumba's supporters, whose position has been strengthened by the recently published letter from Hammarskjold to Kasavubu, urging that Lumumba be tried or released from jail. The conference, which is unlikely to contribute significantly to a solution of the country's political problems, may, however, produce some greater degree of co-ordination between Congolese in Leopoldville and Tshombe's government in Katanga.
