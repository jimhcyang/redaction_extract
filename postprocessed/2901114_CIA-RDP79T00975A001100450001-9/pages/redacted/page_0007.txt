8. Belgian EDC ratification seen unlikely before fall:

An influential and strongly pro-EDC member of the Belgian Chamber of Representatives' EDC committee considers ratification extremely unlikely before the fall session. He cited the possibility of peace talks, France's lethargic attitude, and Foreign Minister van Zeeland's equivocal support of the treaty as delaying factors.

He plans to present, on behalf of his party, a proposal endorsing the opposition view that the treaty requires constitutional revision, and believes that this will later assure ratification.

Comment: This is the first indication that the government may concede the need for constitutional amendment, which requires new elections.
