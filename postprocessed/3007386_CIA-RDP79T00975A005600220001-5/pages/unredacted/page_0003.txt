LATE ITEM

USSR: An earth satellite vehicle--Sputnik X--was successfully launched from the Tyura Tam Missile Test Range at about 0054 EST 25 March. Preliminary evidence suggests that the payload was landed intact, after about one complete orbit of the earth, in an area approximately 200 nautical miles west of Sverdlovsk.
