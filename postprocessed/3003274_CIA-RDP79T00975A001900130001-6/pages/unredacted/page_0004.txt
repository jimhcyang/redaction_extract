Monnet is said to believe that the formation of such a pool is essential if Europe is to compete in the atomic field. The Monnet "declaration" will reportedly also call on France and West Germany to sign a pact committing them to an irrevocable joint policy of building a "United States of Europe."

Comment: Monnet informed the six governments last fall that he would not seek renewal of his term as president when it expired in February, but he is remaining in office pending agreement on his successor.

There have been other reports that Monnet will attempt to obtain from the member governments a commitment in principle to expand the scope of the Coal-Steel Community. American observers in Luxembourg have suggested that transportation would be the most likely field for any expansion of Community authority. Monnet, however, might well be more interested in the dramatic aspects of an "atomic pool" proposal.
