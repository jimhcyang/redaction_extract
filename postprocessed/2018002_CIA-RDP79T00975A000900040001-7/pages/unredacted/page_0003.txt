GENERAL
1. Objections raised to Mexican proposal on Korean war prisoners:

although the Mexican proposal to end the Korean truce deadlock might serve as a basis for discussion, it would need serious modifications for adoption. the principal obstacle was that many countries would not receive "Communists."

Comment: Several Latin American countries have already objected to the proposal, under which Communist prisoners of war resisting repatriation would be resettled in neutral countries. Their objection is probably more racial than ideological.
