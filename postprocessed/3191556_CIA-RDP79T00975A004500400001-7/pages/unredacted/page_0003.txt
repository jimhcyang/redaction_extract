26 JUNE 1959

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC
Comment on Soviet call for Balkan "Zone of Peace".

II. ASIA-AFRICA
Iran--Shah approves plan for Hammarskjold to try to end Iranian-Soviet controversy.
UAR apprehensive over possible Israeli reprisal for Cairo's refusal to permit Israeli use of canal; Hammarskjold arrival in Cairo 1 July makes Israeli action unlikely at this time.
Iraq--Army issues orders designed to tighten controls over Communist-infiltrated Popular Resistance Forces.
South Africa--Authorities concerned over possibility of African outbreaks today.

III. THE WEST
Finance Minister Pinay disagrees with De Gaulle's refusal to permit nuclear arming of nine USAF squadrons in France.
New rebel incursion into Nicaragua.
