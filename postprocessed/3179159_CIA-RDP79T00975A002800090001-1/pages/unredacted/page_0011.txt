There are indications that the regime, in an effort to strengthen its control of the army and appease national feelings, is easing Soviet marshal Rokossowski out of his posts as minister of national defense and commander in chief. General Marian Spychalski, a Gomulka supporter who was named deputy minister of national defense last week, on 28 October signed the army order of the day, which is usually signed by Rokossowski. Several unconfirmed press reports state that Rokossowski is on leave in the USSR, and is not expected to return.
