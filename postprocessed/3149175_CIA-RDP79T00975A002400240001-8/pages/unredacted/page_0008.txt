5. POUJADE REPORTED WINNING SOME "RESPECTABLE" SUPPORT

Pierre Poujade, who has recently been reported receiving growing veterans' support, is being regarded with increasing favor by individuals in military and industrial circles, according to the American embassy in Paris, General Jean Ganeval, director of President Coty's military cabinet, is one of those who favor Poujade.

The embassy comments that Poujade's firm position on Algeria appeals to Frenchmen of all classes who are becoming emotional and alarmed over the possibility that Premier Mollet is losing Algeria.
