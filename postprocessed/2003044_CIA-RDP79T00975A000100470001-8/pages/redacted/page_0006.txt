LATIN AMERICA

6. Violence breaks out in Ecuador:

During a 31 March parade of students, laborers and leftist political parties in Quito, a crowd attacked mounted police with stones. The police in turn fired on the crowd, killing two people and wounding fifteen. US Embassy in Quito has reported that the demonstration was probably a peaceful protest against mishandling of earthquake reconstruction funds, The presidential palace has reportedly been placed under heavy armed guard, and the streets are now patrolled by the army and police.
