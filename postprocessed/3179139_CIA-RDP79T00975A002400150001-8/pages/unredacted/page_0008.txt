THE ARAB-ISRAELI SITUATION
(Information as of 1700, 6 February)

Egypt has announced it will form a "Palestinian national guard" to protect Arab refugee camps and perform "other duties" in the Gaza strip, which is controlled by Egypt. An Egyptian official said volunteers for the new unit, to be recruited from among the 220,000 Arab refugees from Israel who are now in the strip, will be trained with modern arms by Egypt. Creation of this force seems intended to encourage better relations between the Egyptian army and the refugees, who have accused them of weakness in the face of Israeli provocations.
