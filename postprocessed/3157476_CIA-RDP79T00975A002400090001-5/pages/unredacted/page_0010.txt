Syria has recently allotted 54,000,000 Syrian pounds (about $25,000,000 at the official rate of exchange) for the purchase of arms.

Three quarters of the money came from a Saudi Arabian loan. Syria plans to purchase 105 Sherman tanks from France and to use the balance of the money for the purchase of arms from "the other side," presumably Czechoslovakia.
