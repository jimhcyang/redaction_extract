Meanwhile, there is much conflict in Stanleyville between various factions supporting Lumumba. Deputy Premier Gizenga, Louis Lumumba, and Bernard Salumu appear to be involved in a struggle for power, with Salumu at present gaining the upper hand. Reportedly, there is a change of African officials daily, and some supporters of Louis Lumumba and Gizenga have been seized and thrown into the Congo River.
