9 DECEMBER 1960

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC
Top Soviet leaders privately imply Communist manifesto attack on US was concession to Chinese but does not change USSR's desire to improve relations with US.
USSR--Special party meeting to be held in Moscow just before 13 December central committee plenum on agriculture.
North Korea, with official endorsement from Moscow, continues campaign for Korean reunification.

II. ASIA-AFRICA
New Japanese cabinet unites conservative party and improves chances for keeping factional rivalries under control.
Turkish interim President Gursel's illness, minimized in official statements, could have unsettling effect in Turkey.
Congo--Tension between UN officials and Mobutu regime may increase following Hammarskjold criticism of Congo leader.

LATE ITEM
Situation in Laos.

III. THE WEST
Chile--Government concerned over recent Soviet trade overtures.
