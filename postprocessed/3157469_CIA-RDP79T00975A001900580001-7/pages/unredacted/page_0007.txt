6. Comment on death sentences in Cairo Zionist spy case:

Egypt's decision to sentence to death two of the 11 Zionists tried on charges of espionage can be expected to precipitate a new crisis with Israel, which has displayed great concern over the trial.

Despite Israel's warning to the French and Turkish ambassadors that "armed conflict" with Egypt might result if any of the accused were executed, this is not anticipated. Frontier reprisals and a strong diplomatic and propaganda campaign against Egypt, however, are likely if the condemned are executed.

The commutation of the Zionists' death sentences might, however, invite internal repercussions, since the regime executed six members of the Moslem Brotherhood in early December.
