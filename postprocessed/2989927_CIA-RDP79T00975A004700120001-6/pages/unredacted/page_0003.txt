14 SEPTEMBER 1959

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC
USSR indicates embarrassment over events both in Laos and on the Sino-Indian border.

Chinese Communist propaganda now giving full publicity to Sino-Indian border dispute; New Delhi charged with exaggerating incidents.

II. ASIA-AFRICA
Communist propaganda attempting to place onus on Phoui government for continued fighting in Laos.

Sudanese Army command declares alert.

Arab-Israeli tensions heightened by new incidents on eve of UN General Assembly meeting.

III. THE WEST
British-UAR relations continue to improve.

LATE ITEM
Comment on Soviet lunar shot
