Sino-Soviet missions appear to be the primary target of a reported plan to close all diplomatic installations in Yemen; a top-ranking Yemeni official reportedly stated, however, that this action cannot take place for many months. Foreign relations would be conducted through Cairo for the United Arab States--the UAR-Yemeni federation.

Badr has recently expelled a number of Soviet doctors from Yemen and may be taking steps to strengthen his control over other bloc personnel. The recent arrival of an Egyptian economic mission and plans for accepting a similar mission for agricultural training appear to be additional moves to reduce dependence on Sino-Soviet aid.
