11 MAY 1959

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC

USSR launches test ICBM to full 3500-nautical-mile range on 9 May.

Soviet party central committee to convene on 24 June to discuss implementation of economic program.

USSR population 208,800,000, according to official census. Males number 94,000,000, or 5,000,000 fewer than estimated in US.

Moscow commentator says Secretary Herter in his 7 May speech has "compromised faith in good intentions of Western powers."

II. ASIA-AFRICA

UAR reiterates policy of "positive neutrality" to its diplomats abroad.

Yemen - Imam and Crown Prince quoted as wanting to "disengage" from commitments to Sino-Soviet bloc.

Government crisis in Somalia.
