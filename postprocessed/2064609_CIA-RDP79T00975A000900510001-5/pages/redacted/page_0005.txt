SOUTHEAST ASIA

4. Improvement of Red River supply route to Viet Minh indicated:

construction is now in progress on an airfield in Yunnan along the Red River about 150 miles northwest of the Indochina border town of Laokay. The French Air Force officer who gave this report also stated he had confirmed information that construction of a new road south-southwest from Mengtzu in China toward the Red River was begun in August.

Comment: The Red River enters Indochina near Laokay in northwestern Tonkin. Although some facilities already exist in the Red River area, the majority of Chinese supplies to the Viet Minh enter Tonkin by routes considerably to the east.

Improved communications north of Laokay would facilitate the more direct supply of forces in northwest Tonkin, where the Viet Minh has improved its position during this year's campaign.
