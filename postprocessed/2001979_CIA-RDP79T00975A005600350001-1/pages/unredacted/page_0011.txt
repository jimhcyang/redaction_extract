Enemy forces south of the Plaine des Jarres are reported to be widening a trail for a distance of about 20 miles southward toward Pak Sane from their base at Ban Ta Viang. A Pathet Lao company is moving toward the area northwest of Pak Sane with a mission to sever Route 13 linking that town with Vientiane.

Pathet Lao forces are also reported to have extended their control in the Kam Keut - Lak Sao area to about six miles south of Lak Sao on the road leading to Nhommarat. There are indications that enemy supply dumps are being established in the vicinity of Lak Sao.

The Soviet airlift into Laos continues. The Soviet IL-14 en route to Hanoi from the USSR was scheduled to leave Peiping on 9 April.
