7. Syrian extremist

Comment: The situation in Syria is deteriorating markedly. The coalition caretaker cabinet has not yet agreed on a date for the elections and is bitterly opposed by strong army factions. Rumors of the government's resignation and possible coups are circulating.

Hawrani--reportedly with one fifth of the army's officers behind him--appears to be the most serious of many threats to the regime. A government dominated by extreme nationalist Hawrani would probably adopt a non-co-operative policy in its dealings with the West.
