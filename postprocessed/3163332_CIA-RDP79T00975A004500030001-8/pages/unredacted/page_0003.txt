14 MAY 1959

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC
Soviet fighters display increased altitude capability.
Khrushchev invites Afghan prime minister to visit Moscow.
USSR replacing its ambassador to Burma.

II. ASIA-AFRICA
Cairo "not to carry out an action in Jordan at present."
Burma concerned over activities of Chinese Nationalist irregulars in its border areas.
Indonesia - Dissidents demand payment from Goodyear rubber estate as "guarantee" against further attacks.

III. THE WEST
Relations between France and Guinea extremely tense.

LATE ITEMS
Laos - Two former Pathet Lao battalions resist integration into regular army.
Peruvian government alerted to possible coup attempt.
