General Navarre's estimate of the Dien Bien Phu situation, contained in a report to Paris which was shown to Ambassador Heath, is that the Viet Minh has two choices: an all-out attack, or a prolonged "smothering action." Navarre has also informed his government that French intelligence is positive that the enemy has sufficient supplies coming in from China to continue operations at Dien Bien Phu at least until the end of April.
