The French authorities are also in a position to bring sufficient pressure on El Glaloui, the sultan's chief opponent, to prevent him from upsetting the status quo.

While the possibility of clashes between rival factions exists, it is not probable that large-scale disturbances will occur since the 56,000 French security forces are capable of maintaining order. Basic tensions will not be alleviated, however, and El Glaloui and his supporters will continue to be a source of trouble.
