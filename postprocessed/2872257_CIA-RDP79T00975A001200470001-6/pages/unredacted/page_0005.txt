SOUTH ASIA

4. Pakistani army remains calm over Kashmir crisis:
Pakistani army headquarters near Kashmir is maintaining an attitude of complete calm with regard to the recent developments in Indian-held Kashmir, according to the American army attache in Karachi. The Pakistani army is not contemplating cancellation of leaves and does not plan to move additional troops into the area.

Comment: Pakistan, as in previous crises, is apparently taking all possible precautions to avoid a conflict.
