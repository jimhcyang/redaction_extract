3. Afghanistan may be willing to cease Pushtoonistan campaign:

The Afghan government allegedly is willing to cease further agitation for an independent Pushtoonistan and to recognize the disputed Pushtoon tribal area between Afghanistan and Pakistan as belonging officially to the latter,

Afghan willingness, however, is contingent upon finding a face-saving formula, such as giving the tribal territory a separate name.

Comment: These new suggestions for ending the most important dispute between Afghanistan and Pakistan represent a retreat from the usual Afghan position demanding public Pakistani recognition that a problem exists and insisting that the tribal area should be made independent.

It may also be significant that in August the only independent Afghan paper, in which the Afghan prime minister reportedly is interested, ceased printing Pushtoonistan propaganda, which usually constitutes the bulk of Afghan news.
