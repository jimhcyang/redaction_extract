WESTERN EUROPE

8. Reports of Italian troop movements toward Trieste held erroneous:

on 1 September told the American army attache in Rome that there had been no movement of Italian troops in connection with the Trieste dispute. He added that press observers had probably been misled by the movement of units returning to their barracks after summer maneuvers.

Comment: This report suggests that Prime Minister Pella was bluffing when he told an American embassy official on 31 August that one Italian regiment was being dispatched to Monfalcone, on the Gulf of Trieste.
