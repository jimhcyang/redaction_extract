NEAR EAST-AFRICA

6. Comment on selection of Nuri Said to form Iraqi cabinet:

King Faisal's request that Nuri Said, dominant member of parliament, form a new cabinet indicates that Iraq will continue to have a pro-Western government.

Nuri Said, who has been prime minister 11 times in the 33 years of modern Iraq's history, strongly favors Iraqi adherence to the Turkish-Pakistani pact.
