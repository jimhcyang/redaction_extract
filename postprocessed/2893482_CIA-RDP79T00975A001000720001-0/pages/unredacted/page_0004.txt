SOVIET UNION

3. Additional Soviet fighter division located in Austria:

Formation of the unit apparently began in October 1952 when Soviet Air Force personnel were observed on Deutsch Wagram airfield ten miles northeast of Vienna. Since February 1953, a small number of MIG-15's have been reported there.

Comment: This is the second fighter division to be added to the 59th Air Army in Austria and Hungary in recent months and raises the total to four, two of which are deployed in Hungary. A fighter division was transferred in January from the Western USSR to Hungary and is now based near Budapest.

Estimated strength of Soviet and Satellite jet fighter units deployed throughout East Europe has increased over the past year by about 580 aircraft to a present estimated total of about 1700 planes.
