LATIN AMERICA

4. Communists planning to attack American government offices in Cuba:

Comment: Previous reports indicate that the Communists are planning demonstrations against the regime prior to the 1 November elections. The government is reported to be well aware of these plans and has arranged for the close military surveillance of all strategic points during the critical period before and after the election.

Batista, who continues to be backed by a large majority of the armed forces, would probably be successful in thwarting any attempt to oust him.
