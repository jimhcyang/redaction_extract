SOUTHEAST ASIA

2. Vietnamese army reported planning to move against government:

Comment: The American ambassador in Saigon expressed the view on 23 October that a coup d'etat was still possible but not probable. Chief of Staff General Hinh has kept the threat of a coup alive as part of his war of nerves against the Diem government.

3. Increasing Communist activity in north Burma indicated:

Comment: The information regarding the "political commissars" indicates the deepest penetration of Burma by Chinese Communist agents so far reported. Previous reports had indicated that Peiping's agents were concentrated near Yunnan province in an area claimed by Communist China.
