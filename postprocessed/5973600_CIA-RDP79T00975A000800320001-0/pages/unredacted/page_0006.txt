SOUTH ASIA

6. USSR protests foreign exploitation of Afghan oil:
Comment: For some time the Soviet Union has attempted to persuade Afghanistan to abandon its efforts to enlist foreign assistance in exploiting its oil resources. Last spring, however, the Soviet Ambassador assured an Afghan official that the USSR would have no objections to Afghanistan's development of these resources.

It has long been a Soviet aim to prevent the growth of Western influence in northern Afghanistan. The current indecision of the Afghan Government might indefinitely postpone oil negotiations.
