In view of the continuing East German harassing of the movement of West Germans in Berlin, Bonn will probably urge that NATO maintain its retaliatory restrictions on the travel of East Germans to NATO countries. Bonn, however, has thus far given no indication that it intends to halt vital steel or machinery shipments to East Germany in an effort to force the East Germans to implement their 29 December pledge to stop requiring passes for West Germans entering East Berlin.
