Comment: Since Bao Dai is expected
to issue a reassuring statement on Vietnamese adherence to the
French Union, Laniel has a good chance to weather this storm.
The debate, however, will bring increased attention to the French
Union concept, which will be the crucial point in French-Vietnamese
negotiations.

Such heightened French feelings may also
create a serious threat to EDC ratification prospects by highlighting
France's difficulty in reconciling its overseas responsibilities with
participation in European integration schemes.

LATIN AMERICA

British Guiana action:

Comment: Latin American reaction to
Britain's handling of the Guiana crisis has been generally unfavorable.
However, comment thus far has stressed anticolonialism rather than
the possibility that the United States might use Communism as a pretext
for intervention against a Latin American government.

Although London's recent moves were legal
under British constitutional law, interpretations
may spark new protests in Latin America.
