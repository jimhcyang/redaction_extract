26 DECEMBER 1960

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC

Taiwan Strait--Almost no Communist shelling in past month.

II. ASIA-AFRICA

Laos--Peiping endorses USSR's "just stand" on ICC and Geneva conference; bloc air support continues.

Congo--Hammarskjold implies he will try to take UN out of Congo if Mobutu uses force in Orientale Province.

King Saud promises some reforms; popular reaction to his take-over generally favorable.

Ethiopia--Security precautions continue high; Emperor's decision to reconstitute Bodyguard likely to antagonize army.
