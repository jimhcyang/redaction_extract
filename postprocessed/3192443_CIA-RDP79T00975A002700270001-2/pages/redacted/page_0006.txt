7. ARGENTINE POLITICAL SITUATION UNSETTLED

The political situation in Argentina appears uneasy, but the government is expected to maintain control of the situation, despite considerable disagreement within the ruling group.

Comment

Plotting and discontent have been chronic since the overthrow of Peron in the revolt of 16-19 September 1955. The abortive Peronist-nationalist uprising in June of this year underscored this discontent. There may be disturbances before or during the anniversary of Peron's ouster, but the government can probably keep such disturbances from getting out of hand.
