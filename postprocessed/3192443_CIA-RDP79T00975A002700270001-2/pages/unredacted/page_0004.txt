2. EGYPT DISPLAYS CAUTION ON ACCEPTING SOVIET PILOTS FOR SUEZ

Comment

Nasr may feel that acceptance of Soviet pilots at this juncture might provoke Britain and France to withdraw their pilots. Nasr's indicated caution also suggests that he wants to avoid alienating the United States at this time. Egypt may therefore move slowly on accepting pilots from the Soviet bloc and give priority to pilots from "neutral" European countries. On 6 September, Egypt gave orders to Egyptian personnel in the United States not to make any statements or announcements whatsoever on the subject of pilots.
