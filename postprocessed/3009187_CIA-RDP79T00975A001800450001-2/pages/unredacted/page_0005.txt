4. North Korean air force fighters increasingly active in North Korea-Manchuria region:

Fighter units of the North Korean air force have assumed a more important role in air defense activity over North Korea and Manchuria.

Comment: The North Korean units have probably reached a stage of proficiency which warrants their being assigned increased responsibility for air defense. The sizable reduction in the past year of Soviet and Chinese Communist jet fighter strength in the Yalu area indicates that a major share of the responsibility for air defense of the North Korea-Manchuria theater rests on the North Korean air force.
