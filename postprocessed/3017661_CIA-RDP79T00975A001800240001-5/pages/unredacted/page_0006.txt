Caffery interprets the steady rise of stocks of Jewish-controlled companies on the Cairo exchange as evidence of the prosperity and acceptance of Jews in Egypt.

Comment: Current Israeli propaganda is charging Egypt with serious mistreatment of its Jewish minority.

While the Nasr regime has given full support to the Arab line on Israel, it has from the beginning emphasized a concept of national unity which avoids any semblance of discrimination against minority groups.

Minority elements in Egypt have frequently engaged in Communist activities and a number of Jews have in the past been included among those arrested.

6. Comment on Popular Front victories in Greek municipal elections:
Preliminary reports of Greek municipal elections on 21 November show that the Communist-supported Popular Front achieved notable successes over the Greek Rally of Prime Minister Papagos. The Front elected mayors in such important places as Athens, Piraeus, Volos, Kavalla, Larissa and Lamia.
