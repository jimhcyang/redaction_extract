THE ARAB-ISRAELI SITUATION
(Information as of 1700, 28 August)

Egypt's crude oil requirements will be met on a day-to-day basis for some time, since Soviet bloc crude oil deliveries during the year beginning July 1955 were considerably below the amount promised.

The main reason for the slow deliveries was said to be the overtaxed Egyptian refinery and storage capacities.

A joint Syrian-Egyptian command message of 27 August indicates that a Syrian MIG "flight" (or possibly squadron) plans to begin operational flying the week of 3 September. Syria has purchased 20 MIG's from Czechoslovakia. These, according to intercepted messages, were to be shipped in July.
