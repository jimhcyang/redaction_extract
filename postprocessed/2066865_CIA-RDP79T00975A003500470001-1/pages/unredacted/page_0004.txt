28 JANUARY 1958

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC
Soviet-Ceylonese assistance agreement to be signed this week. (1)

II. ASIA-AFRICA
Algerian rebel action expected in March. (2)
Nasir and Quwatli scheduled to proclaim Egyptian-Syrian union this week. (3)
Indonesia - Masjumi leaders cooperating with Sumatran dissidents. (4)
Turks suspect Arabs were responsible for Ankara bombings. (5)

III. THE WEST
Britain points to summit meeting after adequate diplomatic preparation. (6)
Cuba - Batista restores constitutional guarantees in preparation for June elections. (7)
Venezuelan military demands junta adopt firmer policy against left-wing elements. (8)
Iceland - Opposition Conservative party makes gains in local elections. (9)
