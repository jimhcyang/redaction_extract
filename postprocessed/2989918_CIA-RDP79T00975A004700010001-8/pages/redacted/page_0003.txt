1 SEPTEMBER 1959

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC
Khrushchev says Adenauer letter produced "favorable impression," 1

II. ASIA-AFRICA
Nehru labels Chinese Communist border incursions as "aggression;"
press reports of further incursions remain unconfirmed. 2
South Vietnam seeks to aid Laos with troops disguised as civilians. 3

III. THE WEST
Bolivian Government under leftist pressure to accept Soviet loan. 4
Cuba--More anti-Americanism expected as result of rift between inter-American labor organization and its Cuban affiliate. 5

LATE ITEM
Communists riot in Calcutta; call general strike for 3 September. 6
