20 MAY 1960

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC

1. USSR may have attempted and failed recover of Sputnik IV cabin on 18 May.
2. Soviet bloc military activity conveys no threatening implications.
3. USSR, Japan sign 1960 fishing agreement providing further reduction of Japanese salmon quota.

II. ASIA-AFRICA

4. Indian envoy in Moscow feels U-2 forced Khrushchev to show himself ‘tougher’ than colleague but won’t reverse basic detente policy.
5. Indonesia ousts two Chinese Communist consuls in repatriation dispute.
6. Cambodia--Sihanouk calls for June plebiscite to show his domestic support in dispute with Bangkok, Saigon.
7. South Korea studies possible reorganization of armed forces to preclude involvement in politics.
8. Congo--Nationalist leader threatens forceful reaction to planned reinforcement of Belgian security units.
9. Iran withdraws support from Iraqi exiles in Jordan plotting against ‘regime.
10. Guinea--Touré seeks foreign aid for early start on Konkoure hydroelectric project.
