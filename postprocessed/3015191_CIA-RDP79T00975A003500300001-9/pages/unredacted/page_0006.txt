3. SOVIET SUBMARINE ACTIVITY IN ATLANTIC AREA DURING AUGUST

Comment on:

an unprecedented number of submarines were operating in widely diverse areas of the Atlantic during August.

One group, consisting of a minimum of 22 to 26 submarines, was involved in anti-ship exercises in the Norwegian Sea, while a second group was apparently active in waters off eastern Canada. In conducting such extensive operations simultaneously, the Soviet commanders met conditions which they would face in directing an all-out submarine war in the Atlantic.
