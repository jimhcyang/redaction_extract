On 3 August, Chinese Nationalist aircraft attacked the British freighter Inchwells off the Min River estuary, inflicting shock damage from near misses and causing several personnel casualties. Although Taipei has issued several warnings to the Nationalist air force and navy against attacking neutral shipping, it is considered unlikely that these warnings will prevent further Nationalist attacks on British shipping
