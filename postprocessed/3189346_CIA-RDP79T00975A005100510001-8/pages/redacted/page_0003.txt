CENTRAL INTELLIGENCE BULLETIN

29 June 1960

DAILY BRIEF

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC

USSR - Communist China: In the communiqué signed in Bucharest on 24 June the Soviet Union and Communist China reached back to 1957 for an authoritative statement of Bloc policy which both could still accept. The communiqué merely outlined broad principles in very general lines, leaving specific issues open to varying interpretations. For the most part it expressed Soviet views, but Chinese reservations received some consideration. Such an arrangement, by itself, does little to resolve underlying differences between Moscow and Peiping, but the two powers in private talks probably examined ways of easing their dispute.
