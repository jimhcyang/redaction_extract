13 AUGUST 1960

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC

Khrushchev offers aid to Ghana in addi-
tion to that covered in economic aid agree-
ment soon to be announced. (1)

Chinese Communists now moving to soften
their dispute with Indonesia. (2)

II. ASIA-AFRICA

Federation of Mali--Dispute between
leaders of constituent states of Soudan
and Senegal sharpens to the extent their
federation may be threatened. (3)

III. THE WEST

Peru to support diplomatic break with
Dominican Republic in forthcoming OAS
session but is not yet ready to support a
move against Cuba. (4)
