7. French express deep concern over Cambodian situation:

Commissioner General Dejean sent an aide to the American embassy in Saigon on 23 April to express deep French concern at the rapid deterioration in Cambodia. The French feel a coup d'etat that would put the Viet Minh and the non-Communist insurgent leader, Son Ngoc Thanh, in control of the kingdom may be expected at any time.

Comment: French authorities in Saigon appear to be more alarmist on Cambodia than those in Phnom Penh. The acting high commissioner there said on 22 April that the situation was less serious than the Cambodians described it when they appealed for arms.

Son Ngoc Thanh, the only important non-Communist insurgent leader in Cambodia, is believed to have an armed following of fewer than a thousand.
