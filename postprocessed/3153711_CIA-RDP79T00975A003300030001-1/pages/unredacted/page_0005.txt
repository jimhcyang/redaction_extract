3. DISSIDENT CUBAN MILITARY ELEMENTS REPORTED PLANNING REVOLT AGAINST BATISTA

Comment

Growing resentment within the armed forces against the present repressive regime has been reported recently, though support from the military has traditionally been Batista's main strength. Known leaders of an abortive army revolt in April 1956 were imprisoned, but others have evidently continued to recruit dissidents among young officers. Effective use of several planes might immobilize Camp Colombia, giving impetus and protection to other attacks on the government by some of the many groups which are bitterly opposed to Batista.
