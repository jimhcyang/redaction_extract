PLOT TO PROVOKE DISORDER IN TUNISIA REPORTED The Tunisian deputy prime minister has told the American ambassador of a plot by French "Special Services" in Algeria to assassinate an important Tunisian official in order to provoke disturbances in Tunisia. Tunis wishes to avoid public knowledge of the incident, so that the Tunisian public will not force it to take a stronger line against the French army.
