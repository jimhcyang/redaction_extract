8 JANUARY 1960

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC
Flow of Tibetan refugees into Nepal increases; rumors of Chinese Communist incursions continue.

II. ASIA-AFRICA
Laos--Balanced composition of new provisional government should ease strains among various non-Communist groups.
Burmese premier rejects invitation to Peiping for border negotiations.
Afghan premier accepts Iranian invitation to discuss Helmand waters dispute.
Libya may press for major change in American base agreement.

III. THE WEST
Italian premier's resignation this month predicted by prominent Christian Democrats; Gronchi postpones visit to Moscow.

LATE ITEM
USSR makes first public announcement of planned rocket flight tests within specific time period.
