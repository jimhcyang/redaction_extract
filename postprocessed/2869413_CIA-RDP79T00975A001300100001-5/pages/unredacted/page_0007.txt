Comment: The draft Austrian note states that "if the Soviet Union refuses to discuss the short treaty," Austria will no longer support it. The draft fails to mention that the Western powers in their notes of 17 August agreed that the short treaty would not be introduced for consideration at the meeting of the treaty deputies then proposed for 31 August.

Previous Western efforts to revise the proposed note, which will be reviewed by the competent parliamentary committee on 23 September, have been brushed aside by the Austrian government.
