CENTRAL INTELLIGENCE BULLETIN

9 August 1960

DAILY BRIEF

III. THE WEST

Brazil-Cuba-US: President Kubitschek's desire to pry "massive support" from the United States for his Latin American economic development plan, "Operation Pan America," is resulting in a cautious and neutralist Brazilian approach to the Cuban problem and a tolerance for Cuban and pro-Communist activities in Brazil. Kubitschek's policy is based on the views of his key foreign policy adviser, Frederico Schmidt.
