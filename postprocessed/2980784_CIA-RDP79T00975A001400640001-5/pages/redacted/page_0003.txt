SOUTH ASIA

2. Nehru's attack on US observers in Kashmir primarily for internal effect:

The American embassy in New Delhi believes Nehru's announcement on 1 March that India no longer regards American members of the UN observer team in Kashmir as neutral is largely "symbolic" and intended primarily for internal effect. The action may have been taken at the insistence of R. A. Kidwai and K. Katju, two of Nehru's more anti-American cabinet members.
