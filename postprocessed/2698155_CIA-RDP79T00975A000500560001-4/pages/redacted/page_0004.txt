4. French official states views on Indochina situation:

The French Minister of Associated States, Letourneau, who has just completed an inspection trip throughout Indochina, told the American Minister in Saigon that while the outlook is "by no means hopeless it is far from optimistic." He was discouraged by the performances of Bao Dai and Premier Huu, and had warned them that France might not continue its sacrifices unless the Vietnamese Government showed signs of improvement.
