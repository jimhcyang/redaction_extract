23 SEPTEMBER 1959

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC
USSR regards De Gaulle's policies as providing opportunities for exploiting "disunity" in Western alliance.

II. ASIA-AFRICA
Nasir says trend of UAR-Soviet relations is "downward"
Sudan sentences officers responsible for abortive coup effort; takes precautionary measures against possible new attempt
Cambodian - South Vietnamese relations likely to deteriorate

III. THE WEST
Cyprus--Early establishment of new regime could be jeopardized by disclosure and political exploitation of secret Greek-Turkish-UK agreements
Honduras--Friction mounts between armed forces and partisans of governing Liberal party; serious fighting could result
