FAR EAST

4. Thai Premier threatens to resign:

Premier Phibun of Thailand has informed the ruling military clique that he is preparing to resign and has requested that his successor be chosen.

It is believed that Phibun may be attempting to promote a conflict over succession between Police Director General Phao and General Sarit, Deputy Commander of the Army.

Comment:

The threat of resignation has been used by Phibun before to force the ruling clique to accept certain of his policies. There is no apparent issue at this time, however, which would call for this technique.
