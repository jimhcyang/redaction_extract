SECURITY INFORMATION

GENERAL
1. Soviet mission allegedly to study conditions for peace negotiations in Indochina:

Comment: Moscow might dispatch such a mission to Indochina as part of its propaganda emphasis on ending the war through direct negotiations. Any Communist expression of willingness to negotiate now would probably be no more than a propaganda device by which to forestall increased US or possible UN intervention, to undermine Franco-Vietnamese morale and to block expanded military operations.
