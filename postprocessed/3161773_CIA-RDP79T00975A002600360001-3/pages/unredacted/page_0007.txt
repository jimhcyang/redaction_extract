THE ARAB-ISRAELI SITUATION
(Information as of 1700, 10 July)

Secretary General Hammarskjold has announced that he will visit Jerusalem on 19-20 July, and Cairo on 21-22 July. (Press)

The American air attaché in Moscow reported on 7 July that 9 submarines, 6 destroyers and 60 coastal and mine vessels are scheduled for delivery to Egypt by the Soviet bloc. 3 subs, 3 destroyers and 4 coastal mine layers have already arrived in Egypt. On 10 July a Cairo paper with official connections stated that Egypt is to receive "several" submarines from abroad within the next few days. No submarines are known to have been delivered to Egypt, however, although they are reportedly involved in the Soviet bloc arms deal. Egypt is known to have received 2 destroyers and 4 coastal mine sweepers from the Soviet bloc.
