III. THE WEST
Bolivia: All rural militia units in the Cochabamba valley of central Bolivia have mobilized, and widespread violence threatens. Considerable unrest has arisen from bitter rivalry between the two wings of governing Nationalist Revolutionary Movement over selection of presidential and congressional nominees at its convention 15-21 February. This led on 23 January to a violent three-hour battle in a key mining town between left- and right-wing factions of the miners' militia.
