28 APRIL 1959

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC

II. ASIA-AFRICA

Iraqi Army schedules show of force in tribal areas near Turkish and Iranian borders.

Iran - Discovery of major new oil field will reopen question of pipeline through Turkey to Mediterranean.

III. THE WEST

British release of blocked sterling account ahead of schedule puts UAR technically in default on compensation to old company stockholders and jeopardizes IBRD loan for Suez Canal improvement.

Argentina - President Frondizi issues decree banning all Communist party activities, at least during state of siege.
