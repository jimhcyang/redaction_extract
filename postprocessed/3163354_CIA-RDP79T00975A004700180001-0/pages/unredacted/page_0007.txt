South Tirol: Renewed tension between Austria and Italy seems likely to result from Austrian Foreign Minister Kreisky's declared intention of referring to the South Tirol dispute as an "important subject" in his forthcoming address to the UN General Assembly. Although Kreisky has offered assurances to American officials in Vienna that his speech will be moderate he has stated he will make it clear that resolution of the South Tirol problem is essential to the peace of this area of Europe. Even a moderate reference in the UN to the problems of the South Tirol will probably strike the Italians as provocative. (Page 7).
