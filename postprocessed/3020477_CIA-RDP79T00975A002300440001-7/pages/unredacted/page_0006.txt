3. KING SAUD APPROVES POLISH BID TO SURVEY RAILROAD PROJECT

Comment on:

King Saud has approved Poland's low bid for the survey of the Hejaz railroad reconstruction project. According to a Warsaw radio broadcast of 30 December, the contract for this work was signed in Damascus on 28 December.

The successful Polish bid paves the way for Warsaw to win any subsequent contract for actual construction of the rail line from Maan to Medina and possibly contracts for other construction projects in the Near East. (Prepared by ORR)
