20th Party Congress will be aired at the next Netherlands
party congress in September.

Comment
The Netherlands party is the only West-
ern European party to have issued a
statement criticizing the recent remarks of Togliatti.

On 10 April the executive committee of
the Netherlands party reluctantly published a resolution ca-
pitulating to the Soviet party line on Stalin.
