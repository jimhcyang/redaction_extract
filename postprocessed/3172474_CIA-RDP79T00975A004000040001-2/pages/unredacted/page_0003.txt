16 SEPTEMBER 1958

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC

Taiwan Strait - Indications mounting of logistical build-up north of Matsus.

Khrushchev back in Crimea.

II. ASIA-AFRICA

India will again raise question of Chinese Communist membership in UN.

Iraq - Regime attempting to rally public opinion behind Premier Qasim

UAR - High-level military meeting in Cairo.
