22 OCTOBER 1960

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC

II. ASIA-AFRICA

Situation in Congo.

Dispute within Mali Government threatens position of relatively moderate President Keita.

Souvanna Phouma authorizes attack against Pathet Lao in Phong Saly Province in reaction to cease-fire violation; he also indicates intention to control Kong Le.

Repatriation agreement with North Korea becoming major election issue in Japan; Japanese agreement to extend repatriation would antagonize South Korea.

LATE ITEM

Austrian government to resign because of failure to agree on budget.
