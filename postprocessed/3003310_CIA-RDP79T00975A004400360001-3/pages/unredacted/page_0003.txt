24 APRIL 1959

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC
USSR and Nepal agree to exchange of diplomatic missions.

II. ASIA-AFRICA
Iran preparing protest on Soviet propaganda attacks; may take issue to the United Nations.
Dalai Lama wants to make public statement about setting up "Free Tibetan" government; Peiping press more critical of India's role in Tibetan issue.

III. THE WEST
Finnish commander in chief to make "friendship" visit to USSR in early May; USSR may renew pressure on Finns to buy Soviet weapons.
France setting up new military commands involving Africa in move designed to emphasize De Gaulle's desire to mark off specific areas of defense responsibility.
