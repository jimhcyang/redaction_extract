11 APRIL 1959

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC
Ambassador Thompson comments on Soviet policy as foreign ministers’ conference approaches.

II. ASIA-AFRICA
British concerned over reports that Iraq preparing to take military action against Kuwait about 15 April.
More military supplies sent from Egypt to Syria, probably for guerrilla operations against Iraq.
UAR furnishing additional military equipment to Libya.
Pakistani Air Force shoots down Indian aircraft over Pakistani territory.

III. THE WEST
US Embassy in Bonn comments on Adenauer's decision to relinquish chancellorship.
Czech ship seized this week by French Navy was carrying arms to Indonesia and Morocco.
Uruguay considers breaking diplomatic relations with USSR.
