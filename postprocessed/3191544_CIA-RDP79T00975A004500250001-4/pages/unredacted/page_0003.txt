9 JUNE 1959

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC
Communist China - Burma--Peiping receives Burmese proposal for settlement of border problems, but will probably continue its negative attitude. (1)

II. ASIA-AFRICA
Relations of Algerian rebels with Tunisia and Morocco increasingly difficult; military fortunes at new low. (2)

Prospects brighten for Tunisian-French agreement on use of Bizerte base. (3)

India--Effort to force Communists out of office in Kerala State planned; violence possible. (4)

III. THE WEST
(5) West Berlin's mayor apprehensive over effect in city if West German presidential elections are not held there.

(6) Nicaragua--Government position stronger, but incidents continue and further incursions by exiles possible.

(7) Leftist gains in Sicily election pose problems for Christian Democrats.
