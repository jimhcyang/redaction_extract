4. FORMER PREMIER PRIDI REPORTEDLY SEEKING TO RETURN TO THAILAND

Comment
Pridi has been in exile since 1947 and in mid-1954 broadcast from Peiping an appeal to the Thai people to overthrow the Phibun regime because of its pro-Western orientation. Phibun recently stated in public that Pridi, as a Thai national, was free to return to Thailand, but made no promises regarding his future if he did return.

Peiping may consider it useful for Pridi to return--as a nonprovocative, retired "elder statesman"--as a token of its "earnest" desire for friendship and coexistence. Pridi's presence in Thailand would probably revive his still substantial following among civil servants and in nongovernmental intellectual circles, which Peiping might be able to exploit in the future.
