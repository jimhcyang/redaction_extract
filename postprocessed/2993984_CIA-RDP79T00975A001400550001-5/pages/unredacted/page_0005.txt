Comment: Since Ba Swe joined the government in 1952 there have been recurrent reports that he was in contact with the Communists.

In view of the declining military fortunes of the Communists, they could be expected to react favorably to any proposal by the opportunistic Ba Swe that they participate in the overthrow of the present government.
