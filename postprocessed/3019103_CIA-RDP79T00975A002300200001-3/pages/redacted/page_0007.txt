4. URUGUAYAN GOVERNMENT REPRESENTATIVE TO
VISIT PEIPING

Uruguay's honorary consul in Hong Kong told American consul general Drumwright on 6 December that he had instructions from Montevideo to go to Peiping to discuss the question of "relations" with the Chinese Communist government. He said he was leaving that day for Peiping and would be there two or three weeks.

Comment

No government in the western hemisphere maintains diplomatic relations with Communist China. Should Uruguay establish diplomatic relations, other Latin American governments might reassess the question of recognizing the Peiping regime.
