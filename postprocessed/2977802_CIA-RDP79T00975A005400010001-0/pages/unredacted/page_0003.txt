1 NOVEMBER 1960

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC
Reports of serious food shortages in cities.
Albanian premier, in major speech on 25 October, in effect reaffirmed support for Peiping in Sino-Soviet dispute.

II. ASIA-AFRICA
Situation in the Congo.

III. THE WEST
Bonn government apparently now trying to reduce tensions over Berlin access issue by seeking some improvement, at least for time being, in relations with Moscow.
