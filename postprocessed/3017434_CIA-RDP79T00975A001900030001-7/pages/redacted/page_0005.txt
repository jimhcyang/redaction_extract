4. Israeli prime minister says Egypt is "playing with fire":

Israeli prime minister Sharett told Ambassador Lawson on 1 April that Egypt is "playing with fire" and would be well advised to exercise restraint on the Israeli border. Sharett said he wished to emphasize that the situation is extremely tense and that all the elements for an explosion are present.

Comment: This reports emphasizes the likelihood, noted by the American army attaché in Tel Aviv on 30 March, that a "heavy" engagement between Israeli and Arab military forces will result from heightened border tension.
