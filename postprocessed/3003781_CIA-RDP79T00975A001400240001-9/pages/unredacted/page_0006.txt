LATIN AMERICA

6. Czechoslovakia and Poland may seek to establish missions in Panama:

Comment: The Polish minister to Mexico presented his credentials in Panama in November. At that time the Panamanian foreign minister stated that his country was merely following the big nations in maintaining diplomatic relations with Poland. The Czechs reportedly made fruitless overtures on this subject to the previous Panamanian government in 1951 and 1952.
