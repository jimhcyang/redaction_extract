USSR - Satellites: Certain Eastern European satellites have apparently been called on to increase pressure on the peasants to join collective farms. The latest issue of the Soviet party journal Kommunist points out that while collectivization should be a gradual process, liquidation of peasant opposition is necessary and inevitable. If Moscow presses the satellites to take this course, it would be a reversal of previous Soviet policy that each satellite should decide its own speed of socialization and would seem to indicate Moscow's dissatisfaction with the rate of satellite collectivization.
