12 APRIL 1958

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC

Possible Soviet missile-launching submarine sighted.

Soviet submarines engaged in extensive North Atlantic exercise.

Moscow orders Soviet research ship to maintain scheduled course near sector closed for US nuclear tests.

Soviet party journal calls on satellites to intensify pace of collectivization.

II. ASIA-AFRICA

Indonesia - Djakarta forces’ invasion of Tapanuh district may be part of assault on Padang and Bukittinggi.

Saudi Arabia may be forced to seek substantial foreign loan to stabilize currency.

Cairo considering purchase of Soviet civil jet airliners.

Ceylon - Linguistic controversy may cause new political unrest, civil disorder.

Rabat, Madrid encounter difficulties in transfer of southern protectorate area to Moroccan control.

III. THE WEST

Austria considers joining Common Market group if European Free Trade Area does not materialize.
