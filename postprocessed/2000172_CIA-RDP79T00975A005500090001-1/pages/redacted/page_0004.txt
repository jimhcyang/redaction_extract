II. ASIA-AFRICA

Arab States - France: At Nasir's behest the UAR National Assembly has urged all Arab governments to sever economic and political relations with France and to nationalize French properties in protest over Algeria. This appears to be the opening move of a new campaign against the French in the Arab states, and Nasir may soon follow up the assembly's action by nationalizing French investments and expelling French nationals.
