SECURITY INFORMATION

5. Iran threatens to break diplomatic relations with Poland:

Comment: The new regime in Iran has shown itself increasingly sensitive to unfavorable publicity in the foreign press, but this is the first indication that it might break diplomatic relations with one of the countries involved.

Several previous Iranian protests to the Polish government have been of no avail. If Poland is convinced that Iran will break relations, however, it will probably give the required assurances because of its desire to maintain political and economic contacts.
