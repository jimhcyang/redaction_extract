In certain localities its members are not even opposed to affiliating with the Christian Democrats in order to defeat the Stalinist Communists. Comment: The Magnani-Cucchi movement's active support of the anti-Communist electoral coalition in Bologna and in other nearby localities should improve the government's chances of wresting control of many municipalities in the region of Emilia from the Italian Communist Party.
