Saudi Arabian regiment into south Jordan to prevent Israeli seizure of Aqaba.

Saudi forces in the Gulf of Aqaba area have been reinforced and that a Saudi force may enter Jordan shortly. After being cut off by the Israeli drive across Sinai, Egyptian forces stationed at the entrance to the gulf escaped across the straits to join the Saudi garrison. Israel has, meanwhile, informed the United States that it has reliable information that Egypt has transferred 20 IL-28 jet bombers and an unknown number of MIG-15 jet fighters to Saudi Arabia, where Israel fears they are regrouping for an attack.

13 Egyptian aircraft had arrived at Saudi airfields on the previous day, and the American embassy in Jidda reported the arrival and departure of five IL-28 jet bombers.
