22 DECEMBER 1960

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC

USSR-Indonesia--First delivery en route under $277,000,000 naval aid agreement.

II. ASIA-AFRICA

Laos--Chinese Communists continue threats; Phoumi force from Vientiane reported pursuing Kong Le.

Congo--Situation in Stanleyville explosive.

III. THE WEST

Portugal-South Africa--Closer military cooperation in Africa planned.

Brazil--President-elect Quadros plans neutralist foreign policy.

LATE ITEM

Saudi Arabia--King Saud moves to resume control of government.
