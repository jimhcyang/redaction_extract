SOUTHEAST ASIA

2. Comment on Laotian government policy toward Pathet Lao:

The Laotian government appears to be moving closer toward a coalition with the Viet Minh-backed Pathet Lao, a course previously rejected by Premier Katay. Katay announced on 8 January that members of the Pathet Lao would receive appointments "in accordance with the laws," but he left unexplained the nature of those appointments.
