LATE ITEM

*USSR: At about 0640Z (0240 EDT) today the USSR successfully launched a probable lunar probe vehicle from the Tyura Tam Missile Test Range. The vehicle is believed to be the same one which the Soviets had tried to launch on three other occasions since 6 September. If the indications that the operation is to be a lunar probe are correct and assuming that the vehicle's flight is successful, it will probably be about 35 hours before the probe reaches the vicinity of the moon.
