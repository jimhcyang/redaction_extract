12 SEPTEMBER 1959

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC
Chou En-lai again calls for "friendly negotiations" on border dispute but complains India has resorted to force.
Moroccan official says Soviet charge has been offering arms of "any and all types."

II. ASIA—AFRICA
Laos--French call Laotian military reports exaggerated but view internal situation as serious.
Soviet-Iranian efforts to improve relations may be resumed.
Pakistan—-President Ayub's trip to East Pakistan moderately successful; no indications there of efforts to oppose his regime.
