LATIN AMERICA

10. Forceful attempt to oust Costa Rican regime may be imminent:

"Internal trouble" in Costa Rica is
nearing, according to Nicaraguan pres-
ident Somoza. The expected outbreak,
he told Ambassador Whelan on 19 Decem-
ber, will be a purely domestic affair, and there will be no inva-
sion and no "overt" acts by Nicaragua or any other country.

Regarding Nicaragua's dispute with
Costa Rica, Somoza said he would demand Costa Rican presi-
dent Figueres' resignation and abstention from politics as the
price of a settlement.
