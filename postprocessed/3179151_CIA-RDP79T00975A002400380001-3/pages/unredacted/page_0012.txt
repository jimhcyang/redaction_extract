Israeli foreign minister Sharett believes the dismissal of General Glubb from command of Jordan's Arab Legion must be considered part of Egyptian prime minister Nasr's "encirclement" of Israel.

Nasr was on the march and operates through conquest by penetration. The Israeli foreign minister also said he believed the Arab Legion might lose some of its effectiveness, although this might be a slow process. He feared development of a corresponding lack of responsibility and discipline which would intensify Israel's border security problems.
