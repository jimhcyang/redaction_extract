20 JULY 1960

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC
1. USSR to resume substantial arms shipments to UAR; also apparently will provide Iraq with MIG-19 aircraft.
2. USSR may be preparing for another space vehicle launching.

II. ASIA-AFRICA
3. Republic of Congo--Belgian Government considering recognition of Katanga Province's secession; situation in northeast Congo tense, and rising unemployment in Leopoldville Province poses threat of riots.
4. Iraqi oil exports through Persian Gulf likely to be closed down unless Qasim revokes decree raising loading charges.

III. THE WEST
5. Cuba--Castro indicates concern over growing church opposition to Cuba's increased Communist ties.
6. West German defense minister "dubious" of wisdom of Adenauer's position of avoiding "provocative actions" in Berlin.
