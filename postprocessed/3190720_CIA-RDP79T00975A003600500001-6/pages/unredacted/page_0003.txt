28 APRIL 1958

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC

Soviet vehicle launching at Tyura Tam; see Guided Missile Intelligence Committee statement in Daily Brief on page i.

II. ASIA-AFRICA

Nasir's plans for Soviet tour starting 29 April call for considerable negotiation, mostly economic.

Poor Syrian grain harvest will strain UAR budget and may hurt Nasir politically.

European extremists in Algiers renew call for army takeover in Paris; rumors of coup d'etat in Algeria recur.

Indonesia - Bloc jet trainers reportedly will be offloaded at Surabaya; IL-14 transports expected within two weeks. Bukittinggi may now have fallen to central government forces.

Construction of military installation on Yemeni coast at entrance to Red Sea progressing under direction of Soviet bloc engineers.

III. THE WEST

Adenauer stressing disarmament as summit topic; opponents of government's nuclear arms policy will exploit Mikoyan offer.

Guatemalan President Ydigoras ready with propaganda campaign agitating for "return" of British Honduras.
