Guatemala - British Honduras: President Ydigoras is prepared to carry out a strong propaganda campaign to promote the idea of Guatemalan sovereignty over neighboring British Honduras. His primary motive is to strengthen his domestic political position by taking a strong stand on an issue with emotional appeal to all Guatemalans. He threatens a series of dramatic gestures, including an appeal in the United Nations for Latin American solidarity against "colonialism."
