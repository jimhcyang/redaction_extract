16 APRIL 1958

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC
1) Communist China pressing India for civil air agreement; Nehru has signed recently with USSR, may accede to Peiping's request.
2) High-ranking bloc diplomatic delegation to attend Argentine President's inauguration on 1 May.

II. ASIA-AFRICA
3) Indonesia - Central government naval units in place off west coast of Sumatra. Landings reported delayed for a few days.
4) Violence and sabotage continue on Cyprus.
5) Gaza strip demonstrations part of Nasir's campaign to arouse Palestinian Arabs against King Husayn of Jordan.

III. THE WEST
6) Adenauer believes West German furor over nuclear armament dictates de-emphasis of reunification issue at summit meeting.
7) Brazilian Government attacking resurgent Communists, but any legislative action unlikely.

LATE ITEM
8) Fall of Gaillard government makes any French concessions on North Africa less likely.
