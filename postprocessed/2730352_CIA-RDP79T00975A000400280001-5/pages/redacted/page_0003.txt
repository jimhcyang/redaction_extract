FAR EAST

1. Soviet pilots from East Germany arrive in Manchuria for combat:

a group of 100 Soviet jet pilots, ranging in rank from captain to lieutenant colonel, arrived in Mukden in late September. These pilots allegedly were drawn from Soviet Air Force units in East Germany and were scheduled to return there after a two-month combat tour in Korea.

The US Far East Air Force comments that "it had been assumed on the basis of fluctuating tactics and markings of aircraft that units were being rotated...."
