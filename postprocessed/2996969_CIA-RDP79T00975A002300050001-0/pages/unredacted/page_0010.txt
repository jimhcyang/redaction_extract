Several MIG's reportedly have been assembled at Dikheila airfield
and are probably now undergoing test flights. The first
MIG aircraft apparently were delivered to the port of Alexandria by the Soviet freighter Krasnodar on 22 October and
42 Soviet bloc technicians arrived four days later to help
assemble them.

No Soviet vessels have discharged arms
at Alexandria since 3 November, but port authorities are expecting a new series of arrivals the latter part of this month,
according to an untested source with good contacts in Egyptian naval circles.
