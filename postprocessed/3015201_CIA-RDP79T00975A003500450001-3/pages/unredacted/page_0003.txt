25 JANUARY 1958

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC
New Soviet atomic test in Semipalatinsk area. (1)
Soviet nuclear-powered submarines may be under construction at Severodvinsk. (2)
USSR shows interest in establishing diplomatic contact with Saudi Arabia. (3)

II. ASIA-AFRICA
Formation of rival Indonesian government in Sumatra still undecided. (4)

III. THE WEST
France expected to press Tunisia for joint controls on Algerian border. (5)
Rapacki plan for neutral zone in Europe receives negative response from North Atlantic Council members (6)
Venezuela - Citizen groups increase pressure on governing junta. (7)
