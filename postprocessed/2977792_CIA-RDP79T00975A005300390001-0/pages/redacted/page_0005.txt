Argentina: While the crisis over army demands for changes in President Frondizi's advisers and policies has been eased, the situation is still troubled by army unrest. At least 17 of the some 50 generals have presented their resignations. Frondizi is reported to have given in to one of their demands by accepting the resignation of War Secretary Larcher, who is trying to reduce the power of army commander Toranzo Montero. A special meeting of all army generals has been called for 17 October.
