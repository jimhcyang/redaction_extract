It is believed in some coalition quarters that if the West does not take the initiative on the unity question along the lines of the US proposal, the USSR might resort to military threats and pressure on Berlin to bring about four-power talks.

The US High Commission believes that the leak of the American proposal has helped convince the Germans that the United States has not closed the door on German unity, and notes evidence of a "wholesome anxiety" that the US may have an alternative policy to integration.
