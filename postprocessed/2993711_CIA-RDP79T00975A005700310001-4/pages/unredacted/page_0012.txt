On 28 May, only three flights occurred which may have been airlift associated. No flights were noted into Laos. Airlift activity on 29 May included at least six and possibly nine IL-14 sorties to Xieng Khouang. A relatively high level of airlift activity was scheduled for 30 May, with flights scheduled for the Vinh/Dong Hoi area, Xieng Khouang, and Phong Saly.

On 5 May, a radio station was set up at Vang Vieng for the purpose of serving Souvanna Phouma's delegation to the cease-fire negotiations. This station is in direct contact with the Pathet Lao headquarters at Khang Khay in the Plaines des Jarres.
