SECURITY INFORMATION

It is understood that the demonstrators will fight any civilian crowds which the government may raise, but have not decided on the tactics to be used if the troops are called out.

Mossadeq, now in prison awaiting trial, allegedly has approved these plans.

Comment: A demonstration may be attempted in connection with Mossadeq's trial, but it probably will not be large. Most of the pro-Mossadeq groups are relatively insignificant, and it is not believed that the Tudeh is willing to make an all-out effort now. The security forces should be able to control any disturbances.
