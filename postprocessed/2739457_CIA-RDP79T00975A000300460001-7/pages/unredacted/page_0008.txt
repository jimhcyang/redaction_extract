8. Further Niemoeller opposition to rearmament expected:

A resolution opposing West German contribution to European defense is likely to be presented at the meeting in early September of the Council of the German Evangelical (Protestant) Church, according to its head Dr. Otto Dibelius.

In an interview with High Commissioner McCloy, Dibelius stated that the anti-rearmament forces under Dr. Martin Niemoeller would offer such a resolution, which would be defeated, and that they would then make an appeal to the public to resist any legislation on a defense contribution. Dibelius pointed out that many of the Protestant youth now support the Niemoeller position.

Comment: Pastor Niemoeller, now the head of the Evangelical Church of Hesse, has made periodic pronouncements on world affairs. He leads a wing of the Protestant Church which combines attacks on various Allied policies with a neutralist line.

Despite a demand last April by Church leaders that he stay out of politics, he has lately continued his old habits. There is no real indication of how much support his minority receives within the Church.
