3. SAUDI TERMS FOR OPENING TALKS UNACCEPTABLE TO BRITISH

Saudi Arabia has agreed to open direct talks with Britain provided the situation which existed in the Buraimi oasis region before the arbitration attempt last fall is restored.

Comment - British rejection of the Saudi terms would encourage King Saud to take the issue to the UN Security Council, where he expects American support. London probably hopes eventually to reach a settlement by offering the Saudis territory elsewhere in Trucial Oman.
