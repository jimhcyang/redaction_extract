3. ISRAEL TO COUNTER EGYPTIAN COMPENSATION
DEMAND WITH OWN CLAIMS

Israel will submit to the United Nations before 6 January a statement of the damages caused Israel by Egypt during the past eight years,

The statement will assert that damage from Egyptian infiltrators and terrorists alone amounts to about $250,000,000, and the total claim will greatly exceed any estimate which Egypt might make of the damages inflicted by the Sinai campaign.

Comment
The Egyptian foreign minister has been instructed by Cairo to press Egypt's claims for compensation, a move also urged by Moscow, and Britain and France as well as Israel are developing counterclaims. According to some reports, the British intend to cite the equipment seized by Egypt from British bases in the Suez as one of the items on their side.
