Two Polish freighters are presently en route to Alexandria carrying unidentified crated cargoes from Satellite ports.

There is reason to believe that the petroleum products carried to the Suez area in early December aboard the Soviet tankers Gregory Vakulentchuk and Sverdlovsk are intended for military use, and that the cargo of the Vakulentchuk may have included jet fuel.
