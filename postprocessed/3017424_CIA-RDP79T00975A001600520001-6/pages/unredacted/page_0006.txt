LATIN AMERICA

5. Comment on new Argentine "supercabinet":

Peron's appointment on 22 July of a five-man "supercabinet," which is to act as an all-powerful junta in the formulation of government policy, may be merely a step to help correct known government inefficiency. The action lends plausibility, however, to the many reports that Peron is seriously ill.

If Peron is gravely ill, the "supercabinet" would be a convenient medium for transferring power to Vice President Teisaire, who won his post in a special election in April and in the "supercabinet" now occupies a key role as secretary for political affairs. The other new appointees include Foreign Minister Remorino and three other members of the previous 21-man cabinet, which has now been reduced to 16 ministers.
