NEAR EAST - AFRICA

3. Prominent Iranian nationalist assumes leadership of antigovernment coalition:
Hoseyn Makki, formerly a prominent National Front leader, has accepted the leadership of the anti-Zahedi National Resistance Movement.

Comment: The National Resistance Movement, a coalition of groups desiring Mossadeq's return to power, has been unable to promote effective antigovernment measures. Makki, a prominent ultranationalist and rabble rouser, may be able to provide the leadership that the Movement has heretofore lacked.

Makki probably would be more interested in advancing his own political fortunes through control of the party than in supporting Mossadeq, with whom he broke shortly before the latter's removal from office.
