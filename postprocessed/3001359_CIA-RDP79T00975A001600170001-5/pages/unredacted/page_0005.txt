SOUTH ASIA

4. Indian troops moved to West Bengal to control possible Hindu-Moslem disturbances:

Home Minister Katju stated on 9 June that about 5,000 Indian troops had recently been moved into the Calcutta area to control any disturbances that might arise in West Bengal if conditions deteriorate and Hindu-Moslem clashes occur in East Pakistan.

Comment: This precautionary move, which confirms earlier rumors, shows the Indian government's determination to maintain order in West Bengal. India's precautions are likely to be unnecessary, as East Pakistan has been quiet since the promulgation of Governor's Rule on 30 May.
