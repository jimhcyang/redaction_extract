2. RESIGNATION OF FOREIGN PILOTS MAY IMPAIR
FUNCTIONING OF SUEZ CANAL

The canal company has information that Egypt is negotiating with Yugoslavia for additional pilots, but it considers six months the minimum period necessary for a generally expert person to become an efficient canal pilot.

the only effective way for Egypt to sabotage the canal would be to sink a ship loaded with cement across the channel--an obstruction which could be removed in less than a month.

Comment  A large number of the company's employees will probably accept its offer of a generous financial settlement in return for signing a declaration of loyalty to the company and expressing a desire for repatriation by 15 August. The departure of a large percentage of foreign employees would considerably impair the effective operation of the canal.
