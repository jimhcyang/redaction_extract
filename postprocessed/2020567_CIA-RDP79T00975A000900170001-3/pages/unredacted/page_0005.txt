4. Jordanian Prime Minister may resign in November:

Jordanian Prime Minister Abul Huda may resign after the parliamentary speech from the throne on 1 November,

Comment: This information may be a plant designed to obtain British support for the Prime Minister, against whom opposition is increasing, particularly among the Palestinian Arabs now included in the kingdom.
