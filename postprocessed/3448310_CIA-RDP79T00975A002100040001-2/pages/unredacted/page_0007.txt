5. Hammarskjold again advises Peiping to release American airmen:

UN Secretary General Hammarskjold has telegraphed Chou En-lai that continued delay in releasing the 11 American airmen detained in China would be likely to hamper a "constructive approach to wider problems." Hammarskjold's message warned Chou that the question of releasing the airmen was not an appropriate matter for bargaining.

Hammarskjold expressed the hope that it will be possible for him to avoid making to the UN a report of failure in the airmen's case.
