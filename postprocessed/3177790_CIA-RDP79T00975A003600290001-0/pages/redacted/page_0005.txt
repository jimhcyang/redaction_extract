Japan-Taiwan: Nationalist China has apparently rejected a Japanese proposal which would have deferred a showdown on the Communist flag issue until after the Japanese national elections which are expected this spring. While both sides remain willing to discuss a solution, Taipei continues to demand Tokyo's early assurance that the Chinese Communist flag will not fly over a projected trade mission in Japan. Peiping has accused Japan of blocking enforcement of the recent private trade agreement. (Page 4)
