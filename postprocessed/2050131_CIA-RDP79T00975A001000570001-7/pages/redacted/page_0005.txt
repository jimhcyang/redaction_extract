5. Danes adamant on early return of MIG-15 to Poland:

The Danish Foreign Ministry informed the British Ambassador on 9 March that test flights of the Polish MIG-15 which landed on Bornholm last week will not be permitted and that all tests must be completed by 16 March. It indicated that this was the government's final position and stated that a "Danish facade" for examination of the plane must be maintained.

According to the US Embassy in Copenhagen, American technicians now believe a reasonably complete primary examination can be made within four days.
