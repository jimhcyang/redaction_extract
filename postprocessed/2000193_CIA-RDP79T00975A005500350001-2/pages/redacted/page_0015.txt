Of particular concern to Camerounian President Ahidjo are radical elements in Southern Cameroons affiliated with the extremist wing of the Communist-influenced Union of the Cameroons Populations in Cameroun republic. These extremists are continuing to carry on terrorist activities in southwestern Cameroun against the republic's pro-Western government.

British officials in Nigeria have expressed confidence that their battalion in the trust territory is capable of coping with any disorders which might develop. An additional battalion to the territory could be airlifted on short notice if necessary from the strategic reserve in Britain or Kenya.
