Pursuant to resolutions adopted by the UN General Assembly, the alternatives posed in both areas are union with newly independent Nigeria or union with Cameroun, a French trust territory prior to its independence last year. Observers expect the northern sector to opt for Nigeria despite an intensive last minute effort by the Cameroun government to win support there. British officials anticipate that southern voters will favor Cameroun, probably by a small margin. (Backup, Page 5) (Map)
