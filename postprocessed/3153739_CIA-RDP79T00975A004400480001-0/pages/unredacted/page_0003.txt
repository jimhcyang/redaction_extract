8 MAY 1959

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC

II. ASIA-AFRICA

Iraq - Communists renew demands for overt political role; more bloc technicians arriving.
Shah of Iran convinced USSR is determined to overthrow him.
King Husayn and new Jordanian prime minister forecast no policy changes.
Indonesia planning first direct approach to USSR about buying arms.
Sino-Soviet bloc influence in Cambodia abetted by leftist Information Ministry.
Ceylon - Bandaranaike faces another potential crisis as bulk of cabinet threatens to resign.

III. THE WEST

One of Adenauer's top advisers sees reduced threat of war over Berlin.
Austria - Parliamentary elections on 10 May; no major shifts expected.
