22 DECEMBER 1959

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC
Soviet spokesmen seek to blame US for impasse in Geneva nuclear test talks.

II. ASIA—AFRICA
Nehru's flat rejection of latest Peiping note leaves Sino-Indian border dispute deadlocked.
Iranian artillery movement in Abadan area sustains tension with Iraq over control of Shatt-al-Arab navigation.
Morocco--Tension between King and leftists eased for time being.

III. THE WEST
Brazil suggests Latin American chiefs of state attend April dedication of new capital.
