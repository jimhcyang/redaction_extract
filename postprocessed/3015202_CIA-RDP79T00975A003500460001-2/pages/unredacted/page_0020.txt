In Guatemala, General Ydigoras has indicated readiness to bargain with the supporters of Cruz Salazar. Congress, which will select the president, since no candidate received a majority of votes, appears increasingly inclined to select Ydigoras because his lead, especially in Guatemala City, is so large. The decision will probably be made public within ten days after Congress begins checking ballots on 27 January.
