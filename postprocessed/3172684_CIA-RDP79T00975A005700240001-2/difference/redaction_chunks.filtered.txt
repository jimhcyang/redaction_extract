It is preparing to bolster its propaganda outlet--now centered in a weekly paper Thunder--with a daily printed on presses financed by American, British, and possibly some Cuban Communists. The PPP could pick up 8 of the 10 doubtful seats to win a majority if its two rivals continue to work against each other, particularly in view of the Guianese tendency to vote along racial lines.
