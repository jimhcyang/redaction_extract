RUMANIAN COMMUNIST PARTY CALLS FOR REVISION OF ECONOMIC PLANS IN FAVOR OF CONSUMER The 30 December communique of the Rumanian Workers Party central committee calls for a major overhaul of the economy in order to increase agricultural and consumer goods output, according to the American legation in Bucharest.
