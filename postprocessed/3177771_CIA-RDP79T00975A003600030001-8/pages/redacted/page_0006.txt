French Government: Premier Gaillard faces open revolt from the conservative wing of his coalition over his proposal to hold the 1958 military budget to the ceiling agreed on in December 1957. Independent party deputies have threatened to precipitate a crisis unless he agrees this week to reinforce the army in Algeria substantially, even at the expense of France's NATO-committed units.
