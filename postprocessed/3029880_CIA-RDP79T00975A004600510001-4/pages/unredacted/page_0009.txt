Khrushchev to Qasim

Khrushchev has solicited the Iraqi premier's views on Middle Eastern problems prior to the forthcoming meetings with President Eisenhower. Khrushchev said he intends to tell the President that the USSR is determined to "prevent imperialist conspiracies" against Iraq. Khrushchev presumably also gave Qasim assurances that the USSR would not be a party to any agreements behind his back.

Moscow has been careful over the past several months to maintain friendly ties with both Baghdad and Cairo. In addition to such gestures as profuse congratulations by Khrushchev and other Soviet leaders on the occasion of Iraq's and the UAR's revolution-day celebrations, the USSR has been implementing its economic and military assistance programs with both countries.
