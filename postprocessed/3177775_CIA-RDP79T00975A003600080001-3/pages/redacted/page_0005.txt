Cuba: President Batista has agreed to mediation by the church-sponsored "Committee of National Harmony" between his government and the opposition. Army leaders, on whom Batista is ultimately dependent, may be taking an independent interest in the mediation efforts. Rebel leaders, however, are skeptical of mediation talks. Sabotage and terrorism continue, and a student strike in several areas is gaining momentum.
