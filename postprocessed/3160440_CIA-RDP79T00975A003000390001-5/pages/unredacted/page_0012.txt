9. SAUDI ARABIAN LOAN EXPECTED TO BOLSTER MOROCCAN GOVERNMENT

Comment on:

This loan, together with the $20,000,000 aid agreement concluded with the United States on 2 April, can be expected to bolster Rabat's position in financial negotiations with France. Paris has been withholding promised credits to Morocco in the hope of gaining military and economic concessions from the financially hard-pressed Moroccan government.
