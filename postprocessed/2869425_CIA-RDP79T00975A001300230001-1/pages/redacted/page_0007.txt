Conant believes, however, that State Secretary Hallstein will stress to Adenauer the importance of early negotiations on the Saar, so that a settlement might be reached in time to allow the EDC treaty to be brought before the French parliament in November.

Comment: Vice Premier Paul Reynaud on 1 October reiterated Premier Laniel's opinion that only the Saar issue is holding up French EDC ratification. The French expect Adenauer to begin preliminary talks with High Commissioner Francois-Poncet as early as 15 October.

Unless EDC debates in the National Assembly start in November, they will probably be postponed until next year because of the French presidential election in December.
