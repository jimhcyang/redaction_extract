6. REVOLUTIONARY ACTION IN CUBA REPORTED IMMINENT

Comment on:

New revolutionary attempts are expected
in Cuba between 15 and 22 May. The action
may be touched off by several assassination attempts. Eusebio Mujal, secretary general of the Cuban
Workers' Confederation, who has been accused by opposition
elements of "selling out" to President Batista, is considered
a likely victim.

an atmosphere of tenseness exists in Havana and that various
Cuban government officials are showing apprehension, and
anxiety. Outbreaks and assassination attempts have been expected to increase prior to 20 May, Cuban independence day.

16 May 57 Current Intelligence Bulletin Page 8
