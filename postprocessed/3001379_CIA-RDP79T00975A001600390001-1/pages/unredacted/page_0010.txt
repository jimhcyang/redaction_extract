The Costa Ricans participating in such a move would be adherents of ex-dictator Calderon Guardia, who controlled the Communist-supported Picado government overthrown by Figueres in 1948. Despite Calderon's record of co-operation with Communists, rightist regimes in the Caribbean area have long backed him because of their hatred for Figueres. Figueres, who relinquished control of the government in 1949, regained the presidency in a regular election last July and has since given encouragement and probably material assistance to exiled opponents of these rightist regimes.
