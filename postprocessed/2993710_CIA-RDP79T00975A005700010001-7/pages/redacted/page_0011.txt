The PAG was also reportedly ready to issue a statement signifying its willingness to open negotiations with France on the understanding that parallel negotiations would not be conducted with a rival Moslem group, but held up the statement so as not to hamper De Gaulle during the insurrection. Despite the improved prospects for opening negotiations, however, the two sides remain divided on many substantive issues.
