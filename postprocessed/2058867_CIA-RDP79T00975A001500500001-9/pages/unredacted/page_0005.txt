SOUTHEAST ASIA

4. Viet Minh expanding organization inside Hanoi:

The Viet Minh is continuing to expand its organization inside Hanoi.

All Viet Minh units in the city have a sabotage section.

but considerable quantities of weapons and explosives have been smuggled into the city during the past two months.

Comment: The Viet Minh has succeeded in keeping its organization inside Hanoi well concealed and estimates of its strength are not reliable. The general deterioration of the French position will considerably enhance the Viet Minh's recruiting capabilities.

French forces in and near Hanoi now total about ten battalions. It is therefore unlikely that the Viet Minh could capture Hanoi at the present time.
