Warsaw's editorial of 22 October, which endorsed the changes in Poland, criticized President Eisenhower and Secretary Dulles for their remarks on Poland, and reaffirmed Polish solidarity with socialist countries, was republished in Pravda and Izvestia on 23 October. The American embassy in Moscow believes that the USSR is attempting to place the best possible appearance on Polish developments and is leading up to an authoritative statement which will publicly approve the new regime.
