9. Yugoslav attitude toward US reportedly "cooling":

According to a report from the French ambassador in Belgrade, Yugoslavia's attitude toward the United States has recently "cooled" because of the long delay of the United States in designating an ambassador, the reduction in American aid and slowness of economic and military deliveries, the failure of the United States to respond to feelers put out by Tito for an invitation to Washington, and the rise of "McCarthyism" in the United States.
