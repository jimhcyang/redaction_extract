SOUTHEAST ASIA

2. Viet Minh reportedly planning attack on Tonkin delta:

The Viet Minh is preparing to employ three divisions in an attack on the northern portion of the Tonkin delta,

One division, the 316th, is actually moving toward Dong Trieu, a point inside the northern perimeter which is to be the first enemy objective. In addition, quantities of material have been moving southward from a border supply point.
