Text: WESTERN EUROPE 3. West German Social Democrats to step up drive against Paris agreements: Erich Ollenhauer, chairman of the West German Social Democratic Party (SPD), told a meeting of party executives on 12 December that the SPD will launch a strenuous propaganda campaign against Bundestag ratification of the Paris agreements. Plans have been made for mass distribution of pamphlets and posters, radio speeches and public rallies, with addresses by leading Socialists. Such talk, he said, disrupts the party's present campaign against remilitarization.
