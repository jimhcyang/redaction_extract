THE ARAB-ISRAELI SITUATION
(Information as of 1700, 7 June)

Jordan Arab Legion sources charged that Israeli soldiers crossed into Jordanian territory on 6 June at Habla in the Qalqiliya area. Two Israelis were seriously wounded in the clash. An Egyptian military spokesman said that Israeli armored cars opened fire on Egyptian territory at the Gaza strip. (Press)

The Jordanian army is recruiting and training fedayeen at five schools, the graduates will be sent gradually into Israel, adding that these fedayeen have no connection with Egypt's fedayeen activities, which are under the control of the Egyptian military attache.
