5. FRENCH CHARGE IN LAOS UNSYMPATHETIC TO US-UK POLICY THERE

Comment on:

The British ambassador in Vientiane has concluded that the French cannot be counted on to support US-UK efforts to block the entry of Pathet Lao officials into the Laotian cabinet. The American ambassador comments that Soulie has advised Paris not to oppose the formation of a coalition government but to support the Laotian government's 28 December agreement with the Pathet Lao.

The French may feel that by avoiding close identification with US-UK policy they will be in a position to increase their influence in a unified Laos.
