Actions proposed in this article allegedly will include: establishment of diplomatic relations with the USSR; a call for "understanding" instead of condemnation of the Cuban revolution; and an expression of hope for better Latin American relations with Washington, but with an insistence that the nature of these relations must be changed.

Quadros will also call for a conference of all nations interested in studying the problems of the underdeveloped nations, inviting countries from the Middle East, Asia, and Africa, as well as from Latin America.
