Recent US measures to conserve dollars have aroused new concern in Japan about its need for expanded export markets to maintain the present standard of living for a growing population. In addition, Tokyo is reviewing its position on the question of UN membership for Communist China in order not to become isolated from the Afro-Asian group of nations.
