III. THE WEST

Argentina: The armed forces are still split over the appointment of new top military commanders. The army secretary has offered his resignation to avoid a showdown. Although there is surface calm, the situation remains uncertain pending the completion of negotiations now under way to select compromise commanders. This has been demanded by moderate military elements as well as by the minority group of persistent plotters.
