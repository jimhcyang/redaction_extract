THE ARAB-ISRAELI SITUATION
(Information as of 1700, 14 August)

The French chairman of the Israeli-Lebanese Mixed Armistice Commission is being held in custody by the Lebanese police, according to a press report from Beirut, No official reason was given for the detention of the UN truce officer. (Press)

The Damascus branch of the Arab Joint Command advised Cairo that an Israeli patrol crossed the Israeli-Syrian truce line on 13 August but withdrew after meeting machine gun resistance.
