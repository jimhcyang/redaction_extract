4 JANUARY 1960

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC

Latest Chinese Communist note to India contains no significant new offers.

Sino-Soviet bloc extended slightly over $1 billion in new aid credits to non-Communist countries in 1959.

II. ASIA-AFRICA

Laos--Negotiations continue among anti-Communist elements for new government.

New Iraqi law opens way for legal political parties; Qasim reported planning cabinet and military changes.

Belgium apparently ready to accelerate Congolese progress toward independence.
