6. Italy opposes new NATO commitments before elections

Ambassador Bunker in Rome states that because of next spring's national elections, the present time is definitely unfavorable for presenting the proposed bilateral agreement covering US-financed "military production in Italy.

The Italian Government does not want to make any new written agreements involving NATO commitments which would require parliamentary approval before the elections. It fears the effect left-wing attacks on such measures would have on the electorate.
