4 AUGUST 1959

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC
Moscow propaganda says Nixon's
speech evaded issue of military
bases, distorted Soviet foreign
policy.

II. ASIA-AFRICA
Laos--Premier "strengthened in belief" that regular North Vietnamese
forces are involved in attacks on Laotian Army posts.

Morocco--Domestic crisis and anti-monarchy maneuvers may have
prompted King's hasty return from France.

Yemen--Crown Prince Badr and his
Egyptian supporters increasingly apprehensive over impending return of Imam.

UAR intelligence reports Iraq has discovered "another Communist plot"
against Qasim regime.

Ceylon Government remains shaky despite winning parliamentary vote
on budget.

Ministerial meeting of nine independent African states opens today in Monrovia.

III. THE WEST
Argentina--Peronista and Communist
labor leaders threaten to intensify
strikes against stabilization program.
