24 FEBRUARY 1960

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC

Peiping and Moscow sharply divergent in propaganda on eve of President Eisenhower's visit to Latin America.

Soviet officials complain to UAR about Nasir's verbal attacks on international Communism.

II. ASIA-AFRICA

UAR military alert continuing although no further build-up of strength in Sinai.

India--Formation of cabinet in Kerala resolves coalition differences for present; conflicts will hamper efforts to restore effective government.

Japan restive over South Korean seizure and detention of fishermen.
