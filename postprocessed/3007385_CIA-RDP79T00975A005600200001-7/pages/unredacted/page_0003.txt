23 March 1961
CENTRAL INTELLIGENCE BULLETIN

CONTENTS

1. Situation in Laos. (Page 1)

2. Warsaw Pact meeting in Moscow scheduled for 28 March,
day after SEATO Council of Ministers meeting opens in
Bangkok. (Page 1)

3. Congo: Tshombe begins trip today to Leopoldville, Brazza-
ville, Abidjan, and Monrovia; hopes to gain support for
concept of loose confederation. (Page 2)

4. Vietnam: North Vietnamese-directed guerrillas gives timetable for acts of
violence to sabotage 9 April election to South. (Page 2)

5. Communist China: Foreign Minister Chen Yi says
Peiping will not enter UN until US ends "occupation"
of Taiwan. (Page 3)

6. Afghanistan: Daud to visit Moscow; will probably discuss
Soviet aid. (Page 3)

7. Ethiopia: Troops in Addis Ababa protest against low pay;
Emperor promises investigation to rectify inequities.
(Page 4)

8. Morocco: King Hassan II exploiting recent capture
and release of foreign oil technicians to further his
claims to Spanish Sahara. (Page 4)

9. Watch Committee conclusions. (Page 5)
