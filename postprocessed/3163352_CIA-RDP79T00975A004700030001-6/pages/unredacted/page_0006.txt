Obstacles facing the rebels include Tunisian President Bourguiba's opposition to the movement of arms shipments through Tunisia as well as the increased effectiveness of French barriers along the Algerian border. French sensitivity to such shipments may lead to renewed reconnaissance overflights of Libya.

Watch Committee conclusion--Middle East: Situations susceptible of direct exploitation by Sino-Soviet bloc action which would jeopardize US interests exist in the Middle East, particularly in Iraq and Iran. The initiation of significant hostilities is unlikely in this area in the immediate future.
