7. ARMY MAY SEIZE CONTROL IN ECUADOR

Comment
The approach of the 3 June national elections for president, vice president and congress has occasioned numerous reports of plots to gain control of the government by force. The army is the most important factor in Ecuadoran politics.

In the past 30 years Ecuador has had some 22 presidents, only one of whom was regularly elected and served out his four-year term. Velasco was ousted from the presidency in 1935 and again in 1947 because of his dictatorial measures.
