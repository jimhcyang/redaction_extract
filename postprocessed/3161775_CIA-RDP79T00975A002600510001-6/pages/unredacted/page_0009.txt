THE ARAB-ISRAELI SITUATION
(Information as of 1700, 27 July)

As a result of Egypt's nationalization of the Suez Canal Company, a conservative member of the British parliament, Sir Charles Boothby, has announced that he will urge the British government next week to "reconsider its decision not to allow Israel to purchase arms for its defense." (Press)
