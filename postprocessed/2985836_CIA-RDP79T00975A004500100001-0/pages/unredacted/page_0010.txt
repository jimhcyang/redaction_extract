The senior Yemeni representative in Cairo has proposed that Hasan agree, in the event of prolonged illness of the Imam, to Badr's appointment as future Imam, with Hasan to become head of the government. It is not certain that Badr has approved such a proposal. Although Hasan's initial reaction was reported to have been reserved, some of his supporters have in the past advanced such a proposal.
