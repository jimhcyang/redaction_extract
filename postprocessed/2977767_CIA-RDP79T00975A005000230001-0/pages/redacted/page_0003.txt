CENTRAL INTELLIGENCE BULLETIN
26 March 1960

DAILY BRIEF

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC

USSR-UAR: A high-level Soviet economic delegation is in the UAR discussing plans for implementing the Soviet aid program in Syria in response to Cairo's request that economic development there be stepped up.] The $150,000,000 credit extended by the USSR to Syria in 1957 for land and transport development has remained largely unused, in part because of Syrian inaction. Plans for use of this credit now are being redrafted, and additional credits for industrial projects may be offered.
