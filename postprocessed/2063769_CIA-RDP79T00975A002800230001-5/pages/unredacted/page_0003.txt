1. NEW SOVIET MOVES IN MIDDLE EAST AIMED
PARTICULARLY AT ISRAEL

Bulganin will send additional letters to Eden, Mollet and Ben-Gurion demand-
ing withdrawal of foreign troops from Egypt, payment of rep-
arations, stationing of an international force between Egypt and Israel, and estab-
lishment of bound-
aries between Israel and the Arab states on the basis of the 1947 partition reso-
lutions.
