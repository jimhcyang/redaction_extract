WESTERN EUROPE

10. Pella may announce within week intention to act on EDC:

Ambassador Luce in Rome reports that there is reason to believe Premier Pella is considering a pre-Christmas announcement of his government's intention to introduce EDC when Parliament reconvenes on 15 January.

The possibility of this move was pointed out to the ambassador by ex-premier De Gasperi, who expressed the belief that EDC could be ratified without a Trieste settlement if there were an amendment to the EDC treaty which would permit a delay in Italy's full adherence until a reasonable solution of the Trieste issue had been found.

Comment: An Italian Foreign Ministry official recently told the American embassy that in his opinion the effect of the Trieste question on EDC ratification is exaggerated. Nevertheless, Italian ratification of EDC appears to depend on the government's ability to defend its position in Parliament regarding Trieste.
