14 JULY 1960

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC

East German officials concerned over growing peasant movement to leave collectivized farms.

Chinese Communists, in strong protest note to Indonesia, demand "open apology" for killings of two Overseas Chinese.

II. ASIA-AFRICA

Congo situation becoming more critical; food shortage in Leopoldville could lead to civilian rioting.

India--Government workers' response to strike call spotty, but violence and sabotage increasing; unions reportedly plan long siege.

Somali Republic--Naming of premier-designate apparently has ended political stalemate.
