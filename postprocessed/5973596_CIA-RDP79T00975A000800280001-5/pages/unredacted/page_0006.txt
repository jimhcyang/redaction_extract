unconfirmed Tudeh plans for activity within the army. Tudeh members were instructed to join the armed forces and to establish good records. At the same time they were to create friction between civilians and the military.

Comment: The Tudeh has used nationalist attacks on the army to further its own efforts to weaken the latter. The government, alarmed by a series of minor Tudeh disturbances, reimposed martial law in Tehran on 20 August following a Tudeh-nationalist clash. Fifty Tudeh members have reportedly been arrested. a Tudeh congress is scheduled to meet in Tehran soon.
