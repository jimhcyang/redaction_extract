3. PHILIPPINES SEEKING FURTHER DATA TO BOLSTER
POSITION ON AMERICAN BASES

The Philippine Foreign Ministry requested up-to-date reports on the status of United States armed forces or bases in foreign countries. Specific information on arrangements regarding criminal jurisdiction, tax exemptions, and settlement of civilian claims was requested for the use of the Philippine panel in the current negotiations on American bases.
