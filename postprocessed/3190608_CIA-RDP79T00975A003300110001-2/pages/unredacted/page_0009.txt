6. SOVIET POLICY ON PRODUCTION OF MODERN WEAPONS

The USSR's progress in
weapons technology is so rapid--especially
in the missile field--that some new weapons may not be placed
in mass production. "What is new today," he said, "is old to-
morrow."

Only those heavy bombers which are
"absolutely necessary" have been produced.

Soviet bomber output will give way entirely to missiles
which will be "more economical and reliable."
