Ex-premier and Socialist leader Guy
Mollet has been regarded as a possible replacement for
Bourges-Maunoury since the time the latter took office
less than four months ago. Another prospective candidate
is ex-Gaullist and former governor of Algeria Jacques
Soustelle who played a major role in rallying rightist op-
position to the Algerian statute. Ex-premier René Pleven
and Education Minister René Billères, both representa-
tives of center parties which might become the nucleus
of a compromise coalition, have also been mentioned.
