CENTRAL INTELLIGENCE BULLETIN
20 December 1960

DAILY BRIEF

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC

*Berlin: In an effort to prod Bonn into reinstating the interzonal trade agreement which otherwise will lapse on 31 December, the East German mass media threaten "serious disorders."

Soviet Ambassador Per-
vukhin told Ambassador Dowling on 16 December that Berlin access problems could always be settled by Soviet-US bilateral talks, suggesting that Moscow is not interested in a showdown at this point
