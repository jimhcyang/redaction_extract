5. CHINESE COMMUNIST CIVIL AIR SERVICE
SCHEDULED TO RANGOON BY APRIL

Peiping has formally notified Burma of its intention to begin scheduled civil air service from Kunming to Rangoon in April.

Comment
A Sino-Burmese civil air agreement was signed last November.

In view of China's greater resources in trained personnel and equipment, it will have at least a temporary monopoly on this line. The establishment of this route places Communist China in a favorable position to extend air service in both South and Southeast Asia, particularly to India, with which the Chinese are trying to conclude an air agreement. (Prepared by ORR)
