8. TUNISIAN UPRISING REPORTED SCHEDULED
FOR MID-MARCH

Nationalists will attack the French in Tunisia in about a month.

The Tunisians now have enough arms to equip 10,000 men. These arms have been supplied by Egypt, mainly by desert caravan.

The Tunisians hope the unified command now being formed for nationalist forces in all of French North Africa can direct uprisings in Morocco and Algeria, simultaneously with the one in Tunisia. They doubt, however, that this could be done within a month.

Comment
Tunisian extremist Salah ben Youssef, who is believed to be in Tripoli at present, probably is attempting to foment disorders in Tunisia prior to the general elections scheduled for 25 March. A Tunisian revolt on such a large scale, however, is not likely at this time.
