4. YUGOSLAVS PLEDGE CONTINUED CO-OPERATION WITH GREECE

The Yugoslav government has informed Athens that if Greek-Turkish co-operation should cease, the Yugoslavs would strengthen their co-operation with Greece but would not take comparable action with Turkey, according to the Greek chief of staff, General Dovas.
