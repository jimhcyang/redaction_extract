11 AUGUST 1960

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC

1. Plans being made for another meeting of all Communist parties in effort to resolve Moscow-Peiping differences.

2. Comment on Khrushchev's hint he may participate in UN disarmament debate.

II. ASIA-AFRICA

3. Pakistan agrees to discuss Moscow's offer of oil exploration assistance.

4. UAR stepping up drive to expand its contacts and influence throughout Africa.

LATE ITEMS

5. Comment on Congo situation.

6. Comment on situation in Laos.
