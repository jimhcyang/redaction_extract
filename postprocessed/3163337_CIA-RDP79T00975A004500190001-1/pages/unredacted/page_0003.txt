2 JUNE 1959

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC
USSR seeks to use West German Socialists to discredit Bonn's position at Geneva.

II. ASIA-AFRICA
Sudan--Leaders of abortive 22 May coup arrested.
Kuwait agrees to weapons stockpile for emergency use by British troops.
Jordanian attache offers to work with UAR against Amman government.

III. THE WEST
Nicaragua--Unity of opposition elements prompts Somoza to declare state of siege.
