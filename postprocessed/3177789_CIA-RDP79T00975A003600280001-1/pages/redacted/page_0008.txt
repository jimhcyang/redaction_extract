CENTRAL INTELLIGENCE BULLETIN
2 April 1958

DAILY BRIEF

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC

* USSR Council of Ministers: Two men--First Deputy Premiers Mikoyan and Frol Kozlov--will probably be delegated most of the day-to-day responsibilities in the new Khrushchev government. Mikoyan will continue to provide much of the behind-the-scenes brain work for Khrushchev. Kozlov, former Leningrad party boss and more recently premier of the Russian Republic (RSFSR), is perhaps being groomed by Khrushchev as the next Soviet premier.
