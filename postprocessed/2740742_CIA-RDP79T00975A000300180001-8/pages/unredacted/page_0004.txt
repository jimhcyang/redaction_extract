Increased capabilities for offensive action are supported by other indications: the increased volume of enemy artillery fire on several parts of the front in recent days; air sightings of some enemy armor moving toward the front; and the eastward displacement of the 12th Army and the relief of the front line 20th by the rehabilitated 27th Army, suggesting the massing of forces for a drive in the east.

Comment: The present situation suggests that the enemy has the capability for launching a new offensive without committing fresh formations, a measure that has characterized the pre-offensive pattern of several previous operations.
