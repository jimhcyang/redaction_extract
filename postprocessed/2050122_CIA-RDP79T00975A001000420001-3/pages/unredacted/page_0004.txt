WESTERN EUROPE
4. Early West German action on Bonn and Paris treaties seen possible:
High West German officials indicate that Chancellor Adenauer, in the event of a Constitutional Court refusal to accept the government's petition for an opinion on the legality of the Bonn and Paris treaties, is determined to press for final Bundestag approval of the treaties between 10 and 14 March. The court's attitude is expected to be known by 25 February.
