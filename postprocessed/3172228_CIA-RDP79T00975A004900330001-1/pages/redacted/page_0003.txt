10 FEBRUARY 1960

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC
Khrushchev's foreign travel plans mark intensification of his "personal diplomacy". 1

II. ASIA-AFRICA
India Government negotiating with British firms for purchase of ground-to-air guided missiles and long-range radar. 2
Indonesia--Growing differences reported between President Sukarno and Army Chief of Staff Nasution. 3
Burma--U Nu states he will continue Ne Win government's domestic and foreign policies. 4
Iraqi Communist party "orthodox" faction suffers setback in effort to achieve legal status; splinter faction licensed. 5

III. THE WEST
Cypriot independence indefinitely postponed following stalemate in negotiations over size of British bases. 6
