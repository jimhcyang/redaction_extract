Laos: The Souvanna Phouma cabinet decided on 12 October to move the government to Luang Prabang, reportedly within the next ten days. The cabinet also reportedly determined that Laos would seek a loan from the USSR if the United States did not give aid for the armed forces and the police. Soviet Ambassador-designate Abramov arrived in Vientiane on 13 October as scheduled.
