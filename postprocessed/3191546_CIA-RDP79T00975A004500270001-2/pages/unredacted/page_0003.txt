11 JUNE 1959

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC
Geneva--Gromyko makes new Berlin proposal.
Soviet vessel in Gulf of Aden believed carrying Chinese Communist arms for Algerian rebels.

II. ASIA-AFRICA
Turkish foreign minister states that any East-West modus vivendi must keep USSR out of the Middle East.
UAR planning to introduce unified currency in Egyptian and Syrian regions.
Decline in Pakistani exports threatens government efforts to improve economy.
Indian food-grain crop sets new record.
