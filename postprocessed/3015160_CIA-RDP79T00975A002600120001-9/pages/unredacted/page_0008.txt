3. AIR SUPPLY OF ALGERIAN REBELS

Comment on:

Egypt is pressing preparations for the early establishment of an air supply system to the Algerian rebels.

Cairo has been informed that the operation is considered feasible, presumably by Libyan and Algerian accomplices

the "pilots and Algerians to be trained in air-dropping should be sent to Egypt."

Libyan premier Ben Halim had refused an Algerian nationalist request for landing rights to refuel two Cairo-based planes from which arms and ammunition were to be dropped by parachute over "Algeria and French West Africa."
