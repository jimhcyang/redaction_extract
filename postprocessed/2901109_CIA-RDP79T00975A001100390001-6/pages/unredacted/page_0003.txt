GENERAL

1. Satellites considering centralization of Western ship chartering:
Comment: The apparent objective of this plan is the more efficient utilization of cargo space on chartered Western vessels, which are used primarily to carry nonstrategic goods to China and Chinese exports to the Satellites. This would make available more space on Soviet bloc ships for transporting strategic commodities.

The volume of trade between the Satellites and China has been increasing rapidly, causing serious congestion in the Polish port of Gdynia, the major Satellite port for China trade.

2. Orbit attempts to establish Greece as transshipment point:
Comment: The attempt to establish Greece as a transshipment point is apparently a reaction to negotiations by Western nations regarding transshipment controls for such major Western European free ports as Antwerp and Rotterdam.
