Gaza Strip - Jordan

The bid by the Gaza Legislative Council for inclusion in the United Arab Republic may presage increased UAR pressure on the pro-Western regime in Jordan, home of nearly half a million refugees from Arab Palestine.

It is therefore possible that the Jordanian exiles have reached agreement on a cooperative effort to undermine King Husayn. On 27 March, exiled Jordanian Baath party leader Rimawi left Damascus for Cairo for a meeting with Nasir on a "very serious matter."
