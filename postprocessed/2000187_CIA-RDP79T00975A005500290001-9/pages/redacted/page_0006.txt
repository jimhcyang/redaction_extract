Mrs. Bandaranaike plans to visit the USSR in March after the Commonwealth prime ministers' conference in London--her first official trip abroad and the first visit to Moscow by a Ceylonese head of Government.

*Portugal: Some of Premier Salazar's stanchest supporters are reported to be shocked by his inability to cope with the situation produced by the seizure of the Santa Maria and to have stated that he must be replaced at some time in the "not too distant future." No precipitate action is likely, but the various factions within the regime will probably make an early attempt to reach agreement among themselves regarding a successor.
