SOUTHEAST ASIA

4. Bao Dai reportedly sets price for his support of Diem:

Bao Dai has sent word by Premier Diem's brother, who arrived in Hanoi on 28 October, that he is prepared to instruct Generals Hinh, Xuan, and Viet to support the premier in return for certain considerations. The considerations are that Bao Dai not be forced to abdicate, and that he be provided with approximately $1,400,000 and control of the crown domains.

Bao Dai transferred authority over the crown domains to Diem when the latter became premier. The Binh Xuyen has interests in the rich Annam sector of these lands and may have persuaded Bao Dai to ask for their return.
