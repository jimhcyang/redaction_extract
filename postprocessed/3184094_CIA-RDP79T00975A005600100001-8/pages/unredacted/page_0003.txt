11 March 1961
CENTRAL INTELLIGENCE BULLETIN

CONTENTS

3. USSR: Khrushchev plays down urgency of nuclear test ban. (Page ii)

4. USSR: Moscow continuing to purchase oil tankers outside bloc. (Page iii)

5. Vietnam: Hanoi orders Communists in South Vietnam to disrupt presidential election to be held 9 April. (Page iii)

6. East Germany: Economic delegation to Moscow receives little satisfaction in efforts to reduce dependence on imports from West Germany. (Page iii)

7. West Germany: Bonn officials fear emphasis in US foreign policy is shifting away from Europe. (Page iv)

8. Ethiopia: Disorders in Addis Ababa may occur upon execution of leader of revolt.. (Page iv)
