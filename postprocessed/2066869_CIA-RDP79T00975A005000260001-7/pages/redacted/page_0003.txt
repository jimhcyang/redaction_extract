30 MARCH 1960

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC
Projects arranged under Soviet $100 million line of credit for Ethiopia soon giving USSR a "real foothold." 1

II. ASIA-AFRICA
Differences between Nasir, Qasim, and King Husayn likely to be intensified by Arab League meeting on Palestine on 31 March. 2
Morocco-- King pressing France and Spain to agree to evacuate their forces by end of 1963. 3
South Vietnam-- Large-scale Communist guerrilla attacks continuing in southern area. 4

III. THE WEST
Latin American Communists stepping up their Havana-based anti-US campaign. 5
