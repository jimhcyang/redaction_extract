24 MARCH 1958

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC

USSR may be preparing to announce
suspension of nuclear tests

Another Soviet nuclear test noted
near Semipalatinsk

II. ASIA-AFRICA

Indonesia - Military situation in North Sumatra remains fluid, but
central government appears to
have upper hand

Lebanese President's effort to run
for second term may lead to civil

United Arab Republic - Bizri re-
signs as commander of UAR First Army in Syria

Chinese Nationalists may take
further action against Japan in
connection with Tokyo's private
trade agreement with Peiping.

Laos - Communists may make
good showing in 4 May assembly
elections.

III. THE WEST

France - Communists may be
seeking understanding with De
Gaulle.

Haiti - Attempt to oust Duvalier
government may occur shortly.

LATE ITEM

Saudi Arabia - King, as a result
of family pressure, yields "full powers" to Crown Prince Faysal
in domestic, foreign, and finan-
cial policies.
