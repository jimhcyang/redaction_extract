LATE ITEM

Saudi Arabia: Mecca radio's announcement on 22
March that King Saud had granted "full powers" to Crown
Prince Faysal in formulating and executing domestic, for-
eign, and financial policies of the Saudi Arabian Govern-
ment indicates that the King has yielded to family pres-
sure to relinquish his highly centralized authority. Exer-
cise of greater influence by Faysal would probably result
in a relaxation of tensions with the United Arab Republic and
in a more critical approach to relations with the United States.
