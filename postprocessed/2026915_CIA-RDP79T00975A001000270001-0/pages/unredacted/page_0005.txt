4. Indonesia orders bunkering of Polish vessel:
Ambassador Cochran in Djakarta reports that the Indonesian Minister of Economy has ordered the Shell Company to provide the Polish vessel Pulaski with sufficient fuel to reach Hong Kong. The company has agreed to comply.

Comment: The British Government had ordered the Shell Company to refuse the ship's request for fuel because of a belief that it was carrying strategic goods.
This action by the Indonesian Government is a further demonstration of its increasing reluctance to cooperate with Western-sponsored measures against the Soviet Orbit.
