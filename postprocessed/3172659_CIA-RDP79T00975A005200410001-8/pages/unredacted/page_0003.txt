17 AUGUST 1960

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC
North Korean federation proposal is move to take propaganda initiative from Seoul.
Chinese Communist delegation offends Japanese; may hinder Japanese overtures to Peiping.

II. ASIA-AFRICA
Congo--Hammarskjold to seek Security Council backing in dispute with Lumumba.
Jordan--King Husayn believes prospects fair for anti-Nasir coup in Syria; now more inclined to aid plotters.
Iraq--New arrest of conspirators is further evidence of erosion of Qasim's position.

LATE ITEM
The situation in Laos.
South Korean Assembly rejects Kim To-yun for prime minister.
