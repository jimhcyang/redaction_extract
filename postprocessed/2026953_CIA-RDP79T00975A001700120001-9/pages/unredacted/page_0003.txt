FAR EAST

1. Large numbers of Communist landing craft apparently moved from Shanghai:

Comment: The apparent departure of landing craft from their normal concentration area may presage renewed Communist attacks on the Tachens.

Early last May large numbers of Communist landing craft were reported at Shanghai. Shortly thereafter, this concentration was no longer observed, and on 18 May the Communists touched off the current offshore island campaign by taking six unoccupied islands to the north of the Tachen group.

Recent reports have told of a concentration of Communist vessels in the Choushan Islands, midway between Shanghai and the Tachens, from which a major assault on the Tachens might well be staged.

At present, however, a typhoon is reported sweeping toward northern Formosa, and is expected to hit the Tachens within the next 48 hours. The Tachens, therefore, may be safe from any attack planned for the next few days.
