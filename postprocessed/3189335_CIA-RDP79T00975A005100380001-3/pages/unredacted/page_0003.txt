14 JUNE 1960

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC
Burma reportedly to accept Czech credit
of 10,500,000 for industrial equipment. (1)

II. ASIA-AFRICA
Japan--Government may recess Diet
and delay treaty ratification until after
President's visit. (2)

Cambodia and Communist China have
military agreement ready for signature,
according to a British report. (3)

Sino-Indian border problem continues to
give serious concern to New Delhi offi-
cials. (4)

New Congolese government will face
critical financial situation. (5)
