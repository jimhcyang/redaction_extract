5. CROWN PRINCE OF YEMEN TO VISIT SOVIET UNION

Comment on:

The visit of Crown Prince Badr of Yemen to Moscow on 20 May will be the first state visit of a high-ranking Arab to the Soviet Union. The trip is not only symbolic of increasing Soviet influence in the Arab states, but reflects the Soviet effort to disrupt British and American interests in the Arabian Peninsula.

Yemen and the USSR signed a treaty of friendship in October 1955. This spring Soviet and Czech economic missions visited Yemen and a trade agreement with the USSR was signed. A similar pact with Czechoslovakia is expected to be concluded shortly. These contacts have been encouraged by Egypt to undermine the British position in the Aden Protectorate.
