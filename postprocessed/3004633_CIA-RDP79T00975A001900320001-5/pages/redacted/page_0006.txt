7. Saudi bitterness over Iraqi-Turkish pact imperils Dhahran air base agreement:

Ambassador Wadsworth in Jidda believes that Saudi Arabia's bitterness over the conclusion of the Iraqi-Turkish defense pact endangers future Saudi-American relations. He fears that King Saud will soon raise the question of the American air base at Dhahran.
