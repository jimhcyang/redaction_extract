Dertinger, a member of the East Zone Christian Democratic Union, has exercised little influence on the conduct of affairs in the Foreign Ministry. His Communist deputy has been the real authority, and his own activities have been subjected to constant surveillance.

The defection last year of Dertinger's press secretary, Gerold Rummler, may prove to be the primary basis of the charges now levelled against him, and his arrest will provide ample reason for the elimination of any other officials who may be considered part of his entourage.
