It appears unlikely that the Poles would receive ships of the postwar construction program until the Soviet navy has been built up to its intended strength.  

Since early January a large force of Soviet Baltic Fleet warships has been on exercises in the southern Baltic area. It appears possible that the exercises have been held in this area partly to co-ordinate Soviet and Polish training in anticipation of an expansion of the Polish navy.  

WESTERN EUROPE  

France critical of American instructions for Afro-Asian conference:  

Comment: France has consistently opposed discussion of its North African policy in any international forum. In view of the number of Moslem states critical of French policy which are expected to attend the 18-25 April conference at Bandung, Paris probably anticipates strong attacks there on its policies in North Africa.
