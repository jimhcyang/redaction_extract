5. IMPACT OF NEW SOVIET POLICY STATEMENTS ON
INDONESIAN ELITE

Ambassador Cumming in Djakarta is
concerned over the potential impact
of recent Soviet statements on the cir-
cles which form opinion and policy in
Indonesia. He cites as illustrative of a trend a conversa-
tion with an intelligent, widely traveled and anti-Commu-
nist Socialist official in which the latter stressed the im-
portance of the olive branch the Soviets had extended to
Socialists throughout the world.

Comment
The relatively poor showing of the anti-
Communist parties in the Indonesian
elections last fall has in itself brought a leftward re-orienta-
tion in Indonesian political thinking.
