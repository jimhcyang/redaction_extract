British prime minister Eden has asked Jordan to relieve all remaining British Arab Legion officers of command responsibility, but Amman has asked London to reconsider its decision to recall some 15 officers who are not under contract to Jordan. London has informed the 50 British officers under contract to the Jordan government that no requests for transfer will be accepted while their future status is being considered.
