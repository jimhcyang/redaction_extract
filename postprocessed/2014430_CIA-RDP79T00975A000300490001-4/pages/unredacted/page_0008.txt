LATIN AMERICA

9. Comment on Argentine electoral candidates, President and Senora de Peron:

At a giant labor rally, President Peron and his wife, Evita, agreed to become the Peronista candidates for president and vice-president in the 11 November 1951 elections.

This first firm declaration of Evita's electoral intentions will accelerate political maneuvering to dislodge the Perons.
