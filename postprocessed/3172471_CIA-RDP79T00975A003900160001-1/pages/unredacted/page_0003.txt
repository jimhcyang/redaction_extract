20 AUGUST 1958

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC
Cairo announces 25 Soviet ships carrying industrial equipment due to arrive soon.
Signing of formal agreement on test detection will probably set off new Soviet propaganda campaign for West to halt tests.

II. ASIA-AFRICA
Sudan - Popular trend toward closer alignment with radical Arab nationalism increases pressure on Khalil government to accommodate.

III. THE WEST
Venezuela under pressure from labor elements to modify present 50-50 profit-sharing formula in petroleum industry.
