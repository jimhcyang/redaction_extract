2. Indonesian leaders admit elections will be delayed:

President Sukarno, Vice President Hatta and Prime Minister Ali have each admitted separately to the American ambassador that elections cannot be held in Indonesia by July or August of this year as had been hoped. Ali said "perhaps" elections could be held by or during October.
