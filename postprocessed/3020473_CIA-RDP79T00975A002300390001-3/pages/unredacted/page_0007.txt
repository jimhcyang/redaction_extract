4. IRAQI PREMIER REPORTEDLY THREATENS TO QUIT BAGHDAD PACT

Comment Nuri's reported threat would be in
line with his increasing concern over
Iraq's position as the sole Arab member of the pact. The
subsequent British failure to induce Jordan to join the pact
has probably increased this concern and underscored the
fact that there are no other likely Arab prospects.

Iraq's isolation, plus its failure to ob-
tain the benefits anticipated from joining the pact, may
cause it to withdraw from active participation in "north-
ern tier" defense.
