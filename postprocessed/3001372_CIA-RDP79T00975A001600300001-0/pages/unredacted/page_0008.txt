7. Saudi king decrees termination of Point IV program:

King Saud has signed a decree terminating the Point IV program in Saudi Arabia, according to Ambassador Wadsworth.

Wadsworth was informed on 27 June by Deputy Foreign Minister Yusuf Yassin that termination would become official with written notification from the Foreign Ministry.

Comment: Wadsworth was first told on 1 June of the Saudi government's desire to cancel Point IV--a move in line with its increasingly anti-American policy.
