26 JANUARY 1960

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC
Peiping issues inflated production claims in communiqué on 1959 economic results. (1)

II. ASIA-AFRICA
Algeria--De Gaulle pledges restoration of order in Algiers; confronts local authorities with need for decision to use force against insurgent Frenchmen. (2)

Tunisia: President Bourguiba calls for French evacuation of Bizerte base; French position reportedly not negotiable. (3)

Indonesia--Rapid inflation continuing; may lead to unrest. (4)

III. THE WEST
Cuba--National Bank head "Che" Guevara forbids rediscounting of crop loans by foreign banks; could be prelude to nationalization of the banking system. (5)
