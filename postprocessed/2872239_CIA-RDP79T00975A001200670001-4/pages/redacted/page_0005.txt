6. French postpone fall offensive plans in Indochina:

The 1 September progress report on the Navarre plan from the chief of the Military Assistance Advisory Group in Saigon states that the French are now planning to counterattack in the event of a Viet Minh attack on the delta in late September or early October. If the Viet Minh does not attack in the delta, the French will undertake a diversionary action against enemy forces north of that area.

Comment: This planning differs from the original Navarre program, which called for an "offensive to forestall the enemy attack".
