28 DECEMBER 1959

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC

II. ASIA-AFRICA

Indonesia gives Chinese Communist letter cool reception; Djakarta maybe planning to close Bank of China branches in Indonesia.

India--Prospects appear good for anti-Communist parties in February election in Kerala State.

Iraq plans military exercises with live ammunition in two areas along Iranian border; increase in tension expected.

III. THE WEST

Brazilian Government planning action against leftist agitation; Ambassador Cabot notes popular unrest, sees possibility of leftist uprising or coup attempt.

LATE ITEM

Laos--Political maneuvering continues.
