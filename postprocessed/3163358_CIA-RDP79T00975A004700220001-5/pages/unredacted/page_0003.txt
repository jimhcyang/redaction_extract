25 SEPTEMBER 1959

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC
1. Peiping's domestic propaganda reflects opposition to Moscow's approach to the US.
2. Communist bloc continues efforts to establish closer ties with Guinea.

II. ASIA-AFRICA
3. Laos--French military attache in Vientiane tells Argentine member of UN subcommittee there is no foreign military intervention.
4. Indonesian Foreign Minister to visit Peiping to discuss Djakarta's restrictions on Chinese merchants.
5. India--Krishna Menon may lose post of Defense Minister.
6. Japanese Prime Minister threatens to dissolve Diet over disagreement on revision of US-Japan Security Treaty.
7. Yemeni Crown Prince may go to West Germany for reasons of "health."
8. Moroccan Crown Prince attempting to suppress left-wing elements.
