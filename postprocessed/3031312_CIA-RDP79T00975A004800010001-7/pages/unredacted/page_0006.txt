Bolivia: The situation in Bolivia is tense and likely to remain so in the period before the presidential nominating convention of the government party which is scheduled to open on 10 December. Civilian militia groups are being mobilized in support of the conflicting presidential ambitions of both former President Paz--whom moderate President Siles is apparently now supporting--and right-wing leader Walter Guevara. The police and the army are said to have been alerted to quell disturbances, but they probably could not control a widespread outbreak on the part of the militia.

LATE ITEM

*USSR: A launching occurred at Tyura Tam at about 1624 EST on 1 November. It probably involved a test ICBM.
The missile reached the usual ICBM impact area on the Kamchatka Peninsula. The countdown was marked by delays of over four hours, and was preceded by apparently unsuccessful attempts to launch on 30 and 31 October.
The Soviet instrumentation ships on 31 October were apparently en route to Petropovlovsk on Kamchatka and could have been in port during this operation. There is no available information that the ships participated in the event.
