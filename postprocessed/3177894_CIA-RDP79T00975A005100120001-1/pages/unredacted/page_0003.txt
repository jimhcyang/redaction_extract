14 MAY 1960

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC

II. ASIA-AFRICA
UAR--Nasir claims USSR ready to deliver MIG-19 jet fighter aircraft immediately.
Communist capability to disrupt internal security in Laos increasing; widespread guerrilla action could be initiated at any time.
Cambodian leader's international outlook altered by Chou En-lai's public pledges of support; may be more inflexible in disputes with neighbors.
South Korean National Assembly moves to vote new constitutional amendment on governmental changes in about 30 days.
Indian Communist party in recent meeting fails to resolve controversy over party policy.

III. THE WEST
Cuban allegations of US violation of territorial waters may lead to further anti-US demonstrations.

LATE ITEM
Comment on workers' protest meetings being held throughout USSR.
