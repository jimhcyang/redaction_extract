Brazil: President Quadros is apparently seeking a meeting with President Kennedy outside the United States. At his meeting with President Frondizi on 21-22 April it was suggested that the approaching conference of the Inter-American Economic and Social Council, which is to discuss the Alliance for Progress, be held in Uruguay and that President Kennedy be invited to attend. Brazilian Foreign Minister Arinos indicated to Ambassador Cabot on 26 April that a meeting between the US and Brazilian Presidents might prove helpful on the Cuban question
