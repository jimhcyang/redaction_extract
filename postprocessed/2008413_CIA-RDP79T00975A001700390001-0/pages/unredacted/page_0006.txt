5. Syrian army will stage coup:

Comment: This threat was made before the elections on 24 September, in which a Quwatli victory seemed assured. Preliminary results of the election show, however, that a bloc of independents led by Khalid al Azm is running ahead of Quwatli’s Nationalist party. Al Azm and the independents would probably be acceptable to the army but they seem unlikely to win a clearcut majority. The army may therefore intervene in their behalf in the final stages of the voting. The balloting is due to be completed on 4 October.
