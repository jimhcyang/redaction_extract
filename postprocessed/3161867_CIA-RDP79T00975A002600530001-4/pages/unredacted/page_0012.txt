8. CONTRACT SIGNED FOR FIRST SOVIET PROJECTS IN SOUTHERN AFGHANISTAN

Radio Moscow has announced the signature on 26 July of contracts for Soviet surveys of a canal near Jalalabad, east of Kabul, and two dams and a reservoir on the Paltu (or Nahari) River, near the tribal territory east of the Kabul-Kandahar road below Ghazni.
