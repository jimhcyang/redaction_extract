21 DECEMBER 1959

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC
1. Four Soviet submarines from Baltic reach Albanian base.

II. ASIA-AFRICA
2. Khrushchev rejects Iranian offer to ban foreign ballistic missile bases as insufficient for "normalizing" relations.
3. Turks arrest "key members" of Kurdish underground organization.

III. THE WEST
4. Adenauer feels West Germany must have close ties with France against the day when US withdraws military forces from Europe.
5. French to be ready for nuclear test by March 1960.
6. Castro regime reported planning to instigate mass demonstrations of loyalty by spreading false rumors of counterrevolution.
