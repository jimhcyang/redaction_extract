Cyprus: Archbishop Makarios and General Grivas have concluded their three-day talks on the island of Rhodes with a communiqué consisting largely of vague generalities which soft-pedaled their differences. In promoting the talks, Grivas saw the chance to enhance his political prestige in Greece. Makarios probably has been able to avoid any commitments to Grivas which would seriously limit his freedom of action. However, issues which separate the two men apparently persist.
