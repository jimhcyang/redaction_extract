USSR

1. American Mission Chiefs from eastern Europe comment on Orbit public opinion:

At the recent meeting of US Missions Chiefs from eastern Europe it was agreed that within the Soviet Union the Russians were having some success in their "Hate America" campaign and in creating impressions that the United States is driving the world into another war. In the Satellites, however, the majority of the people are still pro-American and hope for an early liberation, even if a war is necessary.
