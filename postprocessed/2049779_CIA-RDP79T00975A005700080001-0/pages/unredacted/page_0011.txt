President Chiari has been concerned over evidence that revolutionary groups anxious to overthrow him are closely allied with pro-Castro elements and are probably receiving money via the Cuban Embassy in Panama. He doubts the ability of the National Guard, Panama's only armed force, to repulse strong opposition. Discontent arising from economic stagnation and unemployment provides fertile ground for political agitation in Panama. (Backup, Page 5)
