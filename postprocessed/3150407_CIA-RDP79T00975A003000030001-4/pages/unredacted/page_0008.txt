5. THAI UNDERGROUND CADRES RETURNING FROM TRAINING IN COMMUNIST CHINA

Thai cadres who have been undergoing training in Communist China have completed their course and are prepared to return to Thailand by a clandestine route through northern Laos.

Communist authorities in Laos stated that the cadres have reported to the "men responsible for the communications lines between Upper Laos and Thailand," and gave instructions to maintain utmost secrecy concerning their trip.
