Nehru is scheduled to make a statement in Parliament on 2 September regarding the resignations reportedly proferred by the three service chiefs and Krishna Menon. There is little likelihood that Nehru can resolve the situation merely by rotating Menon to another cabinet position. In order to satisfy military and public opinion, he may have to dismiss Menon from the cabinet, while giving him some other important post.
