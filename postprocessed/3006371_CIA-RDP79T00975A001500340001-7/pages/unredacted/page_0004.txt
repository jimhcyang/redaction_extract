SOVIET UNION

3. IL-28's and MIG-15's depart Wrangel Island:

two IL-28 jet light bombers and four MIG-15 jet fighters departed Wrangel Island on 18 April.

Comment: This is the first indication of the use of an airfield on Wrangel Island by jet aircraft.

Recent airlift operations and indications of construction activity on Wrangel Island have suggested expanded development of facilities there, possibly including airfield construction or improvement.
