18 OCTOBER 1958

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC
Taiwan Strait - Cease-fire continues; bloc propaganda pointing to "political" means to regain offshore islands and Taiwan.
Moscow "displeased" by Nasir's efforts to supply arms to Iraq.
USSR to deliver more ships to UAR.
Poland - Gomulka moves closer to the Kremlin's ideological line.

II. ASIA-AFRICA
Thailand - Sarit returning to Bangkok to deal with discord among followers.
Lebanon - Current mood of political reconciliation unlikely to continue long.
Nasir's land reform program encountering resistance in Syria.

III. THE WEST
France - Early cease-fire expected in Algeria.
De Gaulle to postpone recognition of Guinea for several months.
Military coup may be attempted in Venezuela on 19 October.
