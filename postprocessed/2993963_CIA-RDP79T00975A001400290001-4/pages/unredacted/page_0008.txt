10. French and German leaders reportedly fear US may adopt peripheral defense strategy:

The American embassy in Paris reports that Premier Laniel and Foreign Minister Bidault are much concerned over the possible withdrawal of American and British forces from the Continent. Laniel intends to take up the matter with the United States immediately after the Berlin conference.

Comment: Fears of a peripheral strategy leading to a withdrawal of American forces from Western Europe were revived following Secretary Dulles' warning in December of a possible policy reappraisal.

11. Paris apparently preparing public for dispatch of more troops to Indochina:

According to the American embassy in Paris, Jacques Fauvet's article on 18 January in Le Monde on French policy in Indochina was encouraged by government sources to prepare public opinion for the news that more men must be sent to that area. The article stated that Defense Minister Pleven has decided to reinforce the French air force there.
