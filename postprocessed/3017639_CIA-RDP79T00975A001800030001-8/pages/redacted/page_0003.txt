GENERAL

1. Soviet official proposes exchange of naval visits with Norway:

The Soviet military attaché in Oslo has proposed to Norwegian defense minister Knudsen that Soviet and Norwegian naval vessels exchange visits to Oslo and Leningrad. According to the Norwegian NATO representative, Knudsen has not yet replied and wants to know if other NATO powers have been similarly approached.

Comment: In response to a Soviet request, Swedish and Soviet naval vessels exchanged visits this summer for the first time since 1925. An exchange with Norway would be the first with a NATO power.

The USSR has made various efforts since early 1954 to improve relations with all the Scandinavian powers and was presumably encouraged in this move by the favorable reception Soviet ships received in Stockholm in July.
