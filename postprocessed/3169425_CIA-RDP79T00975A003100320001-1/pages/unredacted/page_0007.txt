4. SYRIA NEGOTIATING COMPREHENSIVE ARMS RE-EQUIPMENT CONTRACT WITH USSR

Comment on:

The USSR may have agreed to provide sufficient military goods to re-equip Syria's armed forces completely, as it did in the case of Egypt last January.

The USSR has agreed to this in principle, and on 23 May the Syrian government was requested by its arms mission in Moscow to furnish for submission to the USSR a "final list" of Syrian army requirements for the remainder of this year. The Syrian negotiators in Moscow reported that they planned to "submit a complete order for all our needs" in the second half of July.
