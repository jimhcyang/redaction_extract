30 SEPTEMBER 1959

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC
Soviet and Chinese Communist leaders concerned that Nehru is drifting into "American camp."

II. ASIA-AFRICA
UAR role in plans for overthrow of Iraqi Prime Minister Qasim.
India--New oil refinery to be built by USSR.
Japanese Government determined to assure voluntary repatriation of Koreans.
Laos--Tunisian member of UN subcommittee recalled.

III. THE WEST
UN--Poland stand good chance of winning seat on Security Council.
