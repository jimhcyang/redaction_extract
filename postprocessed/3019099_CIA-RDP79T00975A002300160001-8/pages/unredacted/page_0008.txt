5. USSR REQUESTS JORDAN TO ESTABLISH DIPLOMATIC RELATIONS

The Soviet Union has asked Jordan to establish diplomatic relations,

Comment

This Soviet overture to Jordan, following similar overtures to Yemen, Saudi Arabia, and Libya, may find a favorable reception unless Jordan can obtain substantial military aid from Britain.

Egypt's arms deal with the Soviet bloc has been widely approved in Jordan. Jordan, feeling itself particularly exposed in the Arab conflict with Israel, also feels a need to increase its arms.
