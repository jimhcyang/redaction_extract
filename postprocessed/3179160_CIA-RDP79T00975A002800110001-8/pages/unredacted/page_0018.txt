BIWEEKLY SUMMARY
(18 October-31 October 1956)

THE TAIWAN STRAIT

Report of the IAC Current Intelligence Group
for the Taiwan Strait Problem

1. There were no significant combat operations in the area during the period.

2. The Chinese Communists have announced that tracklaying on the trans-Fukien railroad has been completed as far as Changping, about 183 track kilometers from Amoy. Completion of tracklaying by the end of 1956 or shortly thereafter seems likely, but the railway will probably not be in full operation until early next year.
