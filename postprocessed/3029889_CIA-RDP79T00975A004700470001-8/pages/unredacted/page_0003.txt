24 OCTOBER 1959

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC
Recent East German statements may have increased strain with Moscow on some bloc policy issues.
Mikoyan warns Finland against membership in European free-trade area.

II. ASIA-AFRICA
Iraq alerts military units in north; UAR reports Iraqi reinforcements on Syrian border; UAR, fearing Israeli action, alerts units in Sinai.
New Indian - Chinese Communist border incident will delay any plans to negotiate dispute.

III. THE WEST
Italian President Gronchi likely to accept Soviet invitation to visit Moscow.
Cuba--Castro intensifies attacks on United States in effort to muster popular support.
