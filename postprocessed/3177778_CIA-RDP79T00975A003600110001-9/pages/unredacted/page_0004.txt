CENTRAL INTELLIGENCE BULLETIN
13 March 1958

DAILY BRIEF

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC

*Soviet ICBM/ESV launching attempt: The Guided Missiles Intelligence Committee concluded that the USSR had attempted to launch an ICBM or Earth Satellite Vehicle from its Tyura Tam ballistic missile test range at about 1935 GMT on 12 March, but that upon completion of the countdown an undetermined malfunction occurred, causing an extensive delay. (Page 1)

II. ASIA-AFRICA

*Indonesia (as of 0100 EST 13 March): Indonesian government forces which captured the Central Sumatran oil center of Pakanbaru are moving westward along the highway leading to the dissident strongholds of Bukittinggi and Padang. They are meeting some resistance from small dissident units which have managed to regroup several miles outside Pakanbaru. (Page 2) (Map)
