CENTRAL INTELLIGENCE BULLETIN
13 March 1958
DAILY BRIEF

II. ASIA-AFRICA

*Indonesia (as of 0100 EST 13 March): Indonesian government forces which captured the Central Sumatran oil center of Pakanbaru are moving westward along the highway leading to the dissident strongholds of Bukittinggi and Padang. They are meeting some resistance from small dissident units which have managed to regroup several miles outside Pakanbaru.
