Comment: This Chinese Communist operation, involving an estimated 5,000 to 6,000 men, has been minimized as purely local military aid to hard-pressed Viet Minh units. A Chinese invasion in force is still not indicated.

This incursion, however, like previous ones, is indicative of China's apparent willingness to disregard the integrity of the Indochina border, at least in areas where regular French-Vietnamese troops are not present.
