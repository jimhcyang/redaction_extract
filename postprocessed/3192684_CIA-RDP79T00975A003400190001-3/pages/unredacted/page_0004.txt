1. PEIPING MAY JOIN WARSAW PACT AND GET MODERN ARMS FROM USSR

Chinese participation in the Warsaw pact might be part of the bloc's answer to current Western moves to strengthen NATO. A "strengthened" Warsaw pact was one of the objectives enumerated by Communist leaders on 22 November. Peiping's membership might presage the stationing of specialized Soviet units in China while Chinese units are acquiring proficiency in modern weapons.
