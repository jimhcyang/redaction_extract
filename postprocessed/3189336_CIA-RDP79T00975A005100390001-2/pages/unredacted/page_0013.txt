The officials plan to follow up their initial unsuccessful protest to Saud, but are anxious to avoid any move which might make it appear that Aramco is taking sides and are apprehensive that continuation of the royal quarrel might result in new, unrealistic Saudi demands on the company.

In any case, the struggle may have the effect of weakening the position of the dynasty and reducing political stability. The way might be opened to organized plotting by antiroyalist groups in the bureaucracy, the merchant class, and the army.
