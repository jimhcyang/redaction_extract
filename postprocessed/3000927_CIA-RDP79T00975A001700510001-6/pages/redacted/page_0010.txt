LATIN AMERICA

10. Castillo Armas supporters in Guatemala reported growing "disillusioned":

Leaders of the Committee of Anti-Communist University Students threatened on 10 August to withdraw their group's support from junta president Castillo Armas, They claim that many known Communists still hold high public office.

Castillo is still a public hero, but his followers reportedly are "rapidly becoming disillusioned" because of his failure to satisfy them on the Communist question.
