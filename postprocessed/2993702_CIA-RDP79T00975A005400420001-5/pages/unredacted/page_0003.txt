19 DECEMBER 1960  

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC  

II. ASIA-AFRICA  

Laos--Sino-Soviet airlift to Kong Le and Pathet Lao forces increased; Hanoi calls for reactivation of ICC in Laos.  

Congo--Pro-Lumumba elements continue quest for bloc and Afro-Asian help.  

Ethiopia--Reprisals expected as elements loyal to Emperor Haile Selassie restore control following attempted coup.
