4. SOVIET OFFER OF ECONOMIC AID TO INDONESIA

The fact that Soviet leaders Bulganin and Khrushchev did not visit Indonesia on their tour of South Asia may reflect disinterest on the part of the present Indonesian government for closer relations with the USSR.

The next Indonesian cabinet, which will probably be formed in April and is expected to include strong elements from the leftist-inclined National Party, may be more interested in Soviet aid offers. (Concurred in by ORR)
