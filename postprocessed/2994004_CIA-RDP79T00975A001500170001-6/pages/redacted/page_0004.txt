SOUTHEAST ASIA

3. French look for new attack on Dien Bien Phu by 5 April:

The American embassy in Saigon reports the French now believe a new major assault on Dien Bien Phu will be launched by 5 April. The nights will be completely dark after 2 April. The main defensive positions are now completely surrounded by enemy trenches and the southern redoubt, Isabelle, is almost encircled.
