4 DECEMBER 1959

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC

USSR--Disorders this fall in Kazakhstan symptomatic of chronic troubles but pose no threat to control.

II. ASIA-AFRICA

Iraq--Demonstrations celebrating Qasim's departure from hospital likely to provoke clashes. Coup plotting continues.

Mali Federation composed of Senegal and Soudan may become independent within French Community next June.

Philippine nationalist youth movement reported planning anti-American rallies in Manila this week end.

III. THE WEST

France may make new proposal regarding employment of French Mediterranean fleet.

LATE ITEM

Rebellious air force officers set up "revolutionary base" in Brazil.
