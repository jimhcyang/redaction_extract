Communist China in the past, however, has found the signing of trade agreements or the opening of trade offices a useful preliminary move in gaining recognition. This procedure was used in the UAR, Morocco, and Cuba. Last year the Chinese Communists closed a trade office in Lebanon--maintained for four years--because Beirut failed to sever relations with Taipei.
