8 APRIL 1959

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC

II. ASIA-AFRICA
Iraq - Military units in Baghdad placed on alert as rumors of impending disturbances persist. UAR intensifies anti-Qasim propaganda and strengthens air defenses near Iraqi border.
Singapore - Britain to extend financial aid to Singapore moderates in attempt to prevent overwhelming victory by Communist-infiltrated party in May elections.

III. THE WEST
Discontent within Adenauer's party apparently precipitated his decision to run for President; Erhard will probably become chancellor.
Greece - Communist-sponsored party gets setback in municipal elections.
Argentina - Discontented military group, disturbed by recent riots, gives Frondizi 30 days to rid government of Communists and corrupt elements.
