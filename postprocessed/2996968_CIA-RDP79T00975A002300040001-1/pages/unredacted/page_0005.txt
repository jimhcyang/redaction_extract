THE ARAB-ISRAELI SITUATION
(Information as of 1700, 16 November)

No significant military activity has been reported. Israel is withdrawing troops in substantial numbers from the Syrian frontier. It is not known where these troops are going. Israeli Foreign Ministry officials continue their efforts to convince the West of the serious dangers in the situation. One such official, speaking in Geneva, avers that recent developments have changed Israel's "sense of isolation to one of strangulation" and that "Israel is left to its limited but forceful wisdom."
