THE FORMOSA STRAITS

Report of the IAC Current Intelligence Group
for the Formosa Straits Problem

This report is based on information received in Washington up to 1600 hours 4 April 1955.

1. The Chinese Nationalists have reported sighting 39 Chinese Communist vessels at sea in the Peiling Peninsula - Min River estuary area on 3 April. The sighting report included six to 11 LST's, sufficient for the initial lift of an assault force of 15,000 to 30,000 troops against islands of the Matsu group.

Although this report is not completely accepted, it is viewed with concern. If true, it would mean that the Chinese Communist navy had, on station opposite the Matsu Islands for the first time, amphibious lift sufficient to launch successfully an attack.
