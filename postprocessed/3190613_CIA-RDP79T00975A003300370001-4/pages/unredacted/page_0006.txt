3. TURKISH-SYRIAN BORDER SITUATION

Reference:

Turkish military maneuvers originally scheduled for 15 October have been postponed until the end of the month, according to the American air attache in Ankara. A Turkish general officer indicated to him that the maneuvers, concentrated in the sensitive area on the Syrian border, now are scheduled to begin on 29 October or 1 November.

These dates would postpone the maneuvers until after the Turkish national elections on 27 October.

15 Oct 57 Current Intelligence Bulletin Page 5
