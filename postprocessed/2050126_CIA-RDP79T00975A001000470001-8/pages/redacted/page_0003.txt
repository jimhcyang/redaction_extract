GENERAL

1. Israel wants specific UN condemnation of Soviet anti-Semitism:

Israel is drafting a UN resolution which is to be a restatement of the basic principles of the UN charter and to conclude with a paragraph on the anti-Semitic character of Soviet persecutions. It is seeking roughly 12 sponsors for this resolution, including the United States, Britain and France.

Comment: The United States has been interested in a UN resolution on Communist denial of basic human rights. Any resolution which specifically refers to anti-Semitism is likely to bring up Zionism, thus beclouding the issue and inviting Arab and Moslem opposition.
