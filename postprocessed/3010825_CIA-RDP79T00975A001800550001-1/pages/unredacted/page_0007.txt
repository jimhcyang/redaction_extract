Comment: This is the first identification of an anti-Nasr group within the army. The group probably reflects strong nationalist and neutralist sentiments rather than Communist sympathies. Reports from Cairo have suggested that Salim's speech of 27 December, which is said to have outraged Nasr, may mark a schism in the council, with Salim championing Arab solidarity in opposition to Nasr's policy of co-operation with the West.
