7 JULY 1960

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC

Congo Republic--Mutiny within Force Publique jeopardizes security in Leopoldville vicinity.

Somali Republic as yet unable to form government because of sectional rivalry and tribal difficulties.

Japanese leftists plan demonstration at US air base (Atsugi) on 10 July.

III. THE WEST

West Berlin coalition government's stability may be shaken by political activity of Mayor Brandt.

West Germany protests Khrushchev's abuse of Austrian hospitality in attacking Adenauer; top Austrian officials regard protest as support of their objections to Chancellor Raab's association with Khrushchev.

Paraguay--Pressures to oust Stroessner dictatorship growing.
