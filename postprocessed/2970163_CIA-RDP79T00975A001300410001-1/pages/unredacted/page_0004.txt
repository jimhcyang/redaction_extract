SECURITY INFORMATION

NEAR EAST - AFRICA

3. Arabs seek punitive action by UN against Israel:

At its 23 October meeting in Amman, the Arab League decided to request the UN Security Council to take punitive measures against Israel as an aggressor in accordance with articles 39 and 41 of the UN charter.

The league also decided to reject any UN decision calling for peace negotiations with Israel and to insist on Israeli compliance with UN resolutions on partition, the refugees problem, and the status of Jerusalem as a prerequisite to peace in the area.

Comment: This report, supplementing earlier summaries of the league's decisions, indicates that the Arabs hope for UN sanctions against Israel in support of their own economic blockade.
