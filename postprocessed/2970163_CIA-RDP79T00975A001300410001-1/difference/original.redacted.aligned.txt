EASTERN EUROPE

6. Yugoslavs seen willing to make concessions on Trieste:

In the opinion of the American charge in Belgrade, Yugoslavia is prepared to make substantial concessions in a five-power conference on Trieste in return for a face-saving solution. Yugoslav leaders hinted in recent conversations, however, that the conference would fail if they were "backed into a corner" by the other four powers. They suggest that some agreement on substantive points be reached in advance of actual talks.
