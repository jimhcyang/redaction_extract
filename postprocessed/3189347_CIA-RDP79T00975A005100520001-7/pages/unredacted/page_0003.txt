30 JUNE 1960

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC
British report Soviet commander in East Germany conciliatory toward their protest on 21 June police incident.

II. ASIA-AFRICA
Nepal--Clash with Chinese Communists will hamper border settlement.
Vietnamese Communists double clandestine communication network.

III. THE WEST
Venezuela claims proof of Dominican involvement in assassination plot.
Rebel groups pose growing threat to Paraguayan Government.
