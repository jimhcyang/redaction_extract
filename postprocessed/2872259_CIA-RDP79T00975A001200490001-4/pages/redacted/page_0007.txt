7. Comment on prospects for Pella's new Italian cabinet:

Prospects are reasonably good for parliamentary approval of the new all-Christian Democratic cabinet of Italian premier Giuseppe Pella. Even without a vote of confidence, however, Pella would remain as head of a caretaker government until his special powers expire on 31 August, and could probably obtain parliament's extension of the provisional budget beyond that date.

The new premier has made substantial concessions to his party's reform-minded left wing by appointing its leaders to head the Ministries of Defense, Interior, and Public Instruction. Elimination from the cabinet of both right-wing Christian Democrat Giuseppe Togni and former premier De Gasperi, together with the urgency of the budgetary situation, should ensure for Pella the necessary votes of the minor democratic parties and the abstention, if not the support, of the extreme right.
