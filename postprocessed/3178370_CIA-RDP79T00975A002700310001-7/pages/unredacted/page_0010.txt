8. PATHET LAO TROOPS TO OBSERVE LAOTIAN CEASE-FIRE

The Pathets to propagandize among the government troops to make them understand that "we are no longer against them, but wish to join them in resisting the Americans and in making Laos independent and free".

Comment. Laotian premier Souvanna Phouma, prior to his departure for France on 8 September, expressed concern over numerous "shooting incidents" which were undermining Vientiane's faith in Pathet Lao good intentions. The Communists are anxious to avoid a breakdown of the royal government-Pathet Lao negotiations. The Pathets, after some delay, have finally announced the composition of their political and military negotiating committees and have scheduled their arrival in Vientiane around 15 September.
