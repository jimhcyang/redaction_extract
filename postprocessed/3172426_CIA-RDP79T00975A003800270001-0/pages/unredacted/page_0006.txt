* Cambodia: Cambodia has agreed to extend full diplomatic recognition, including an exchange of ambassadors, to Communist China. The decision will be made public within a few days. This will give a strong boost to the pro-Peiping Chinese community in Cambodia, and probably force the withdrawal of the unaccredited Chinese Nationalist consul. It will reinforce Saigon's strong distrust of Prince Sihanouk, and defeat any hope for an immediate Cambodian/South Vietnam conciliation.
