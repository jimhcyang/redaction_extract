9 OCTOBER 1958

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC

Taiwan Strait - Chinese Communist diplomat believes cease-fire can be prolonged and that "satisfactory" results are possible in Warsaw talks. Chinese Communist naval strength in strait area further augmented.

Chinese Communists insist that any Afro-Asian UN declaration calls for US withdrawal from Taiwan.

Chinese Communists reportedly threaten to send troops into Macao in event of serious disturbances between pro-Nationalist and pro-Communist Chinese on 10 October.

Chinese Communists recognize Guinea.

II. ASIA-AFRICA

Laotian Government may fall over monetary reform issue.

Cambodia to oust Chinese Nationalist representative.

Parliamentary attacks on Jordanian prime minister may be prelude to forcing his resignation.

Nasir trying to increase control over Syria through UAR administrative re-organization.

Turkish negative attitude threatens any progress made toward Cyprus solution.

III. THE WEST

Student strikes in Panama could lead to riots.
