LATE ITEMS

*Mali Federation: The newly independent Mali Federation--comprising the states of Senegal and Soudan--appears to have broken apart as a result of the vote of the National Assembly of Senegal on 19 August to withdraw from the federation and proclaim the independence of Senegal. Senegal's action raises the imminent threat of civil war, because Mali President Modibo Keita recently warned that secession was unconstitutional and would lead to bloodshed. The main differences concern foreign policy and the allocation of top administrative posts.
