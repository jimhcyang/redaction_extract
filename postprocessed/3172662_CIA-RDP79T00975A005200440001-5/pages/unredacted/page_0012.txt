Hanoi seems to want to avoid giving any impression that
overt North Vietnamese intervention is likely under present
circumstances. A recent Hanoi broadcast emphasized the
internal nature of the "struggle" and pointedly stated that a
countercoup would be opposed "exclusively" by Laotians.
On 18 August, North Vietnam expressed its "satisfaction"
with the Souvanna government and called for "friendly re-
lations in all fields."
