25 APRIL 1960

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC

II. ASIA-AFRICA

Arab labor federation plans boycott of American shipping in all Arab states over Cleopatra case. (1)

Turkish Government's harassment of political opposition may lead to protest demonstrations during NATO meeting in Istanbul early next month. (2)

Closer Cambodian ties with Peiping may result from Chou En-lai's visit scheduled for 5 to 9 May. (3)

Chinese Nationalist High Command shows concern over recent movement of Communist jet fighters into Taiwan Strait area. (4)

Rhee plan given cool reception in South Korea; new disturbances predicted unless further concessions are made. (5)

III. THE WEST

Unity of Italian Christian Democratic party threatened in continuing political crisis. (6)

Pressure on Venezuelan President from labor confederation, which allegedly is demanding the admission of Communists into the government, may set off new power struggle. (7)
