LATIN AMERICA

9. Argentine labor may attempt coup:

The General Confederation of Labor may attempt an early coup,

Comment: The death of Senora de Peron has increased the likelihood of conflict between labor and the army. This is the first report suggesting that labor is prepared to take direct action to preserve its influence on the government.

The reported existence of an army plot to overthrow Peron, tentatively scheduled for the near future, might cause the General Confederation of Labor to attempt a preventive coup to "protect" Peron.

The General Confederation of Labor is effectively infiltrated by Communists.
