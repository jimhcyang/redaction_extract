THE ARAB-ISRAELI SITUATION
(Information as of 1700, 24 February)

An exchange of fire between Egyptian and Israeli patrols along the Gaza strip has been reported by Cairo.

Syrian chief of staff Shuqar told the American army attaché on 22 February that an agreement in principle has been reached with Czechoslovakia to supply arms. All that remains to be done is to select the types and quantities desired. Shuqar said the total amount of money involved was not known, but he believed Syria would get all the arms it needs.
