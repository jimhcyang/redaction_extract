6. NEW EVIDENCE OF SOVIET AID TO COMMUNIST CHINA'S AIRCRAFT INDUSTRY

Comment  These are the first observed contacts of this nature between the Soviet and Chinese aircraft industries.

These Soviet aid efforts may be aimed at improving the embryonic Chinese aircraft industry's capability for overhauling or modifying aircraft. On the other hand, they may be initial efforts in Communist China's long-range program for producing combat aircraft. Other current developments which could contribute to such a program are the construction of a fully integrated aluminum industry in Manchuria and the training of aeronautical engineers at an institute in Peiping. (Prepared by ORR)
