WESTERN EUROPE

8. Total ban of Communist youth organization in West Germany predicted:
official believes that a total ban of the Communist Free German Youth (FDJ) will be imposed by the government. Officials are still considering a simultaneous ban of the Communist Party and the neo-Nazi Socialist Reich Party. The West German cabinet has discussed possible measures against the FDJ, partly as a result of the demonstration against rearmament held on 17 June in the vicinity of Bonn. Ministry of Interior officials comment that this demonstration, which was eventually broken up by the local police, was extremely well planned and expertly camouflaged from prior police detection.

Comment: While it may not be possible to ban the FDJ before the World Youth Festival scheduled to be held by the Communists during August in Berlin, the stiffening attitude of the Federal Republic is likely to reduce FDJ preliminary activity and limit the participation of West German members of the FDJ in the festival.
