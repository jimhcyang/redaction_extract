8. VIOLENCE IN LEBANON

With President Chamoun out of the country on a visit to Spain and Greece and a number of ministers absent, foreign and domestic opposition elements may believe the time is opportune to unseat the Sulh cabinet, and possibly Chamoun. They have been encouraged by clashes on 3 November between police and Beirut municipal workers which resulted in the arrest of strike leaders, and the workers' case has been taken up by the opposition National Front in an effort to embarrass the government. The army, under the command of pro-Western General Shihab, is the key to the situation.
