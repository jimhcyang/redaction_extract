18 DECEMBER 1959

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC
USSR and bloc nations attempting to moderate UAR criticism of Qasim.
USSR delivers three motor torpedo boats to Iraq.

II. ASIA-AFRICA
Yemen--Troops sent against dissident tribes complain pay is in arrears.
Israel--Ben-Gurion forms new government; offers Arabs nonaggression pacts.
Morocco--Uneasy relations between King and leftist government placed under new strain.
Brussels offers to accelerate progress toward self-government for Congo.
Nepal plans to establish diplomatic relations with Czechoslovakia.
South Korea may be planning seizure of Japanese fishing boats as reprisal for Japanese - North Korean repatriation.

III. THE WEST
Haitian cabinet resigns.
