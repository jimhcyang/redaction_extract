5. No death sentences expected in Cairo Zionist spy case:

An official of the Egyptian Ministry of National Guidance is reported to have stated that those tried in the Zionist spy case will be given "moderate" sentences and none will be condemned to death. The verdicts, he said, have already been decided and will be announced "soon."

Comment: Israel has displayed unusual concern over the possible execution by Egypt of any of the 13 Jews on trial in Cairo on charges of espionage for Israel.

Sharett and Israeli Foreign Ministry officials have expressed doubts that their government could prevent "armed conflict" with Egypt if any of the accused were executed.
