III. THE WEST

Dominican Republic: The Dominican economic and financial situation, already in serious condition, will rapidly worsen between now and the end of the year unless the present abnormal level of military expenditures is curtailed or an unforeseen sharp price rise occurs in Dominican export commodities. Discontent is mounting in upper- and middle-income groups, and unemployment is rising.
