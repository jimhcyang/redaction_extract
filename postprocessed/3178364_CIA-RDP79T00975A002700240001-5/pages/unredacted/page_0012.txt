THE ARAB-ISRAELI SITUATION
(Information as of 1700, 6 September)

According to King Saud, King Hussain of Jordan desires to conclude a military agreement with Saudi Arabia similar to that recently concluded between Jordan and Syria. This agreement is believed to involve a joint military command and a joint operations staff under the authority of the Arab joint command in Cairo. Jordan's move toward co-operation with Saudi Arabia may be part of the price of major Egyptian-Syrian-Saudi arms aid for Jordan's National Guard. King Saud has invited military representatives of Egypt, Iraq, Syria and Lebanon to meet in Riyadh on 7 September to discuss measures to improve defense of the Jordan-Israel border.
