The American acting army attaché in Damascus reported on 9 October, following a reconnaissance of northern Syria, that Syrian army maneuvers appeared to have ended and that most units had returned to their home stations. No Syrian build-up was noted in northern Syria, nor were there indications of a large-scale mobilization. Reports of recent call-ups were believed to apply only to specialists.
