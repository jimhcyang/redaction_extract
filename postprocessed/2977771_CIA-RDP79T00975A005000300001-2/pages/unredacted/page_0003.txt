4 APRIL 1960

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC
Comment on Khrushchev - De Gaulle communiqué. (1)

II. ASIA-AFRICA
Nepal accepts Soviet offer to make survey for a road across its southern plain. (2)
South Africa--General strike weakened in some areas; disturbances may abate for time being. (3)
Discussions believed taking place on resumption of UAR-French relations. (4)
Thailand--Government may participate in trade tour of Eastern European countries. (5)
