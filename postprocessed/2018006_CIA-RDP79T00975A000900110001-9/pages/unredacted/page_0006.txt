WESTERN EUROPE
6. East Germans reportedly activate First Army Group headquarters:
The East German Government has activated the First Army Group headquarters at Pasewalk, north of Berlin, under the command of Brigadier General Hermann Rentzsch. The headquarters allegedly commands three infantry divisions with supporting arms, with a total strength of 30,000 to 35,000 men, and it may also control an additional 18,000 to 20,000 men in a major unit on Ruegen Island.

The formation of such a headquarters for administrative purposes is possible, but the state of training and armament of the Alert Police makes it unlikely that full divisions exist as yet or that a new headquarters would have any tactical responsibilities.
