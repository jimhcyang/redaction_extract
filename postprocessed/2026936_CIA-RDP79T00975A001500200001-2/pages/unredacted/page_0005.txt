Comment: The spring cultivation directive is a statement of the Peiping regime's basic agricultural policy. In previous years Peiping recognized the unfavorable effects of socialization programs on agricultural production and discontinued such activities in rural areas before spring. Last year, for example, the annual directive on spring cultivation called encouragement of output "the central task which supersedes all other tasks," and instructed cadres to postpone or cancel other work which might obstruct spring cultivation.

The continued formation of mutual aid teams and farm producers' cooperatives through the growing season was unexpected and reflects the speed of Peiping's movement toward a controlled economy.

3. Chinese Communist MIG division at Canton strengthened:

The Chinese Communist 18th Air Division at Canton in South China has been strengthened by the addition of 28 MIG-15's and now has 82 planes.

Apparently to absorb the increase, a third regiment of the division has been activated for the first time. Two of the division's regiments, with a total of 54 planes, are operating from Paiyun airfield at Canton. The third, with 28 aircraft, is flying from the recently completed field at Foshan, about 20 miles southwest of Canton.

Comment: An increase of jet fighter strength raises the estimated jet fighter strength in Central-South China to about 190. Combat-trained in Korea, the 18th Division is the only known jet fighter unit based along China's southern coast.
