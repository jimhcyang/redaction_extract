FAR EAST

2. Indian firm repeats offer of lubricating oil to China:
On 12 May a firm in Calcutta offered the Chinese Communist official trade agency in Kunming 300,000 gallons of lubricating oil.

Comment: Indian offers of this lubricating oil to the Chinese Communists were first noted in 1951. However, American consular officials were assured at the time that the Indian Government was "taking steps to put off shipment."
