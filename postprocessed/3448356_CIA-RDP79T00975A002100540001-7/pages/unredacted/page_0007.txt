4. PERU TO PERMIT CZECH LEGATION IN LIMA TO RESUME ACTIVITY

Comment

The last Czech chargé in Lima resigned in March 1948, shortly after the Communists took over his country, and the legation property since then has been in a caretaker status.

Resumption of Czech diplomatic activity in Peru, now the seventh country in Latin America to have Soviet bloc representation, is evidence of the success being achieved by the bloc in its drive to expand diplomatic and commercial relations in the western hemisphere.
