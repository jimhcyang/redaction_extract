Comment: French Ambassador Joxe, although anti-Communist, is reported to have definite leftist tendencies, and to be impressed by Soviet sincerity regarding the dangers implicit in "aggressive" American intentions.

Prime Minister Pinay's recent rejection of the American note on military expenditures and the universal press approval for his independent stand with regard to American "interference" indicate that the French Government, given sufficient encouragement by the USSR, may strike out on a course of its own with respect to such policies as EDC and rearmament.
