SOVIET UNION

3. USSR conducts military training against atomic weapons:
Soviet military personnel are being trained in defense against atomic attack. In late January, the 23rd Antiaircraft Artillery Division stationed near Vienna engaged in a tactical exercise including a simulated atomic bomb attack.

Comment: Such training in the defensive aspects of atomic warfare would logically proceed from Soviet knowledge that the United States has developed nuclear weapons of a tactical type.
