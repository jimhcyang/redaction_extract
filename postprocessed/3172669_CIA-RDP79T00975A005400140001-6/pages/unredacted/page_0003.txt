II. ASIA-AFRICA

New Soviet arms shipment arrives in Guinea; USSR to train personnel for Guinean Navy.

El Salvador-- Military preparing counter-coup to oust pro-Communist and pro-Castro civilian members of ruling junta; latter reportedly planning peasant militia.
