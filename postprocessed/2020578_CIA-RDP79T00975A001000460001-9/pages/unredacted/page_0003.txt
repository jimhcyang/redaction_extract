SOUTHEAST ASIA

1. Viet Minh concentration near the Tonkin delta reported:

During a period when inclement weather and Viet Minh troop reorganization are curtailing all military operations in Tonkin, the insurgent forces appear to be still concentrating generally in the Phu Tho area, about 50 miles northwest of Hanoi.

2. Laurel and Magsaysay reportedly to head Philippine Nacionalista ticket:

Nacionalista Party senators agreed on Senator Laurel as the party's candidate for president and Defense Secretary Magsaysay for vice president. The latter has reportedly agreed, and has also been promised the defense portfolio.

Laurel might possibly withdraw at the convention in favor of Magsaysay.

Comment: Both Magsaysay and independent Senator Tanada have informed the American Embassy of an agreement signed in early November between Tanada and a group of Nacionalista senators to support Magsaysay as the presidential candidate at their party convention now scheduled for 12 April. Doubt has been expressed, however, about the good faith of some of the signers of the agreement.
