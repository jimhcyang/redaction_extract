LATIN AMERICA

7. Argentine ambassador may be intermediary for re-establishment of Soviet diplomatic relations with Latin American countries:

Ambassador Bohlen reports that rumors in the diplomatic colony in Moscow suggest that Argentine ambassador Bravo is acting as intermediary in efforts to re-establish diplomatic relations between the USSR and certain Latin American countries. Bravo was evasive when Ambassador Bohlen raised the subject.

Comment: The Soviet Union at this time maintains diplomatic relations with only three Latin American countries -- Argentina, Uruguay and Mexico.
