10 JUNE 1960

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC
Chinese speakers at WFTU meeting in Peiping encourage dissent from Moscow’s views on East-West relations, solicit support for China’s hard line toward US. (1)

II. ASIA-AFRICA
Real power in Turkey reportedly in hands of officer group which arranged Menderes ouster (2)

Widespread Congolese opposition to Lumumba poses threat of major political crisis in Congo even before 30 June independence date. (3)
