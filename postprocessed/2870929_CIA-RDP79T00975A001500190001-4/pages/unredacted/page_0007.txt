8. Comment on attempts of Egyptian RCC to show it has army support:

Colonel Nasr's group is attempting to create the impression that it has the support of the armed forces.

The extent of genuine army support for Colonel Nasr and the council is, accordingly, open to question. The council is therefore likely to avoid a situation which would directly test the army's loyalty.
