6 MARCH 1959

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC
Khrushchev's willingness to postpone 27 May deadline seen intended to undercut Western objections to negotiations.
Khrushchev may meet with leader of West German opposition party.
USSR indicates it will intensify pressures on Iran.

II. ASIA-AFRICA
Britain to supply Iraq with military equipment if US and Baghdad Pact agree.
Sudan - Abboud forms new Supreme Council; stronger neutralist policy likely.
Ceylon to negotiate civil air agreement with Communist China.
Nationalist China using traffic accident to press for status-of-forces agreement with US.

III. THE WEST
De Gaulle and Adenauer concerned over Macmillan's endorsement of disengagement.
Adenauer's party rejects his decision to nominate Erhard for president; Erhard now likeliest successor as chancellor.
France preparing intensified efforts against Algerian rebels.
