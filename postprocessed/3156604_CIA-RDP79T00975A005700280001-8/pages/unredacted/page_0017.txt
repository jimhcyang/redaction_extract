Supplementing the program, Nasution has replaced the ineffective army commander in Central Java and the deputy commander in East Java by forceful anti-Communist officers. Former rebel troops from North Celebes are also to be stationed in these areas.

Nasution has informed the American ambassador that he has representatives in the Netherlands who are in contact with leaders of Dutch political parties as part of an effort to influence the Dutch toward a settlement of the New Guinea issue.
