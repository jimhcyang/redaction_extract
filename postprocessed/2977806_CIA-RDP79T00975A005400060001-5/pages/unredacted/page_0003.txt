7 NOVEMBER 1960

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC

II. ASIA-AFRICA

Iraq--Leftist mob and police clash in Baghdad on 5 November; riot area still under heavy guard on 6 November.

Opposition to Sudanese military regime continuing to spread, with dissident army elements rumored planning an early coup.

Situation in the Congo.

III. THE WEST

De Gaulle's 4 November speech deepens rightist apprehension, tempers leftist impatience over Algerian issue.

Argentine general strike scheduled to begin today may produce some disturbances.

El Salvador--Army officers, apprehensive over leftist gains in provisional government, reportedly plan early countercoup.

LATE ITEM

Soviet and Chinese spokesmen on eve of October Revolution anniversary celebrations indicate neither side has retreated from its positions in Sino-Soviet dispute.
