28 JULY 1959

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC
Khrushchev planning visit to Rumania on 23 August.

II. ASIA-AFRICA
Iraq bans Communist-inspired purge committees in government offices.
Imam of Yemen accepts invitation to visit Nasir on trip home.
India--Government decision impending on whether to intervene in Kerala.
African protests increasing against French plan to test nuclear device in Sahara.

III. THE WEST
French Air Force apparently intends to proceed with proposed reconnaissance of Libya for Algerian rebel activity.

LATE ITEM
Soviet missiles fail to reach intended impact area following two valid countdowns at Tyura Tam.
