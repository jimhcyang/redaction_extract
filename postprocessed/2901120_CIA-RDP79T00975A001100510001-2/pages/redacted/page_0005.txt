NEAR EAST - AFRICA

5. Arab Legion reported regrouping on Israeli frontier:

Ambassador Green reports that the situation along the Israeli-Jordanian border is "more potentially explosive" now than at any time in the past 10 months. He has been authoritatively informed that Arab Legion units on the border are being reinforced and regrouped in preparation for any eventuality.

The ambassador warns that his counsel of moderation may fall on deaf ears because the Jordanian prime minister appears to lack his predecessor's restraint.

Comment: In the past the presence of the Arab Legion, which is under British control, has been a moderating influence in Jordan's relations with Israel. This fact, along with the superior strength of the Israeli forces, suggests that the legion's moves are precautionary.

On the diplomatic front, the Jordanian government has asked the United States, as a guarantor of the status quo, to take steps against Israel's "aggressive activities."
