7. Grave financial difficulties threaten French Government:

Leaders of the major non-Communist French political parties predict that the government's grave financial predicament will cause Premier Mayer's downfall in May.

The American Embassy in Paris expects that in any event parliamentary opposition to new taxes, investment retrenchment, and administrative reforms will force cuts in the French military program. It also foresees a real danger that any new premier would agree to put off EDC ratification in return for support on urgent economic problems.
