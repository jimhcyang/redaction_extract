While the projects were offered as gifts, former Prime Minister U Nu later insisted that Burma pay for them in 20 annual payments of rice. A technological institute, hotel, and hospital included in this offer are well on their way toward completion, but the Burmese Government delayed initiation of the other two projects for budgetary reasons. The mission has been instructed to obtain projects in the fields of irrigation, flood control, or port development, all on a grant basis.
