22 JULY 1959

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC
Ambassador Thompson comments on Khrushchev's designation of Koslov as his eventual successor.
Two additional Soviet destroyers reach Indonesian waters, apparently for transfer to Indonesian Navy.

II. ASIA-AFRICA
Yemen--Tension rising at prospect of Imam's return.
Burma--Military regime making creditable record; faces internal disagreement on best means of staying in power.
Afghanistan--Soviet technicians to assist in bridge construction in sensitive area near Pakistani frontier.

III. THE WEST
Conclusions of special USIB committee on Berlin situation.
