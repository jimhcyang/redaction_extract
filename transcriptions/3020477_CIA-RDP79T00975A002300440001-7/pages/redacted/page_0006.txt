THE ARAB-ISRAELI SITUATION
(Information as of 1700, 5 January)

The American military attaché in Tel Aviv reports that Israel has taken steps to increase its petroleum stocks by 250 percent by 1 March. According to the chairman of the principal organization for settling immigrants in Israel, Tel Aviv has decided to cut its investment in new immigrant housing in order to meet increased military expenditures.

Saudi Arabia has presented a formal request to purchase from United States 25 F-86F jet fighters, and six T-33 jet trainers.
