The Indonesian parliament, with the Masjumi abstaining, on 18 December passed the dual nationality treaty which deals with the status of the Overseas Chinese and was negotiated with Communist China over two years ago. This action was probably prompted by Peiping's strong support of Indonesia's Irian claim. It may also foreshadow similar action on the long-pending Soviet aid agreement.
