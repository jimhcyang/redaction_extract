Laos: The Kong Le - Pathet Lao forces are beginning to exert pressure against the government's build-up area some 40 miles south of Xieng Khouang town in what may be an attempt to break up General Phoumi's planned offensive to recapture the Plaine des Jarres. An advance government command post in this area reportedly has withdrawn southward following an artillery-supported enemy attack. Meanwhile, the government is strengthening Luang Prabang's defenses on the basis of new reports of a threatened Pathet Lao attack from the north.

Airlift operations into Laos by bloc transports continued through 13 January. Five Soviet LI-2 (C-47 type) transports have been scheduled for Peiping from Irkutsk. They are probably destined for use in the Laos airlift.
