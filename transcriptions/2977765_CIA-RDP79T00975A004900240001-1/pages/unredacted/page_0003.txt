30 JANUARY 1960

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC

GMAIC statement on Soviet 20 January mid-Pacific test.

Soviet missile test range apparently failed 29 January in new attempt to launch to mid-Pacific.

USSR agrees to supply UAR with additional combat jet aircraft.

II. ASIA-AFRICA

Tunisia bringing pressure on Paris to negotiate on Bizerte base question.

Belgian Congo--Africans reject Belgian plan for stringing out transfer of sovereignty.

Afghanistan--Government's decision to force social and economic reform likely to arouse further tribal opposition.

Japan--Kishi considering calling early elections on issue of new US treaty.

Laos--Communists avoiding clashes but their subversive campaign is undiminished.

III. THE WEST

Latin American governments wary of Cuba's proposed "hungry nations" conference but some interest expressed in Asia and Africa.

LATE ITEM

Algeria-France--De Gaulle's reaffirmation of his self-determination policy for Algeria and his command to the army to restore order has infuriated Algerian insurgents.
