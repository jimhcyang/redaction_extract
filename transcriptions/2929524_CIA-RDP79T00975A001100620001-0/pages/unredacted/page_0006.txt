7. Egypt considers financial aid to Libya:
Comment: This is the first evidence that Egypt is considering financial aid to Libya. The state is at present dependent on Britain, which under terms of a temporary agreement, covers the annual deficit amounting to more than $3,000,000 for 1952-53.

Substantial aid from Cairo would considerably increase Egyptian influence at the expense of the West and would further orient Libya toward the Arab Middle East, a trend which started when it joined the Arab League last March.

8. East Germans reverse decision on physical-military training for party members:
Abandonment by the Socialist Unity Party of previously announced plans for physical-military training of party members and candidates in the 18-30 year age group is attributed by American observers in Berlin both to Soviet objections and to technical difficulties.
