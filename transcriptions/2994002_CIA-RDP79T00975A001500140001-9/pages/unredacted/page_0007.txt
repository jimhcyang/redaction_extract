5. Chinese Communist TU-4's may have begun night training:
Comment: Introduction of night training would signal another phase in the progress of Peiping's TU-4 unit. Since the arrival in China of ten TU-4's from the USSR in February 1953, Chinese Communist bomber crews have carried out practice bombing flights ranging up to 1,100 miles. The period of their familiarization training with this B-29-type aircraft may be coming to a close.
