1. INITIAL EGYPTIAN REACTION TO US STATEMENT ON HIGH DAM

Comment on:

Egyptian spokesmen in Cairo state that replies to the American and British withdrawal of offers on the dam will be made after the government studies reports from its ambassadors in Washington and London.

The Egyptian press, which will be followed by the Arab press generally, has taken a bitter line toward the US and Britain over the withdrawal of the offer to help build the dam and asserts that the dam will be built whether the "imperialists" help or not. Radio Cairo, meanwhile, is broadcasting reports that Soviet news releases are commenting favorably on Egypt's "new and independent policy" and quoting Soviet press criticism of the American action.
