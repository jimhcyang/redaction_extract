SOUTHEAST ASIA

NEAR EAST-AFRICA

4. Egyptian group organized to curb French interests:

An Egyptian committee, headed by former Foreign Minister Salaheddin, has been organized to unify all groups which support the North African nationalists. Special subcommittees have been established to spread propaganda and to organize a boycott of French trade and culture.

The committee reportedly intends to ask General Nagib to curtail French activities in Egypt.
