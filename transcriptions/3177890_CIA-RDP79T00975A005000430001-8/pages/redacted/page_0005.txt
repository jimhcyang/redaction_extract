III. THE WEST

Cuba: Fidel Castro is apparently worried about his inability thus far to suppress the guerrilla bands now operating in the rugged Sierra Maestra, where outlaw bands have traditionally sought refuge.

The guerrilla activity will be a test of the support Castro claims to have among the peasants of the Sierra Maestra, since the guerrillas cannot operate there long without peasant support.
