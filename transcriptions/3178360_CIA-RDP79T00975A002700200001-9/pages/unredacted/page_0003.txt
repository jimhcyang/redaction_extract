1. FRENCH AND BRITISH REPORTEDLY DETERMINED TO IMPOSE SOLUTION ON NASR

Comment
The current overt French show of force, just before the negotiations between Nasr and the Suez committee, indicates Paris' desire to give the appearance of "imposing" a solution on Nasr despite disclaimers of this intention by government spokesmen. Other moves in this direction can be expected in view of the dominance in French thinking of the idea that "all is lost in Algeria" if Nasr is not forced to back down.
