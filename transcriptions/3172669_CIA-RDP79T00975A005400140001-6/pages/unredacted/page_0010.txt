In the political realm, little apparent progress was made in separate talks the King held with Phoumi and former Premier Phoui. Phoumi reportedly tried to persuade the King to withdraw his support from Souvanna and announce the formation of a new government, but was rebuffed. The King insisted he must act within the constitution. On the other hand, the King showed little hope that a meeting between Souvanna, Phoui, and Phoumi would produce a political settlement.
