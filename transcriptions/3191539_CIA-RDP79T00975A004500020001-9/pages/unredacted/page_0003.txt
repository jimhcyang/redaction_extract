13 MAY 1959

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC

Recent Khrushchev speeches attempt to convey attitude of reasonableness coupled with claims of Soviet military predominance.

Further reorganization of Soviet agriculture likely.

USSR planning pipeline connecting Urals-Volga oil field with refineries in East Europe.

II. ASIA-AFRICA

Morocco asks for joint commission to fix precise evacuation period for US bases.

Communist press steps up pressure on Qasim to legalize parties and appoint Communists to cabinet.

Nine independent African states to hold conference on Algerian problem this summer.

Indochina - France to curtail its financial contributions to ICC.

III. THE WEST

De Gaulle willing to discuss "over-all problems" of Algeria with rebel leader Abbas.

IV.

Conclusions of special USIB committee on the Berlin situation.
