7 JANUARY 1961

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC

Khrushchev reportedly cites need to establish contact with new US administration as reason for postponing Soviet party congress.

II. ASIA-AFRICA

Nigeria severs diplomatic relations with France.

Somali defense officials indicate opposition to proposal for Anglo-Italian military training mission, with preference for UAR assistance.

Situation in the Congo.

Situation in Laos.

III. THE WEST

Dominican Republic seeks diplomatic relations with USSR and others of Soviet bloc.

Panamanian security forces alerted to possible clashes in capital between pro- and anti-Castro factions.
