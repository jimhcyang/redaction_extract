8. TURKISH TROOPS MOVING TO SYRIAN BORDER

At least two Turkish infantry regiments and three light artillery battalions of the 28th Infantry Division and units of the Fifth Armored Brigade, stationed at Ankara, have begun moving south to Iskenderun, according to the American army attache in Ankara. Eight to ten days will be required for completion of the move.

These troops, in addition to the 39th Division, which has already moved to the area, will raise the total troop strength to over 20,000 along the Syrian border between the Euphrates and the Mediterranean.
