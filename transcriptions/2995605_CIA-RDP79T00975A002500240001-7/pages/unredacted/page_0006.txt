3. SOVIET TECHNICIANS DEPART FOR BURMA

the USSR wishes to send the "best available men and material" to Burma for the technological institute offered by Bulganin and Khrushchev last December.

Eight experts, including a director and deputy directors of a technological institute in Moscow, left for Rangoon by air on 17 April,

Comment
If Moscow fulfills its commitments to Burma promptly and competently, without making any political demands, it would probably allay Burmese suspicions of Soviet intentions and reduce Burmese reluctance to admit large numbers of Soviet advisers and technicians.
