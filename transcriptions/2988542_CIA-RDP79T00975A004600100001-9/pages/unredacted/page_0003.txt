11 JULY 1959

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC

Khrushchev told Harriman he and Mikoyan agree on Kozlov as their eventual successor.

Chinese Communists establishing propaganda center in Cuba as part of effort to woo Latin Americans.

II. ASIA-AFRICA

Iraq--Qasim preparing to meet Communist show of force he expects during celebration of 14 July revolution.

Malayan stability threatened by split in ruling Alliance party.

III. THE WEST

Geneva Conference--Bonn will probably press for greater role for West Berlin's representatives.

French and West Germans disagree over questions of relations between European Common Market and projected little free trade area.

Cuba to negotiate for arms factory from Czechoslovakia.
