2 JULY 1960

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC

II. ASIA-AFRICA

Japanese leadership crisis unresolved; leftists continue agitation. (Page 1)

South Korea renews seizures of Japanese fishing vessels. (Page 3)

New Turkish regime concerned that its "revolutionary" aims are too little known. (Page 4)

Nationalist extremist sentiment appears rising in Nyasaland; Federation of Rhodesia reported planning to move in troops in case of disorders. (Page 5)

III. THE WEST

Bonn inclined not to hold early Bundestag sessions in Berlin as customary to avoid giving Moscow pretext for protests or harassment. (Page 6)
