THE ARAB-ISRAELI SITUATION
(Information as of 1700, 5 April)

See item 1, page 3, for comment on Egyptian-Israeli fighting along the Gaza strip.

Israeli regular units have been withdrawn from the Lebanese border during the past two weeks, according to the Lebanese chief of staff. A similar Israeli withdrawal from the Syrian frontier was reported previously. These movements may indicate that Israel is more immediately concerned over possible action along the Egyptian border.
