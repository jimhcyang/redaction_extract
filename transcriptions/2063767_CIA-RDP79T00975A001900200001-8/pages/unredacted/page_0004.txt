SOUTHEAST ASIA  

Laotian government agrees to continue talks with Communists:  

Later information on the "cease-fire agreement" between the royal government of Laos and the Communist-backed Pathet Lao indicates that it provides merely that each side shall henceforth refrain from hostile acts against the other, and that a "mixed political committee" will be formed. It is anticipated that the committee will hold talks in Vientiane to fulfill the royal government's pledge at Geneva to reintegrate the Pathets into the national community.
