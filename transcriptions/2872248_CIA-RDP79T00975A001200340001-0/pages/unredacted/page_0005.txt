Comment: Many right-wing members of the government party are landowners. Although the decree reportedly will call for a gradual, orderly process of reform with compensation for expropriated lands, the controversy over it may bring to a head various intraparty disputes.

Meanwhile the powerful Bolivian Labor Confederation last week passed a resolution demanding land nationalization without compensation, and organization of the Indians into collectives to work the landed estates. If the agrarian syndicates, some of which are led by Communists, follow this line rather than the expected milder government proposals, serious civil disturbances may result.
