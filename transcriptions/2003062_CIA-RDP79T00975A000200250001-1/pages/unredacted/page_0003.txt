2. De Lattre again exhibits antagonism toward US economic aid to Indo-China.
US Minister Heath in Saigon reports that, while General de Lattre's "apparent new policy" of respecting Vietnamese aspirations and his support for the creation of a truly National Army is intelligent and beneficial, De Lattre has, however, evidenced increased determination to exclude "foreign" (i.e., American) influence from French-Viet relations.
