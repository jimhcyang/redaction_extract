FAR EAST

5. Rhee plans to arrest Communist truce supervisors in South Korea:

President Rhee instructed Army Chief of Staff General Chong Il-kwon about two weeks ago to prepare for the arrest of the Czech and Polish members of the neutral nations truce inspection teams in South Korea, Eighth Army commander General Taylor informed embassy officials on 17 July.

Chong, who said confidentially Rhee would not do anything imprudent prior to his visit to Washington next week, is deeply concerned about possible action after Rhee's return.

Comment: Rhee has said previously he would eject Communist truce supervisors and possibly Swiss and Swedish members as well.
