5. COMMENT ON PROSPECTS FOR MILITARY COUP IN BRAZIL

According to the press, anti-Kubitschek forces are planning a move to disqualify Kubitschek and Goulart by court action on the charge that their margin of victory was accounted for by Communist votes. Since the Communist Party is illegal and a federal law prohibits candidates from financial dealings with illegal parties, they presumably hope that Kubitschek's election would be invalidated.

there were too many variables in the situation to predict with certainty the likelihood of a coup but that if one is attempted the most favorable time would be within the next three weeks.
