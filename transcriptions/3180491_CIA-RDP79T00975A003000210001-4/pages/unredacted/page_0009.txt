4. SOVIET BLOC AGREES TO SHIP ARMS TO ALGERIAN REBELS

an arms contract with Czechoslovakia would be signed the arms would be sent to Poland for shipment

Although the Czechs have indicated willingness to provide arms for rebel activity in Algeria, they are demanding that a credit be opened immediately and that the arms be paid for prior to the departure of the ship. The Egyptian negotiators in Prague stated that an arms contract with a West German firm had been canceled to provide the necessary funds, which would be available by 28 March.
