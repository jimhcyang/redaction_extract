2 MAY 1959

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC
Peiping takes unusual steps in preparing rebuttal to Nehru's assertion that Tibetan nationalism was cause of revolt.

II. ASIA-AFRICA
Dalai Lama differs with Nehru on course of action on Tibet; he plans to press for independence.
Iraq - US ambassador's interview with Qasim.
Jordan - Report from British sources of plan to assassinate King.
Pakistan planning ambitious economic program.

III. THE WEST
De Gaulle's views on Algeria.
