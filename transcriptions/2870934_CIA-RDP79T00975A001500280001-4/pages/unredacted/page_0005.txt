NEAR EAST - AFRICA

3. Saudi king may not renew Dhahran Air Base Agreement:

Comment: The Dhahran agreement is operative until June 1956 and is scheduled to continue thereafter for five more years unless six months' notice is given by either country of an intention to terminate or modify the agreement.

This is the first indication that Saud, who has been king only since last November, does not plan to renew the agreement. As yet the Saudi government has not intimated its intentions on the matter.

WESTERN EUROPE

4. Monnet's tenure as CSC president seen threatened:

Jean Monnet is "on his way out," as president of the High Authority of the European Coal-Steel Community,

told an American embassy official in Rome on 7 April that Monnet is "completely opposed" by the French government, and his capabilities are now being questioned by West Germans. He added that Monnet has lost his former drive.
