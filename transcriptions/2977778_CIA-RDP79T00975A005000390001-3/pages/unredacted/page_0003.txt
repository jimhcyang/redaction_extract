14 APRIL 1960

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC
Polish grain crop badly damaged. Considerable increase in grain imports will be required.

II. ASIA-AFRICA
Algerian rebels to accept volunteers for fighting in Algeria.
Moroccan King says projected schedule for delivery of US military equipment "completely unacceptable."
South Vietnam--Guerrilla warfare specialists say it will take three years to curb Communist terrorism.
South Korea--President Rhee considering harsh measures to restore order at Masan.

III. THE WEST
Cyprus--Positions of British, Greek and Turkish Cypriots reported closer together.
