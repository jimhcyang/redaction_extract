LATIN AMERICA

8. Chile supports Bolivia in tin negotiations with US:

The Chilean Ambassador to the United States has told his Foreign Office that in accordance with previous instructions, he has worked to support the Bolivian Ambassador in the difficult tin negotiations with the Reconstruction Finance Corporation.

In a circular to its representatives in Latin America, the Bolivian Foreign Office reported that the President of Chile sent a letter to President Truman supporting Bolivia's position, and that other Latin American opinion is also favorable. The representatives were then instructed to renew their propaganda efforts against the RFC's intransigence.

Comment: Bolivia has stressed that its problem affects all of Latin America which is vitally dependent on the production of raw materials.
