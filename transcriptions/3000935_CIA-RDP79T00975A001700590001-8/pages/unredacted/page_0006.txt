4. Upsurge in Moroccan terrorism expected:

Anti-American feeling has risen sharply following the beating of a Moroccan by an American officer in Rabat.

Comment: Ever since the violence of early August, terrorism has continued in Morocco on a reduced scale despite the severe security restrictions employed by French military forces. Terrorism could flare up sharply at any time and take an anti-American tack, though this has been avoided up to the present because of the nationalists' hope for American support.

King Hussain of Jordan, in protesting to Ambassador Mallory against repeated Israeli army incursions into Jordan, declared that if Israel continues to ask for trouble, Jordan can provide plenty.
