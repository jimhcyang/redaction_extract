Ecuador-Czechoslovakia: Foreign Minister Chiriboga has announced that a Czech legation will be opened in Quito shortly and that Ecuador will establish a diplomatic mission of the same rank in Prague next February. The former Ponce regime ordered the closure in September 1957 of the Czech legation—the only bloc mission in Quito—without severing relations, but generated accreditation to a Czech minister in Brazil a few weeks before its new administration was inaugurated on 1 September.
