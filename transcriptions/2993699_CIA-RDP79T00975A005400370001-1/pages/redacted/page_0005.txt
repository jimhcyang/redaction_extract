III. THE WEST

Ecuador-Czechoslovakia: Foreign Minister Chiriboga has announced that a Czech legation will be opened in Quito shortly and that Ecuador will establish a diplomatic mission of the same rank in Prague next January. The former Ponce regime ordered the closure in September 1957 of the Czech location--the only bloc mission in Quito--without severing relations, but granted accreditation to the Czech minister in Brazil a few weeks before the new administration was inaugurated on 1 September. The latest Ecuadoran minister of education recently returned from Prague where he signed into a preliminary agreement to purchase about $300,000 in technical equipment for schools.
