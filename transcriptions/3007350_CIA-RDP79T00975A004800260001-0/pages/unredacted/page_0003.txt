1 DECEMBER 1959

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC
Communist China probably has MIG-19 supersonic fighters in at least regimental strength.

II. ASIA-AFRICA
Japan--Reaction to disorders of 27 November likely to hurt leftist efforts to rally support against US-Japanese Security Treaty.
Indonesia--Communist party's support of Peiping policies has placed it in embarrassing position.
Nationalist China plans to airdrop small-arms ammunition to KMT irregulars in northern Burma.
Nepalese prime minister to visit New Delhi and Peiping.
Greece declare Soviet second secretary persona non grata; warns ambassador.
