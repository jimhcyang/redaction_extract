5. COMMUNISTS REPORTED PLANNING FURTHER ATTACKS ON AMERICANS IN SOUTH VIETNAM

Comment on:

Communist agents in South Vietnam are planning to attack members of the American delegation to the Colombo Plan conference prior to their departure from Saigon. Other reports from Saigon have also stated that the 22 October bombings which injured 13 Americans would be followed in the near future by additional assaults on American personnel and property.

As a result of this information, President Diem offered to provide bodyguards for all members of the United States delegation. The American embassy also has taken extensive precautions to ensure the safety of Americans in South Vietnam.

26 Oct 57 Current Intelligence Bulletin Page 7
