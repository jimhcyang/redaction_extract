3. American Ambassador comments on Burmese statement concerning Chinese Nationalists:
Ambassador Sebald, in commenting on the text of Premier Nu's statement charging aggression by the Chinese Nationalists, notes the absence of any recrimination against the United States. He believes this indicates that the Burmese Government will continue friendly relations with the West. He points out, however, that the speech also reflects a fear of the attitude Peiping might take as a result of possible UN discussions and foreshadows closer Burmese cooperation with the Chinese Communists. The Ambassador also emphasizes the bitterness in the speech toward the Nationalist Government and the Burmese sense of hopelessness at facing the issue alone.
