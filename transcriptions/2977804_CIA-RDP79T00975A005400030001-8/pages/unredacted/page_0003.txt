3 NOVEMBER 1960

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC
Moscow apparently has accepted Iranian proposal for "good-will mission" to USSR headed by Premier Sharif-Emami.

II. ASIA-AFRICA
Gursel regime in Turkey, its prestige damaged by political trials and university dismissals, faces prospect of increasing organized opposition.
Situation in the Congo.

III. THE WEST
Leftists in El Salvador launch bid for power; situation appears heading for open clash between military and mobs stirred up by leftist agitation.
Possible threat to Panama's newly installed Chiari administration seen in activities of country's powerful Arias Madrid family.
