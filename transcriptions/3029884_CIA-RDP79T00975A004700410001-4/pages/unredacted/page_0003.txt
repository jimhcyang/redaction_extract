17 OCTOBER 1959

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC

Soviet decree calling for increase in consumer goods will not affect investment or military programs.

Khrushchev says any Berlin solution acceptable, except for absorption of West Berlin by West Germany.

II. ASIA-AFRICA

Iraq--Military Governor Abdi indicates UAR to be blamed for attempt against Qasim.

Prince Faysal says Saudi Arabia will not compromise in dispute with ARAMCO.

Yemeni Imam's brother en route to Moscow; may be receptive to Soviet aid offer.
