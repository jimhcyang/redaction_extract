WESTERN EUROPE

7. French minister planning attack on EDC on constitutional grounds:

Edmond Barrachin, ex-Gaullist and minister of state for constitutional revision in the French cabinet, told the American embassy that he plans to lead an attack on the EDC treaty on the grounds that it contravenes the constitution. He is convinced that sufficient votes can be marshaled in the upper house of parliament to call for a decision by the National Constitutional Commission.

Comment: An adverse decision by the commission, composed of the presidents of the two houses and ten other members elected by but not from the two houses of parliament, would make ratification dependent on revision of the constitution by the Assembly. This would take a minimum of three months, after which Assembly action on EDC ratification would have to begin again.
