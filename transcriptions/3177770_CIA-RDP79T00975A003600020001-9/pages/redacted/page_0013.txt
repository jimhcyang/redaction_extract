CENTRAL INTELLIGENCE BULLETIN
3 March 1958

DAILY BRIEF

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC

Tunisian situation: The Bourguiba government is trying to arouse world opinion against French plans to seal the Algerian-Tunisian border by evacuating local populations and creating a large "no man's land" on the Algerian side. Although French Minister for Algeria Lacoste has issued a communiqué denying that measures to create such a zone have been started, Tunisia claims to have discovered French land mines on Tunisian territory.
