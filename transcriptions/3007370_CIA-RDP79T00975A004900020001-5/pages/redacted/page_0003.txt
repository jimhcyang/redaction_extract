4 JANUARY 1960

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC
Latest Chinese Communist note to India contains no significant new offers. (1)
Sino-Soviet bloc extended slightly over $1 billion in new aid credits to non-Communist countries in 1959. (2)

II. ASIA-AFRICA
Laos--Negotiations continue among anti-Communist elements for new government. (3)
New Iraqi law opens way for legal political parties; Qasim reported planning cabinet and military changes. (4)
Belgium apparently ready to accelerate Congolese progress toward independence. (5)
