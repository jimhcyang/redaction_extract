8. TOP SOVIET DIPLOMATIC OFFICIALS IN MEXICO VISIT ECUADOR AND PERU

Comment

The ambassador and the first secretary are not known to have traveled previously outside of Mexico in Latin America.

The USSR does not maintain diplomatic relations with Ecuador and Peru. The stop at Quito, which included considerable time spent with the Czech chargé there, may therefore be related to the reported possibility of an early diplomatic break between Ecuador and Czechoslovakia. The Czech chargé has been reported seriously concerned over Ecuadoran president Velasco's statements that the recent student riots, which for a period threatened the stability of the Quito regime, were inspired by foreigners and "Ecuadoran professors trained in Czechoslovakia."
