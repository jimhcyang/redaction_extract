10. Costa Rica threatens appeal to OAS in dispute with Nicaragua:

Costa Rican foreign minister Esquivel informed Ambassador Hill on 13 April that his government was demanding retraction of the unofficial Nicaraguan charges, ascribed to President Somoza, that high Costa Rican officials were involved in the recent plot against Somoza and that Costa Rica is a center of Communism. Esquivel stated that if Nicaragua did not comply by midnight, his government would ask the Organization of American States to investigate the dispute.

The American embassy believes that this action "will create a grave situation."
