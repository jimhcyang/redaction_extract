GENERAL

2. British charge at Peiping speculates on Chinese Communist position at Geneva:

The British chargé at Peiping believes the Chinese Communists at Geneva will put forth a unification proposal for Korea similar to the Soviet plan for Germany: withdrawal of foreign troops, establishment of a coalition government, and general elections conducted by that government.

The charge thinks the Chinese will not withdraw their support of the Viet Minh in Indochina and are probably willing to increase their aid considerably if necessary to prevent a Viet Minh defeat.
