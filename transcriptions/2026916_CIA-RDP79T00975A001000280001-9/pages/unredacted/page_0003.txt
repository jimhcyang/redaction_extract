SOUTHEAST ASIA

1. Reduction in Viet Minh military operations indicated:

Comment: The bulk of the Viet Minh forces engaged in the fall campaign in western Tonkin have withdrawn to the northwest of the French defense perimeter where, they are undergoing additional training and are being resupplied.

The pattern of previous campaigns suggests that the Viet Minh will not undertake another major offensive in Tonkin before March at the earliest.

2. Burmese Communists anticipate additional defections:

The Burmese Communist Party expects more of its members to surrender to the government in the near future.
