1. ARAB "SUMMIT" COMMUNIQUE

The communiqué issued following the conclusion of the meetings of the four Arab chiefs of state in Cairo glosses over serious differences of opinion which arose during later stages of the talks. The statement gives some details on King Saud's report of his talks in the United States. The four leaders reaffirm, however, their "determination" to "abide by the policy of constructive neutrality." The remainder of the communiqué is a reiteration of Arab grievances against Israel and a protest against the actions of the British in Yemen and the French in Algeria.
