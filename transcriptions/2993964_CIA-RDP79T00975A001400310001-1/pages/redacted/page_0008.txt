10. Peru reportedly may not attend Caracas conference:

Unless a solution for the Haya de la Torre case can be found prior to the opening in Caracas on 1 March of the Tenth Inter-American Conference, Peru will probably refuse to attend, according to the American embassy in Lima.

Peru's refusal to date to grant Haya de la Torre a safe-conduct and thereby end his five-year asylum in the Colombian embassy in Lima has been motivated primarily by the ruling families' bitter hatred of the Peruvian opposition leader.
