3 OCTOBER 1959

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC
Chinese Communists persist in boosting communes despite lack of Soviet agreement.
Heavy air activity accompanies Soviet Pacific Fleet exercise.

II. ASIA-AFRICA
UAR continues to attack Communist China for permitting Bakdash speech in Peiping.
Laos--UN subcommittee continues limited survey work.
Pakistan--Retirement of army chief of staff points up friction within armed forces.
Japan--Kishi improves his position for gaining approval of revised Security Treaty.

III. THE WEST
Turkey--Relations between two major parties embittered by physical attacks on opposition deputies.
Cyprus--Makarios and Grivas will meet next week to discuss differences.
Guatemala--Anti-US and pro-Communist political forces gain strength.
