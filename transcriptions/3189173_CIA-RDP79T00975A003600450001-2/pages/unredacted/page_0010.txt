II. ASIA-AFRICA

Situation in Indonesia

Padang has remained under central government control since 17 April,
there had been a dissident counterattack on 20 April. Djakarta troops apparently are moving toward Bukittinggi, the last dissident stronghold in Sumatra, by way of Solok, and claim the capture of Indarung, a town on the road connecting Padang and Solok. The Padang airfield, which had been damaged by the dissidents before the invasion, has been repaired and is being used by government aircraft.
