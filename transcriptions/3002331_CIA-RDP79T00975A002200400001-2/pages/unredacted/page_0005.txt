2. RHEE TO EXPEL LEADING AMERICAN BUSINESSMEN

President Rhee informed the American embassy on 13 October that a number of leading American businessmen must be expelled from Korea "at least for a time" in order to "save face" for Korea in the taxation dispute. He asserted that the recent public attack by the local American Chamber of Commerce had "shamed Korea before the world" and that "someone must suffer." He requested the embassy to provide the names of the "troublemakers."
