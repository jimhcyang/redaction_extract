21 APRIL 1959

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC

Khrushchev convinced Western disunity can be exploited to gain acceptance of status quo in Eastern Europe, according to West German diplomat.

East German Government gives workers wage increases in further effort to improve production; previous wage hikes led to increased work norms.

II. ASIA-AFRICA

Further shake-ups in Sudanese Government likely; Soviet economic mission leaves Khartoum without concluding any major agreements.
