India - East Germany: Indian Foreign Ministry Secre-
tary Dutt believes India may be compelled to recognize East
Germany by the end of 1960. Although Prime Minister Nehru
has made it clear to East German representatives that he has
no intention of extending such recognition in the near future,
Dutt currently believes trade and political considerations may
force Nehru to change his position.
