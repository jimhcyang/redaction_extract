20 JANUARY 1958

Zhukov may receive new appointment soon.

USSR maneuvers against Baghdad Pact meeting.

French intercept Yugoslav freighter with arms for North Africa.

Declaration of Sumatran independence postponed.

Venezuelan President moves to strengthen control.

Guatemalan election results expected this afternoon.

Nasir wary of Egyptian-Syrian union.

Finland to join Western economic groups.

Moscow-Bonn trade talks to be resumed 30 January.
