7. Iraq offers plan to break Anglo-Egyptian impasse:
Iraq's Prime Minister, Nuri Said, has suggested a plan to break the current Anglo-Egyptian impasse.
At the UN General Assembly meetings in Paris, Nuri's plan apparently provides that each Middle Eastern country should have its own "regular troops" and should also seek "auxiliary troops" from other nations to supplement its own forces.
