3. FRENCH RESIDENT GENERAL ACCUSES SPAIN OF AIDING MOROCCAN REBELS

Resident General Boyer de Latour told Assistant Secretary of Defense Burgess on 12 October that Spain is permitting Moroccan rebels to obtain supplies and to fire on French troops from Spanish territory. According to the commanding general of the Fez region, the Spanish are also jamming French military radio communications.

Boyer de Latour said he would use artillery and planes against rebel positions in the Spanish zone if such aid continued.

The American consul general in Rabat believes that the French campaign is not going too well and says there is evidence of bickering among French commanders over responsibilities and operational methods.
