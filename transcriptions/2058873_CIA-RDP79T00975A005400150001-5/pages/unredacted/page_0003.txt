17 NOVEMBER 1960

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC

II. ASIA-AFRICA

USSR to provide Morocco with jet aircraft, reportedly within six weeks or a month.

Negotiations between Iraqi Government and IPC at an impasse, with some nationalization of present oil concession a possibility.

Chinese Nationalists reorganizing their irregulars in Burma.

III. THE WEST

Guatemala--Communists now trying to capitalize on the revolt by agitation in the capital; government forces recapture Puerto Barrios.

Bolivia sending mission to Europe to explore possibilities for Soviet and West European aid.

Denmark--Governing Social Democrats to face difficulties in reconstituting coalition government following general election, but no basic changes anticipated in foreign or defense policies.
