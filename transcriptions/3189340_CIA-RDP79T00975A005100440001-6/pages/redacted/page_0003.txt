DAILY BRIEF

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC

USSR-Ethiopia: The Soviet Union, departing from its usual policy of not extending cash loans to nonbloc countries, has deposited in a New York bank $2,000,000 to the account of the Ethiopian Government. The sum is to be debited against the Soviet $100,000,000 line of credit previously extended to Addis Ababa.
