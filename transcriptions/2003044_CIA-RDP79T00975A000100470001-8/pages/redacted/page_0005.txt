EASTERN EUROPE

5. USSR ships grain to Czechoslovakia in excess of Czech needs:

On 15 March, Czech President Klement Gottwald stated at a meeting of the Central Committee of the Czech Communist Party that the USSR will ship 155,000 metric tons of bread grain to Czechoslovakia before the end of May and 600,000 tons more before the next harvest.
