SECURITY INFORMATION

GENERAL
1. Spanish interests reportedly negotiating oil contract with Iran:

A Liechtenstein firm, the Tracosa Trading Company, Ltd., reportedly is acting as a go-between and "Huguenin," described as a watchmaker, has made an initial deposit of $175,000.

Comment: This is the first indication that Spanish interests are trying to buy Iranian oil. The contract may already be in the process of implementation,
a tanker had just left Italy for Abadan

As more tankers are now available and as the preliminary Italian and Japanese court decisions have been favorable, more oil may leave Iran.

SOVIET UNION
2. Moscow embassy reports rumor of Beria disgrace:

Ambassador Bohlen reports that a rumor has been circulating in Moscow to the effect that L. P. Beria is in difficulty if not in disgrace because of the recent security breakdown in East Germany.

Bohlen was informed of this rumor by a "very reliable diplomat who still has occasional Soviet contacts," but comments that it may have arisen solely from Beria's absence on 27 June when all other top Soviet officials attended the opera.
