NEAR EAST - AFRICA

4. Britain reportedly considering five-year agreement with Egypt:

The British Foreign Office is reportedly considering limiting the proposed Anglo-Egyptian defense agreement to five years. This limitation would be proposed in the hope that after five years Egypt would feel able to join a regional defense arrangement which would secure Western defense needs at the Suez base.
