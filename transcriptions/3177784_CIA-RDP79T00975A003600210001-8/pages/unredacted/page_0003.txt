25 MARCH 1958

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC  
Latest Soviet aide-memoire repeats USSR's agenda proposals, makes no new suggestions.

II. ASIA—AFRICA  
Djakarta Communists seek to incite antiforeign demonstrations; attack on US Embassy possible.  
King's brothers believed to hold upper hand in Saudi Arabia.  
Egyptian troop movements to Syria facilitate Nasir's consolidation of control over Syrian forces.

III. THE WEST  
France - De Gaulle's intentions remain inscrutable; British ambassador doubts he plans return to power.
