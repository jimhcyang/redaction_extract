30 JULY 1959

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC
USSR may be furnishing Afghanistan an additional $24,000,000 in military aid.
Pattern of North Vietnamese clandestine communications could indicate new terrorist incidents in South Vietnam.

II. ASIA-AFRICA
Laotian communique charges partisans attacking Sam Neua army posts are "well-organized units" under North Vietnamese command.
Indonesia--Nasution seeks to increase military participation in government.
Top Pakistani Government officials will move from Karachi to Rawalpindi this fall.
UAR investigating extent of Communist indoctrination of UAR students in bloc countries.
Soviet-Iranian diplomatic impasse continues.

III. THE WEST
French Air Force flies mission over Libya to check for Algerian rebel activity.

LATE ITEMS
India accepts Soviet aid for Third Five-Year Plan.
USSR--Probable ICBM successfully launched at Tyura Tam.
