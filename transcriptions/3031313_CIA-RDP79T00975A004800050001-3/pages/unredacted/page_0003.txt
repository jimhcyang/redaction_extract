6 NOVEMBER 1959

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC
Comment on Soviet position at nuclear test talks.
Poland--Recent developments suggest possible trend away from liberalization.

II. ASIA-AFRICA
Misleading impression gaining currency in Morocco that US is about to start evacuating its bases may have serious repercussions.
Pakistan--Ayub sets date for elections to local boards, first step toward return to "constitutional" government.
Thai--Cambodian relations worsening again.

III. THE WEST
Austria Government appears still determined to take South Tirol dispute to UN.
Peru--Anti-US sentiment being aroused over government controversy with an American oil company.
