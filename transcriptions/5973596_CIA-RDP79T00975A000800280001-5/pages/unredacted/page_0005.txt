4. Burmese have evidence of Chinese Nationalist-Karen cooperation:
the Karens have arrived at an "understanding" with the Chinese Nationalists and are exchanging food and tungsten for arms and ammunition.

Comment: This is the first indication that the Burmese Government has what it considers convincing evidence of Chinese Nationalist cooperation with the insurgent Karens. Chinese Nationalist cooperation with the insurgent Karens might prejudice US-Burmese relations because of the belief in Rangoon that the United States is involved with the Nationalists. The fears that Nationalist support of the Karens would seriously impede the government's campaign to suppress the Communist rebellion.
