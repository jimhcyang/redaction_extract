6. Mau Mau apparently receiving funds from British Communists:
The Kenya government has discovered the British Communist Party has supplied funds to the Mau Mau.

Comment: This is the first report of significant ties between the Mau Mau and the British Communist Party. Although previous reports had suggested the presence of a loosely organized group of card-carrying Communists in Kenya and the circulation of a mimeographed leaflet parroting the Communist line, local officials have maintained that they have been unable to establish any direct Mau Mau-Communist ties.
