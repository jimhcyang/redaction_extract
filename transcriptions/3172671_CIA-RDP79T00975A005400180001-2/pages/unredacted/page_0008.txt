Situation in the Congo

The UAR ambassador in the Congo is concerned about the increasing power of Colonel Mobutu and the "agents of imperialism."

The ambassador admitted that Mobutu's parade on 17 November was more successful and excited more crowd enthusiasm than had been expected.
