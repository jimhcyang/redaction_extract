29 JUNE 1960

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC
Bucharest communique does not resolve Sino-Soviet dispute, but conflict may be muted. (1)
Moscow designates Central Pacific impact area for tests of "multistage carrier rockets" in July. (2)
Peiping informs Katmandu that Chinese troops engaged in quelling rebel disturbances have been sent into demilitarized zone on Nepalese-Tibetan border. (3)

II. ASIA-AFRICA
Ceylonese prime minister's illness likely to damage conservative party's prospects in 20 July elections. (4)
Mozambique tribesmen attacking Portuguese administrative posts. (5)

III. THE WEST
British Labor party's "softer" stand on defense will not satisfy left-wing extremists. (6)
