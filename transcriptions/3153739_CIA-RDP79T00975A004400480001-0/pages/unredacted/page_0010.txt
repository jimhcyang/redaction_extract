The exiled tribal chiefs, however, who supported Premier Mossadeq before his overthrow in 1953, may have some capacity for creating trouble by cooperating with the nationalists. Nasr Khan, predominant among the exiled chiefs, claims that he recently received offers of Soviet arms to pursue his tribal property claims. While the estimated 300,000 Qashqais still hate the Shah and his family, they probably are no threat in themselves, but could cause unrest if they ally with other tribes.
