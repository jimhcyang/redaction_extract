26 JANUARY 1960

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC
Peiping issues inflated production claims in communiqué on 1959 economic results.

II. ASIA-AFRICA
Algeria--De Gaulle pledges restoration of order in Algiers; confronts local authorities with need for decision to use force against insurgent Frenchmen.
Tunisian President Bourguiba calls for French evacuation of Bizerte base; French position reportedly "not negotiable."
Indonesia--Rapid inflation continuing; may lead to unrest.

III. THE WEST
Cuba--National Bank head Che Guevara forbids rediscounting of crop loans by foreign banks; could be prelude to nationalization of the banking system.
