30 JUNE 1959

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC
Peiping charges US air intrusions over Paracel Islands; announces new "serious warnings".

II. ASIA-AFRICA
India--Communist administration in Kerala refusing to resign and submit to new elections.

III. THE WEST
Extreme rightist Grivas, former Cypriot EOKA leader, reported planning bid for political power in Greece.
Iceland--Communists suffer reverses; Conservative elements gain strength in 28 June elections.
Dominican Republic--Trujillo tells US ambassador he will not permit Dominican forces to attack Cuba.
