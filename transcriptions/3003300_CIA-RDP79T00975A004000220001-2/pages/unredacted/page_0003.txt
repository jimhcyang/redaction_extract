7 OCTOBER 1958

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC

Taiwan Strait — No Communist artillery fire against Chinmens since 5 October.

II. ASIA-AFRICA

Algerian agents to purchase more arms in Prague.

Iraq - Military mission scheduled for Moscow. Baathist cabinet member intends to resign as pressure on Prime Minister Qasim.

Syria - Action against American-owned oil company probably intended as pressure on Tapline.

Iran - Reported conspiracy aims to limit powers of Shah.

Tunisian-Egyptian relations continue difficult.

III. THE WEST

West Germany moves toward relations with East European satellites.
