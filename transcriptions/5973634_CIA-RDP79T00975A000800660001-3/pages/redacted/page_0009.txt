WESTERN EUROPE

9. Further reports on West German Socialist intention to compromise with West:

Erich Ollenhauer, the new party leader, is trying to open the door for a compromise with the Western Powers and for his party's participation in the Bonn government after next year's election. Though Ollenhauer promised to continue the policies of Kurt Schumacher, he is reported to have rejected conference attempts to fight "Americanism," and to have sidetracked demands for large-scale public opposition to EDC and the contractual agreements.
