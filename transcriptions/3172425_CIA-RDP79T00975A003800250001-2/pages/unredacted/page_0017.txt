The general populace of Saudi Arabia is overjoyed at the overthrow of the Iraqi Government. In Egypt there is a surface calm, but progovernment elements are worried over Anglo-American moves. In neighboring Libya the UK has sent a warship to Tobruk with a commando unit to protect and, if necessary, evacuate King Idriss should Nasir attempt a coup there.
