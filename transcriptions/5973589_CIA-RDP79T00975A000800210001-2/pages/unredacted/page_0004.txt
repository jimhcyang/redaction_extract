NEAR EAST - AFRICA

3. Turkish participation proposed in imminent Greek-Yugoslav military talks:

A Yugoslav military mission will arrive in Athens on 17 August, and that the Greeks have proposed that Turkey be included in the forthcoming talks.

Comment: Despite previous agreements, the Greeks had not been keeping the Turks informed of their negotiations with Yugoslavia. Turkey did not expect the Greek-Yugoslav meeting before the end of September.
