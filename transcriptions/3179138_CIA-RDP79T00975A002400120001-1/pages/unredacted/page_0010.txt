THE ARAB-ISRAELI SITUATION
(Information as of 1700, 2 February)

Increased Egyptian paratroop training has been observed recently. Formations of as many as eight C-46 aircraft have been observed participating in drop maneuvers and additional C-46's are being reconditioned.

Syrian chief of staff Shuqayr stated that Syria does not want war, but war will begin in Banat Yacov if the Israelis want it. Shuqayr also said he expected Egypt to reply by 1 February to Syria's request for anti-aircraft equipment. He said that if the Egyptians refused, he would authorize purchase of the equipment from Czechoslovakia.
