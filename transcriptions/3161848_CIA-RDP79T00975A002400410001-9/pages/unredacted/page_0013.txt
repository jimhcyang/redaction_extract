A Saudi ship from the Red Sea port of Jidda was expected to arrive at the village of Haqal on the Gulf of Aqaba no later than 15 March, carrying an unspecified number of troops and 200 tons of equipment.
The ship was to have been met by an advance party under two officers which had come overland with 18 vehicles. This move places a Saudi force within 20 miles of the Jordan border and the port of Aqaba, and continues the steady increase in Saudi-Egyptian forces in the Gulf of Aqaba area.
