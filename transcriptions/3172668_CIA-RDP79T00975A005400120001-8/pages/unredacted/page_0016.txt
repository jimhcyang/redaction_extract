The Cuban government was reliably reported last month to have provided the Guatemalan Communists and pro-Communists with financial support and the promise of arms aid for a revolution against Ydigoras.

While there is no evidence at this time that the 11 November rebel attacks in Nicaragua and the current revolt in Guatemala are coordinated moves directed from Cuba, the Castro regime is known to be deeply involved in Central American revolutionary plotting.
