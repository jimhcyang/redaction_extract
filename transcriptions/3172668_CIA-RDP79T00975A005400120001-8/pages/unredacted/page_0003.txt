14 NOVEMBER 1960

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC

II. ASIA-AFRICA
Situation in South Vietnam. (1)
Laos--Coup group in Luang Prabang establishing ties with Phoumi's committee in Savannakhet. (2)
Thailand--Discontented junior officers, apprehensive over moves to improve Thai-Soviet relations, allegedly planning coup against Premier Sarit. (3)
Turkey--Shake-up in ruling military group appears to have strengthened hand of moderate pro-Western elements. (4)

III. THE WEST
West Germany--Adenauer trying to improve relations with USSR but the prospect of a Bonn-Moscow "deal" is discounted. (5)
Venezuela--Betancourt to exclude pro-Castro URD from reorganized cabinet. (6)
Nicaragua--Claims that Castro regime directed recent rebel incursion from Costa Rica remain unconfirmed; government expects attack from the north. (7)
Guatemalan insurgents attack key military installations; regime decrees state of siege throughout country. (8)
