27 JULY 1959

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC
USSR may mark time at Geneva pending outcome of Nixon-Khrushchev talks.
Soviet ICBM test on 25 Jul fails in flight.

II. ASIA-AFRICA
Chinese Nationalists anxious to resume escorted reconnaissance of mainland opposite Taiwan.
UAR remains conciliatory toward Iraq in public while strengthening covert contacts with anti-Qasim nationalists.
Ethiopian Foreign Ministry believes Emperor's visits to Cairo, Moscow, and Paris won added support on Somali issues.

III. THE WEST
France--Debré considering military proposal to smash Algerian rebel bases in Tunisia.
West Germany adamantly opposed to Western acceptance of any all-German commission proposal at Geneva.
Belgian Finance Minister says Brussels cannot comply with NATO minimum force requirements.

LATE ITEM
A new valid countdown in process at Tyura Tam.
