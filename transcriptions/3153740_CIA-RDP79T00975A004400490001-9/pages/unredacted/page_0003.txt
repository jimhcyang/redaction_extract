9 MAY 1959

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC

USSR planning to raise procedural
problems at opening of foreign min-
isters’ conference.

USSR might attempt to launch space
vehicle toward Venus in early June.

II. ASIA-AFRICA

Algerian rebels seek to counter mil-
itary setbacks by embroiling Tunisians
with French Army.

Qasim may be planning federal system
for Iraqi minorities.

Thailand plans Red Cross talks with
North Vietnam on repatriation of
Vietnamese refugees.

Laotian officials oppose continued
French control of Laotian army train-
ing.

South Korean Coast Guard attempts
seizure of Japanese fishing craft
outside “Rhee line.”

Chiang Kai-shek will retain
presidency beyond expiration of sec-
ond term next year.
