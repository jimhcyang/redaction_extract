FAR EAST

3. Burmese Premier may resign:

Burmese Premier Nu "agreed" to remain in office for one month to give his colleagues an opportunity to choose a successor, although Nu may only have been testing his strength, he might retire if anyone but Defense Minister Ba Swe were selected.

Comment: This is the second recent report that Nu may be actually considering retirement, but he has threatened to resign several times since assuming office in 1948.
