1 DECEMBER 1960

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC

Communist China--Completion of 1961 trade plans with bloc partners further delayed; could reflect economic difficulties or Chinese unwillingness to make commitments pending outcome of Moscow Communist conference.

Communist China signs large-scale economic cooperation pact with Cuba.

USSR apparently seeking increase in trade and cultural exchanges with Thailand.

II. ASIA-AFRICA

The situation in the Congo.

LATE ITEM

Earth satellite vehicle launched from Tyura Tam under circumstances which suggest recovery attempt will be made.
