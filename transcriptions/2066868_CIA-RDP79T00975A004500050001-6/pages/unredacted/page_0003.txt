16 MAY 1959

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC
Khrushchev continues to show irritation over Nasir; Cairo informed of Soviet leader's latest criticism.

II. ASIA-AFRICA
Nasir not hopeful of UAR rapprochement with Jordan and Tunisia.
Yemen seeking improved relations with Saudi Arabia.
Thai and South Vietnamese officials to hold mid-May meeting to plan covert operations against Cambodia.
South Korea's allegations that US supports "Rhee line" reported damaging American interests in Japan.

III. THE WEST
De Gaulle indifferent to advice of French officials that he permit US atomic storage sites in France.
Argentina - Economic stabilization program encountering intense opposition; Frondizi reorganizes cabinet.
