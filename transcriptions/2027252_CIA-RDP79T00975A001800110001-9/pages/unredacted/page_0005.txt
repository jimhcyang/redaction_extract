4. Withdrawal of Soviet jet fighter units from Yalu area indicated:

Comment: Soviet jet fighter units stationed near the Yalu supplied most of the Communist air effort during the Korean war. At least ten Soviet regiments with approximately 400 fighters were based in this area. During the summer and fall of 1953, three of these regiments were apparently deactivated, and their aircraft presumably turned over to Chinese Communist or North Korean units.

The current moves follow news of American air force withdrawals from Korea, and bear out previous indications that the Communists do not anticipate a resumption of hostilities in the Korean theater.
