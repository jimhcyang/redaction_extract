22 OCTOBER 1959

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC
Chou En-lai hints economic retaliation over Indonesian plan to force Overseas Chinese merchants out of rural areas.

II. ASIA-AFRICA
Laos--Government to try imprisoned pro-Communist leaders; Hammarskjold believes Moscow will abide by establishment of UN "presence" in Laos.

III. THE WEST
Cuba--Military commander of Camaguey Province arrested and charged with treason.
