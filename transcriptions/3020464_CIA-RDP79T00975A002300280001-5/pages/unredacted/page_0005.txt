Comment
The 1956 budget is scheduled to be presented in the Supreme Soviet session beginning on 23 December. Directives for the sixth Five-Year Plan are on the agenda of the 20th Party Congress which will be convened on 14 February 1956.

Maintenance of published military expenditures at the 1955 rate would keep them at a postwar and peacetime high despite the announced intention to reduce troop strength by 640,000 men. (Prepared by ORR)
