11 SEPTEMBER 1959

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC
USSR tries to dissociate Moscow
from Laotian and Indian border
situation in order to preserve fa-
vorable climate for Khrushchev
visit.
Chinese Nationalist artillery on
Chinmen to return Communist fire;
unusual Communist military com-
munications in Taiwan Strait area
continue.

II. ASIA-AFRICA
Laos--Government sources continue
to report Communist attacks.
Corruption in Indonesian Army head-
quarters may be leading to government
crisis.

III. THE WEST
Guatemala--Rightist coup plotting may
lead to showdown with government.
Bolivian President threatens resigna-
tion.
