Khrushchev's Bucharest speech reaffirms detente policy, criticizes Peiping stand. (1)

Libyan prime minister says he will need further US concessions to satisfy critics of US base rights. (2)
Terrorists apparently attempting to disrupt Cameroun's economy. (3)

III. THE WEST
Finland--Formation of majority center government believed imminent. (4)
