GENERAL
1. Rumania plans to send bearings to China:

Comment: Rumanian imports a large proportion of its own bearing requirements. These export plans and recent Bulgarian shipments to China of items which Bulgaria also imports, such as machinery and electrical equipment, demonstrate that even these less-developed European Satellites are being called upon to furnish a share of the industrial goods required for the Chinese industrialization program.

FAR EAST
2. Communist pilots in Korea demonstrate high degree of proficiency:
Communist pilots during the week ending 28 December showed an unusually high degree of proficiency, according to the Far East Air Force. Although avoiding engagements with UN F-86's, the Communists displayed an unusual variety of tactics designed to position themselves for brief firing passes.

This departure from the pattern of the previous six weeks suggests that more experienced pilots are again over Korea for additional combat training. FEAF believes that well-trained units are experimenting with tactics to develop an intercept pattern against F-86's, or aircraft of comparable performance.
