Comment  Molotovsk has been considered the most likely site for the construction of the USSR's first nuclear-powered submarine. It has convenient air and rail communications with Moscow and Leningrad, the locations of the principal design bureaus, research institutes, and administrative offices which would support such a project.

The current estimate on the Soviet nuclear energy program accords the USSR the capability of having a reactor suitable for a submarine in 1957. The growing volume of evidence indicates that the USSR either has an atomic submarine or will complete one very soon.
