23 NOVEMBER 1960

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC

II. ASIA-AFRICA
Situation in Laos.
The Congo--Lumumba adherents reportedly losing ground in Stanleyville.
Gabon--President Mba survives plot against his pro-West regime.

III. THE WEST
Cuban foreign exchange reserves reportedly exhausted, with no normal replenishment in sight before January.
Switzerland reportedly planning to counter Chinese Communist harassment of Swiss nationals by imposing restrictions on Chinese activities in Switzerland.
