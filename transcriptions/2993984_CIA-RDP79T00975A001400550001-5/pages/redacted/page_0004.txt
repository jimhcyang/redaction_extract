4. Comment on General Navarre's statement in Saigon:

General Navarre's statement to the press in Saigon on 19 February that his forces face a stalemate and he does not expect to be able to inflict decisive defeat on the enemy next year appears to be a public acknowledgment that the Navarre plan, as originally conceived, has been abandoned. The plan called for the build-up of French Union forces and the clearing of rear areas this year in the expectation that in 1955 the back of the Viet Minh resistance could be broken.
