18 JULY 1959

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC
Chinese Communist leaders may be meeting outside Peiping to review basic policy.
Floods in India and Pakistan give bloc nations opportunity to offer disaster relief.

II. ASIA-AFRICA
Moroccans introducing new customs procedures as additional pressure tactic toward ultimate US evacuation.
Large-scale Algerian rebel operation may give French a pretext for pursuit in force into Tunisia.
West African "summit" meeting marked by differences over nature of any West African union.
Iraq--New outbreak of violence in Kirkuk marks 14 July celebration.
Sudanese military government facing restiveness among junior officers and new pressures for return to civil government.

III. THE WEST
Ouster of Cuban President opens way for Communist gains
