USSR: Moscow is continuing its efforts to acquire large oil tankers to transport rising Soviet petroleum exports. Since the USSR embarked on its tanker procurement program in the latter half of 1960, more than 20 tankers have been ordered from the Netherlands, Japan, Italy, West Germany, and Yugoslavia. Delivery of these tankers will raise the tonnage of the Soviet tanker fleet from about 990,000 in mid-1960 to at least 1,580,000 tons. (Backup, Page 5)
