LATIN AMERICA

6. Chilean Communists may attempt demonstrations:

The Communist Party of Chile will reportedly attempt a demonstration against US Assistant Secretary Miller when he arrives in Santiago to attend the UN ECOSOC meeting now in session.

Comment: Assistant Secretary Miller is scheduled to be in Santiago March 5-7. The Communists can be expected to use his visit as a springboard for propaganda and agitation against the coming Inter-American Meeting of Foreign Ministers and in support of any Soviet propaganda condemning “US exploitation of Latin America” at the current UN ECOSOC meeting. Continuing labor unrest in Chile favors Communist agitation at this time, A Communist demonstration denouncing the US and French roles in ECOSOC occurred on 22 February and was promptly suppressed.
