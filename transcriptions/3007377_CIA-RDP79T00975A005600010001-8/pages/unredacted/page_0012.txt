France has reacted to this situation with moderation in an effort to preserve as much as possible of its former position and to avoid providing any additional ammunition to pro-bloc extremist elements in the regime. However, it has recently requested a written understanding as to the fields in which the Mali Government is willing to cooperate. Mali's moderate President Modibo Keita's response was to make such an understanding contingent upon a prior agreement providing for the evacuation of the bases--a demand France has not yet moved to satisfy because of concern over possible attempts to aid the Algerian rebels via Mali.
