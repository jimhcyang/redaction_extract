FAR EAST

3. Major problem with South Korea seen unless Geneva talks are completely terminated:

Anything short of complete and unconditional termination of the Geneva talks will create a major problem in United States-South Korean relations, Ambassador Briggs reported on 1 June. He said that Rhee desires a clean break at Geneva, with "no leftovers gathering mould in the UN icebox." Anything Rhee interprets as temporizing or appeasement increases the possibility that he will publicly denounce the talks and withdraw his delegation from Geneva.

Briggs also said that all of Rhee's views, including his belief that he is being excluded from the forthcoming Washington military talks, stem from his conviction that time is running out.
