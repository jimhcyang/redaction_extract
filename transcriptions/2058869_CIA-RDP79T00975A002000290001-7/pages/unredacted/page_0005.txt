SOUTHEAST ASIA

4. Strong pro-Diem sentiment reported in former Viet Minh area:

An informal survey taken early this month revealed widespread pro-Diem sentiment in the area of central Vietnam now being evacuated by the Viet Minh. Those interviewed knew that Diem led the national government even though they knew nothing else about it. Comments about the premier were favorable. For these people, Diem personified one side, Ho Chi Minh the other.

In three different towns recently liberated from eight years of Viet Minh control, thousands of persons gathered in apparently spontaneous demonstrations on 2 and 3 May. Banners called on the French to go home and asked for full support for Diem and the abdication of Bao Dai.
