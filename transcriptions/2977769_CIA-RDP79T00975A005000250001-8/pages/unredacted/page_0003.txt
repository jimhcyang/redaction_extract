29 MARCH 1960

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC

Khrushchev offers "nothing new" so far
in talks with De Gaulle.

II. ASIA-AFRICA

New Delhi continues its support to Tibetan
resistance leaders in India.

Indonesia--Communist influence enlarged
in new parliament appointed by Sukarno;
some military and civilian elements oppose it.

South African official says union will
leave UN if riots issue is inscribed on
Security Council agenda.

New political grouping in Kenya fore-
shadows increased pressure for inde-
pendence.

British Somaliland--Local government to
demand independence for 1 July.

Other French African areas likely to
follow up quickly on Madagascar's lead
for independence.
