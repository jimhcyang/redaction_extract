I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC

II. ASIA-AFRICA

Indian Government Continues to Support Tibetan
Resistance Movement

Nehru reportedly conveyed to the Dalai Lama recently his regret that India was unable to provide greater political support for the refugee Tibetan leaders.

Nehru also promised that New Delhi's rehabilitation program for Tibetan refugees in India would be continued, although perhaps on a smaller scale.
