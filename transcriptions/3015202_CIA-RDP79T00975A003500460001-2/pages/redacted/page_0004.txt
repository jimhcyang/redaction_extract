II. ASIA-AFRICA

The National Bank of Egypt has apparently been offered a Soviet loan at current banking interest rates to provide foreign exchange up to the equivalent of about $15,000,000. Despite its foreign exchange shortages, no decision has been taken by Cairo, probably because it is still hopeful that a financial settlement can be reached with the British in the talks scheduled to begin on 30 January.
