9 JULY 1959

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC
1. Peiping announces new program for "reform" in Tibet; Dalai Lama considering taking his case to UN.
2. Peiping apparently exaggerating bad weather and floods; may be seeking excuse for likely failure to reach crop goals.

II. ASIA-AFRICA
3. De Gaulle informs Tunisia France will not give up Bizerte base.
4. Morocco confronted by financial problem which may affect French-Moroccan relations.
5. Comment on new government in Nepal.

III. THE WEST
6. Castro thinking of relinquishing post as prime minister to direct agricultural reform; he would remain Cuba's dominant figure.
7. Trinidad--Anti-US premier stepping up campaign for revision of US-UK base rights.

LATE ITEM
8. South Vietnam--Two Americans killed in attack on MAAG post.
