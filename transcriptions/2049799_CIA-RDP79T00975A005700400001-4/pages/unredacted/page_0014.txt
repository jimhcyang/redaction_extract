Bloc airlift operations for 8 June involved 4 sorties, 3 of which were into Laos. Preliminary field information indicates that a relatively normal level of operations was scheduled for 9 June. The IL-14 that flew to Hanoi from Moscow on 5 June and returned to Irkutsk on 8 June has been scheduled for another flight to Hanoi from Irkutsk on 9 June.
