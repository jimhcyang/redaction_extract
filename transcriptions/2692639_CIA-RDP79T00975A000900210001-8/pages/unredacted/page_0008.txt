The only potential Communist striking force in Austria, the Werkschuetz or factory guard, is now estimated to number 3,000 men armed with small weapons. Neither the Werkschuetz nor the Communist youth "activists," however, are believed to have received military training.

Comment: Despite the Communist sympathizers in the police and the potential availability of the Werkschuetz, there is no evidence of any serious deterioration in the security situation in Vienna.

Outside Vienna, the most serious security problem is the poor logistical support of the Austrian gendarmerie and Soviet domination of the border guards along the eastern frontiers.
