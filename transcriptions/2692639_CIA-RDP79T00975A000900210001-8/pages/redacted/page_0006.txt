"British administration in the Sudan would make a grievous error if they fought this agreement because the whole of the Sudan would turn against them."

Comment: The Egyptian Government on 2 November announced that the several parties in the Sudan which advocated union with Egypt had merged into a single party which would endorse the Egyptian proposal. The strong endorsement by the pro-British Sudanese leaders who have opposed union with Egypt will put pressure on Britain to include at least some of the Egyptian proposals in the new constitution.
