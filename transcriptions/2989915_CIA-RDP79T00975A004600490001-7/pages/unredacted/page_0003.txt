26 AUGUST 1959

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC
USSR--Latvian deputy premier fired in latest shake-up reflecting Moscow's increasing concern with nationalist and localist tendencies.
Chou En-lai reaffirms resumption of Chinese Communist drive for economic achievement; secret party meetings may still be under way, possibly on military problems.

II. ASIA-AFRICA
American officials in Laos assess capabilities of Communist forces in Laos.
South Vietnamese elections expected to result in government victory, but may be marked by incidents of terrorism.
Cameroun--Terrorism increasing; security forces capture Czech-made arms.
