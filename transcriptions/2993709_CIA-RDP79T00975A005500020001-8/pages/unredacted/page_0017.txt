It is not known whether the guard houses erected on the Berlin sector borders to enforce this law are to be dismantled.

In view of the highly legalistic bases which both East and West use to justify their present positions in Berlin, the actual scope of the reported concessions may be less than Bonn claims. In addition, the East Germans were apparently successful in insisting that their concessions would remain in effect only if the strictest secrecy were maintained. This provision will enable East Germany to renege on the agreement almost at will, since some publicity is probably unavoidable.
