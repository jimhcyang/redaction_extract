I. THE WEST

Cuba: Fidel Castro's experience at the United Nations has apparently strengthened his view of himself as a leader of the peoples of all underdeveloped countries in their "struggle against imperialism." He evidently plans to return to the UN. Upon his 28 September departure from New York he said he would return in a few weeks; New York hotel officials reported on 3 October that Cuba is seeking reservations beginning 23 October for a delegation that may be headed either by Fidel or Raul Castro.
