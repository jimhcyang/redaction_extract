I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC

Tito Again Attempting to Influence Satellites

Tito is renewing his efforts to influence developments within the Eastern European satellites through closer party relations. He is counting particularly on expanding existing relations with Poland and Rumania. Although Belgrade is extremely skeptical of recent Hungarian approaches, it will continue to encourage them.

Aware that his past crusading in the satellites was offensive to Moscow, Tito probably does not contemplate any accompanying campaign in the press and other propaganda media. Belgrade is dissatisfied with the trend of events in the bloc, but foresees no increased tensions between Moscow and Belgrade. Both Belgrade and Moscow are trying to avoid any public discussion of their disagreements.
