7 MAY 1959

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC

Chinese Communist rebuttal of Nehru's remarks about Tibet hints at desire to improve Sino-Indian relations; further exchanges likely.

Soviet note charging Kishi with wanting to involve Japan in nuclear arms race is aimed at influencing June elections for Japanese upper house.

Gromyko makes strong statement to Iranian ambassador on Iranian-Soviet relations.

II. ASIA-AFRICA

Iraqi Embassy in Moscow empowered to issue visas to bloc personnel without Baghdad.

Iraqi security forces with aid from irregulars regaining control of tribal areas near Turkish and Iranian borders.

West Germans to build pipeline and terminal facilities for Iran from Abadan to Persian Gulf.

Arms en route to Algerian rebels believed to have come from Saudi Arabia.

III. THE WEST

British Icelandic fishing dispute aggravated by more incidents.

Nicaragua - Exile groups still planning action against Somoza regime.
