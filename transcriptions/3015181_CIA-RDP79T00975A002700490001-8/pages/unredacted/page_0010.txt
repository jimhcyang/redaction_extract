Rapid Egyptian response to Jordan's appeal for military aid following the Israeli raid resulted in delivery of 21 tons of arms by air and 60 tons on the following day,

the arrival of five Egyptian transport aircraft, described in the press as IL-14's, on the 28th, and an additional eight aircraft were expected later. three planeloads of Egyptian arms were delivered to Amman, and a shipload of undisclosed size was dispatched to Jordan via the Syrian port of Latakia. In addition, Syria is attempting to expedite delivery of $1,300,000 worth of arms to Jordan from Czechoslovakia.
