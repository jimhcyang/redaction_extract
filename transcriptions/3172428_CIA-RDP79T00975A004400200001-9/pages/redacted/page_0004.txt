Greece-Yugoslavia: (In an apparent shift in tactics, Greek leaders now seem to favor holding early bilateral military talks with the Yugoslavs, who recently repeated their earlier invitation to General Dovas, chief of the Greek General Staff, to come to Belgrade for such talks. While such meetings would not be held under the aegis of the Balkan Pact, they might lay the groundwork for its early reactivation.) (SECRET NO FURTHER DISSEMINATION) (Page 2)
