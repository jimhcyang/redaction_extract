SOUTHEAST ASIA

3. Maintenance of Viet Minh effort in Laos is indicated:
the Viet Minh may be continuing to ship military supplies to Laos.

Comment: A likely destination for these arms is the Xieng Quang area in eastern Laos, where elements of the Viet Minh 304th Division have been reported digging in around French defenses. Minister Letourneau recently stated that the principal effort of the French command over the next "weeks and months" must be to prevent consolidation of the Viet Minh hold over the areas recently occupied.
