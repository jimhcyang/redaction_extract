The new cabinet line-up includes representatives of most major factions, but the relative absence of "top" names suggests that an enduring coalition of party factions will depend on the outcome of the elections. The new foreign minister, Zentaro Kosaka, and trade minister, Mitsujiro Ishii, are supporters of close ties with the United States.
