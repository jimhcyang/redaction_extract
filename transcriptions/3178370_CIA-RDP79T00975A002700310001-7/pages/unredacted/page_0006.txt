4. USSR SEEKS POSITION REPORTS FROM ITS MERCHANT SHIPS

On 13 September, the deputy head of the Black Sea State Steamship Agency at Odessa, which controls dry cargo vessels sailing from the Black Sea to foreign ports, ordered all ships to report their position immediately. Such special orders are normally issued only in cases of emergency. The order probably reflects a desire to control the movements of all Soviet merchant ships in the Suez area to assure their safety and noninvolvement in the explosive Suez situation. The immediate occasion for this action is probably the announced intention of the majority of non-Egyptian Suez pilots to terminate their duties this week end.
