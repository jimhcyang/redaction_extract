SOUTHEAST ASIA

2. Sino-Viet Minh propaganda on Indochina becomes more belligerent:

Chinese Communist and Viet Minh propaganda on developments in Indochina has become more ominous in the past few days. The United States is accused by Peiping of creating a "serious threat" by obstructing implementation of the Geneva accords, masterminding the Saigon riots, and of "sparing no effort to dismember Vietnam and turn South Vietnam into an American military base." Peace in Indochina, therefore, is facing a "crisis," this propaganda asserts, and the United States "will be responsible for the grave consequences."
