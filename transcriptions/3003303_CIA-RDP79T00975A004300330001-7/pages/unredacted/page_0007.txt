LATE ITEM

Thailand-Cambodia: Thailand and Cambodia have agreed to resume diplomatic relations at the ambassadorial level beginning 20 February.

The agreement, to be announced in a joint communique on 6 February, was effected through the good offices of UN mediator Beck-Friis.

Cambodian Premier Sihanouk had broken off relations with Thailand last November out of pique over anti-Cambodian commentary in the Bangkok press.
