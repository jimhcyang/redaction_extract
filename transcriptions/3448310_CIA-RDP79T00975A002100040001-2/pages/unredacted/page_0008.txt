Comment: Runways at two fields--Nantai and Lungtien--near Foochow are now considered serviceable. Foochow will probably direct future operations at these two fields and at Chenghai near Swatow, which is also considered serviceable.

The latest photo reconnaissance suggests that runways at two fields near Amoy--at Chingyang and Lungchi, which may also be controlled by Foochow, will be completed soon.

Aircraft have not yet been spotted at any of the five fields.
