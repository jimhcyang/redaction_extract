While Phoui and Souvanna are the most obvious candidates for premier, the CDNI and the King are reported opposed to both and appear committed to Tiao Somsanith, the widely respected interior minister in the caretaker government. Souvanna and Phoui have thus far shown no sign of willingness to stand aside in favor of Somsanith or any other compromise premier.
