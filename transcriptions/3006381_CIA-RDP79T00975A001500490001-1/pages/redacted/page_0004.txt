SOUTHEAST ASIA

4. Comment on fall of Dien Bien Phu:

The French have lost approximately 16,000 men in the fighting at Dien Bien Phu, a large proportion of them first-quality colonial and Foreign Legion troops. The Viet Minh's casualties are estimated at more than 18,000 killed and wounded.
