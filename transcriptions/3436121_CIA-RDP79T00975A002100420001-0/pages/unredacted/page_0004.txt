Although earlier plans called for different armed services to assault each of the five commission compounds in South Korea on Liberation Day, 15 August, the army and navy chiefs of staffs have indicated that they would not approve the use of force in such a venture. Police-inspired demonstrations by civilians appear likely to continue, however. Pusan is particularly liable to new violence because of the presence there of a paramilitary force completely loyal to Rhee--General Won Yong-tok's Provost Marshal General Command.
