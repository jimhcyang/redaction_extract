Nicaraguan Rebels Seek Cuban Assistance

Nicaraguan rebel leaders in exile are turning in desperation to Cuban Prime Minister Castro for assistance following the failure early this month of the invasion of Nicaragua by political moderates based in Costa Rica. Castro has promised $100,000 to $150,000 and "a large number of Cuban troops to fight in Nicaragua," according to a rebel emissary who returned to Costa Rica on 15 June from a hurried trip to Cuba.
