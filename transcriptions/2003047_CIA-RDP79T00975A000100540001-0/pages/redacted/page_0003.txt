2. Negotiations for defense of Iceland being finalized:

Iceland's Foreign Minister has presented a draft of the US-Iceland defense agreement which, in the opinion of the US Legation in Reykjavik, leaves no important points unresolved. The Foreign Minister reportedly hopes to obtain speedy approval of the draft by the Cabinet and by party leaders.
