SOVIET UNION

2. Soviet Third Long Range Air Army transports fly to Chukotsk area:

at least 12 transports of the Third Long Range Air Army, believed to be based in the southern part of the Maritime Military District, departed for the Chukotsk area on 12 June. the northward progress of the operation from Magadan to Markovo on 18 June; on 19 June all 12 were engaged in shuttle flights between Markovo and Tanyurer.

Comment: The Soviet Union has been steadily developing the military and economic potential of the Chukotsk area since the war.

During April of this year an air shuttle operation involving an unprecedented number of aircraft occurred along this route with the apparent destination of Povarnya Krestik. The present activity, and the earlier airlift, may be connected with a unit move into the Chukotsk area or support of some unidentified economic activity in the area. (See map on page 4)
