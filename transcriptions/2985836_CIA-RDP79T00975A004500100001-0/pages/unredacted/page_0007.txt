Soviet Submarine Operations

A relatively high number of Soviet submarines continue operations outside of their local fleet areas.

Some submarines of the Northern Fleet detachment, which is believed to be unusually large, are probably deployed in the north-central Atlantic. Additional submarines of this detachment are also probably operating in the northeast Atlantic and it is possible that others are in the southern Norwegian Sea and/or off the northern tip of Norway.

More than one submarine is believed to be conducting out-of-area operations in the Pacific and one Albanian-based Soviet submarine apparently continues to operate in the Mediterranean.
