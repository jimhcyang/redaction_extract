SOUTHEAST ASIA

5. Vietnamese government crisis still in balance:

Ambassador Heath in Saigon reported on 13 September that tension between Premier Diem and General Hinh seemed to be relaxing and that the premier appeared to have won the first round. Diem's hand had been strengthened by a telegram from Bao Dai supporting the premier's order that Hinh "take leave." Moreover, General Ely told Heath that he agreed on the desirability of Hinh's departure for the time being.
