1. Finnish vessel reportedly delivering tanks to Communist China:

while in Gdynia he observed trucks, trailers and tanks being loaded aboard the Finnish vessel Korsholma for China.

Comment: This is the second recent report that Finnish vessels under Satellite charter are delivering arms to Communist China. In October the Herakles was observed unloading six-inch guns from Gdynia in Canton's port of Whampoa. The Korsholma left Suez on 30 October for China.

In most cases the terms of Finnish charters to the bloc exclude transport of arms and ammunition, but some shipowners are willing to overlook the charter limitations in view of the profitable nature of this trade.

SOVIET UNION

2. Comment on 24 November Pravda article:

As a possible forerunner of a new Soviet propaganda emphasis, a 24 November Pravda article points to "normal relations" and "cooperation" with the United States as a major condition for easing international tension. The timing suggests it may be intended to encourage British and French pressure at Bermuda for a more conciliatory attitude regarding the Soviet position on such issues as China's admission to the United Nations.

Consistent with long-standing propaganda, however, the article alleges that Secretary Dulles is not interested in normalizing relations and maintains that "the activity of subverting peaceful states is inseparably bound to the aggressive policy of US ruling circles."
