SOUTHEAST ASIA

3. Viet Minh may intend thrust into Laos:

The concentrating of Viet Minh regular forces in the Vinh area of northern Annam as a result of current troop movements places the enemy in an excellent position to move southwestward into Laos to threaten French communications with Saigon along the Mekong River, according to the US army attache in Saigon (see map, p. 5).

One regiment of the 304th Division has already reportedly moved southward from Thanh Hoa to join the 325th Division at Vinh, while a regiment and the headquarters element of the 320th Division have moved from Phu Nho Quan to Thanh Hoa.

The southward movement of elements of the 304th and 320th Divisions may reflect a reallocation of defensive responsibilities in the Thanh Hoa-Vinh base area, which would become necessary if the 325th Division were committed to offensive action in Laos.
