GENERAL

2. Italy and Japan agree to end state of war:
Italy sent a note to Japan with the approval of the US, the UK and France, proposing that the state of war between the two countries be terminated, and that outstanding questions shall be negotiated in accordance with the principles of the Japanese peace treaty. The Japanese Government has agreed. Instruments of ratification will not be exchanged until the Japanese peace treaty comes into force. Italy is anxious that its agreement with Japan be announced simultaneously with the signature of the multilateral treaty.
