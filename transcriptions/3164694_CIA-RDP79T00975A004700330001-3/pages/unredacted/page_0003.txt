8 OCTOBER 1959

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC

II. ASIA-AFRICA

Sihanouk tells Cambodians to choose between him and dissident exiles in "referendum."

Rebel harassment continues in Laos.

Ceylonese prime minister tells US ambassador he will follow moderate course; regrets closing of British bases.

Lebanon--President Shihab forms new, expanded cabinet.

LATE ITEM

Baghdad quiet under increased security measures after assassination attempt on Qasim.
