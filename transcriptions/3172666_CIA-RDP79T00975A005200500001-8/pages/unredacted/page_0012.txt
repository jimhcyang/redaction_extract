The American army attaché reports that, except for scattered Moroccan units, only the Irish UN contingent in Kivu Province has established effective control in its area.

According to a recent reliable report, Soviet officials are providing funds for the permanent housing of the Congolese UN delegation in New York. This is in line with previous information on private assistance--for example, the USSR paid Lumumba's hotel bill during his early August stopover in New York--which in addition to overt announced aid indicates a Soviet effort to involve Congolese leaders in a personal obligation toward Moscow.
