GENERAL

2. USSR reportedly assures Egypt of veto on Suez resolution:

Soviet UN delegate Vyshinsky reportedly has assured the Egyptian UN delegation that the USSR would veto any Suez resolution which "failed to uphold Egypt's dignity".

Comment: A Soviet veto of the proposed resolution concerning Egyptian shipping restrictions would be consistent with Moscow's contention that Suez should come under Egyptian sovereignty, and would be the second "pro-Arab" veto this year.

In 1951, the USSR abstained on the Security Council resolution calling on Egypt to terminate restrictions on shipping.
