Ex-premier and Socialist leader Guy Mollet has been regarded as a possible replacement for Bourges-Maunoury since the time the latter took office less than four months ago. Another prospective candidate is ex-Gaullist and former governor of Algeria Jacques Soustelle who played a major role in rallying rightist opposition to the Algerian statute. Ex-premier Rene Pleven and Education Minister Rene Billeres, both representatives of center parties which might become the nucleus of a compromise coalition, have also been mentioned.
