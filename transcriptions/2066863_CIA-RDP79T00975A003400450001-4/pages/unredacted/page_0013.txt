10. SAUD WARY OF NASIR AS CROWN PRINCE VISITS CAIRO

Comment on:

Nasir has invited Saudi Crown Prince Faysal to visit Cairo on his way home from the United States.

The visit could be beneficial if it contributed to an understanding with the Egyptians, or “at least to their refraining from troubling us either by statement or insinuation.”

Cairo radio continued to misrepresent Saudi policy.

Crown Prince Faysal is expected to arrive in Cairo on 1 January for a week following five months’ visit in the United States for medical treatment.

The Saudi envoy in Cairo, in reporting Nasir’s invitation early in November, said that Nasir had expressed pessimism about the prospect for improving relations with Saudi Arabia as long as King Saud was “bound to the policy” of Lebanese President Chamoun, King Hussayn of Jordan, “and others who carry out imperialist policy.” Nasir will attempt to undercut the king by trying to sway key Saudis such as Faysal who may be somewhat inclined to consider Egypt’s viewpoint.
