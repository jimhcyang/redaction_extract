NEAR EAST - AFRICA

4. Nasr counting on extensive American aid:

Egyptian prime minister Nasr is counting on receiving at least $50,000,000 in American economic aid this year and an equal amount of military aid,

Comment: According to present allocations, Egypt will be offered $20,000,000 in economic assistance and an equal amount of military aid.

Nasr's disappointment in the extent of American aid, after two years of anticipation, together with extremist opposition to the Suez settlement, may lead him again to threaten to adopt a neutralist position as a lever in bargaining with the West.
