WESTERN EUROPE

9. Large antibiotic transshipments made through Switzerland:

Approximately 7,500 metric tons of streptomycin were transshipped to Prague through a Swiss firm during 1951, according to the British Consul in Basel. About 20 percent of this quantity reportedly came to Switzerland through Israel. The Consul states that the shipments were sent to Prague via Swissair and KLM.
