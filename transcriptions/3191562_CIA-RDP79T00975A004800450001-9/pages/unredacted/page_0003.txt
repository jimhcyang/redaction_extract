23 DECEMBER 1959

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC
USSR and Communist China take divergent lines in commemorating Stalin anniversary.

II. ASIA-AFRICA
Afghan Foreign Minister decides to cancel a meeting with Pakistani president.
Burma--U Nu's prospects for winning February elections improve; army decides on nonpartisan role.
Lower Congo nationalists intensify demands for early independence from Belgium.

III. THE WEST
UAR balks at restoration of full diplomatic relations with France.

LATE ITEM
Laos--Reformist group (CDNI) allegedly plans to demand resignation of Phoui government and, failing that, to stage coup on 26 December with army support.
