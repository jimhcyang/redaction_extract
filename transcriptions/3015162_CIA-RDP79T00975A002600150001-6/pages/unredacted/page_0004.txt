1. SYRIAN ARMS SHIPMENT TO ALGERIAN REBELS
SCHEDULED TO BEGIN ON 14 JUNE

a plane "for the transport of the arms and ammunition" would arrive in Damascus "on Thursday," with another following "on Friday." A third aircraft is to come "after the 18 June celebrations marking the final evacuation of Suez by the British."

Comment

the 12-ton shipment of arms and ammunition which the Syrian chief of staff promised some time ago to make available to the Algerian rebels.

the shipment was to be collected in Syria on 28 May and transported to an unspecified destination, in four flights on consecutive days.
