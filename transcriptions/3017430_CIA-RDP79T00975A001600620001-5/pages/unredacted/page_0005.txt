Indonesia is expected to reopen the question of Netherlands New Guinea at the Netherlands-Indonesia talks which are about to be resumed at The Hague. Anticipating continued Dutch refusal to discuss New Guinea at The Hague, a leading member of the Indonesian delegation there stated that on 18 August, Indonesia will commence the "battle for West Irian."

Both the Netherlands and Indonesia have requested American support for their conflicting positions regarding New Guinea.

FAR EAST

3. Three Chinese Communist MIG-15 divisions apparently withdrawn from Yalu area:

Comment: One of the three MIG units believed transferred from the Yalu area is the 17th Air Division, which is now based at Ningpo on the East China coast about 100 miles south of Shanghai. There is also some evidence that a second jet fighter division was moved to the Shanghai area in March. The identity and location of the third division are not known.

The withdrawal of the three divisions reduces Communist jet fighter strength on the Manchurian side of the Yalu to about 400, including 250 Soviet planes. Peiping's continued reduction of its once great jet strength in Manchuria had been expected. The apparent build-up of air power in East China, where MIG's now number between 200 and 250, directly threatens Chinese Nationalist islands in the coastal waters of the East China Sea.
