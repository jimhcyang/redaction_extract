28 OCTOBER 1959

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC

Soviet economic plan for 1960 calls for 8.1-percent rise in industrial output; budget allows for continued expansion of military programs.

Personnel shifts in Poland reflect regime's concern over economic situation.

II. ASIA-AFRICA

Algerian rebels seeking clarification of De Gaulle's terms for a cease-fire.

French military chief in Algeria reports army's concern over De Gaulle's policy.

UAR has postponed issuing decree that would raise fees paid by Tapline.

III. THE WEST

Cuba -- Castro's strong anti-American line lays ground for more extreme measures against US interests.
