2. Turks restate eagerness for membership in NATO:

The Turkish Ambassador in Moscow has made the following statements to US Ambassador Kirk:

(a) Turkey does not consider its current treaties with the UK and France a satisfactory safeguard and wants the immediate support of all the NATO countries in the event that Turkey is attacked;

(b) The Ambassador, himself, is uncertain of the genuineness of British support of Turkey's application to join NATO, despite the recent British declaration; and,

(c) He does not favor an Eastern Mediterranean Pact for fear that the Arab States's insistence on joining would have disastrous repercussions vis-a-vis Israel.
