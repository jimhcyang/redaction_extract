22 MARCH 1958

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC

Two new Soviet nuclear explosions—
one at Semipalatinsk on 20 March
the other at Novaya Zemlya on 21 March.

USSR may have acquired re-entry
data from 30 January ICBM test
vehicle firing.

II. ASIA-AFRICA

Indonesian Army now said to plan
attack on Padang in second week
of April.

Japan planning to proceed with
private trade agreement with Com-
munist China, but seeks way to
satisfy some Chinese Nationalist
objections.

Libya - King Idriss may be con-
sidering important shifts in per-
sonnel and structure of government.

III. THE WEST

Gaillard continues to stall for
time on Tunisian issue; further
delay may lead Bourguiba to ap-
peal to UN Security Council.

France may test first nuclear
weapon late this year, possibly
in November.
