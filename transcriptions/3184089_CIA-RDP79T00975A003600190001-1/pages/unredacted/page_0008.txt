The waters adjacent to the Kamchatka Peninsula were
the intended impact area, possibly for both the 30 January
and 12 March operations. Water impact has previously
been predicted as very likely by US intelligence.

In any event, it
seems clear that this test ICBM launched from Tyura Tam
achieved its intended range of about 3,500 nautical miles
and that the USSR may have acquired some re-entry data
from this firing.
