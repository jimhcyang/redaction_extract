CENTRAL INTELLIGENCE BULLETIN
22 March 1958

DAILY BRIEF

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC

II. ASIA-AFRICA

Indonesia: The Indonesian army G-3 states an attack on the dissident center of Padang is planned for the second week of April. A dissident warning that an attempt will be made to sabotage Caltex and Stanvac installations in Central Sumatra rather than permit Djakarta to benefit from renewed operations is causing concern to company officials. In North Sumatra, government forces claim to be successfully dispersing the dissident forces which withdrew from Medan on 17 March.
