SOVIET UNION

1. IL-28 bombers apparently leaving East Germany again:

Comment: All IL-28 bomber and reconnaissance units were withdrawn from East Germany for unknown reasons in late June and early July. It now appears that the return of some of these aircraft was temporary and may have been an exercise in tactical redeployment from rear area bases. It may also reflect a decision to retain the IL-28's in the western USSR with former bases in East Germany serving as forward tactical airfields.
