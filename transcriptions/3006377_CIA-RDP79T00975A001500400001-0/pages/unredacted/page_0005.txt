5. Viet Minh stresses propaganda value of successes at Dien Bien Phu:

a captured Viet Minh propaganda directive states: "We must profit from Dien Bien Phu victories and make use of the confused political situation in France to break down the morale of the French Union forces." A new psychological warfare campaign was ordered, to be coordinated with the current battles and the discussions at Geneva.

The reporting office comments that if Dien Bien Phu falls, the Viet Minh may make some spectacular "humane" gesture aimed at the French home front, such as offering to release some of the captured garrison.

NEAR EAST-AFRICA

6. Comment on selection of Nuri Said to form Iraqi cabinet:

King Faisal's request that Nuri Said, dominant member of parliament, form a new cabinet indicates that Iraq will continue to have a pro-Western government.

Nuri Said, who has been prime minister 11 times in the 33 years of modern Iraq's history, strongly favors Iraqi adherence to the Turkish-Pakistani pact.
