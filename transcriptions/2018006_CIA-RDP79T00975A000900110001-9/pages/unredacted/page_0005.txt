EASTERN EUROPE
5. "Rebellion" reported in northeast Rumania.
Rumanian security police and militia were investigating "the beginning of a rebellion" in the Dorohoi area in northeast Rumania and had already arrested 90 persons.

Comment: considerable peasant unrest and some overt acts against local authorities, although nothing on the scale of a "rebellion." The militia has also been recruiting additional men for rural posts.
