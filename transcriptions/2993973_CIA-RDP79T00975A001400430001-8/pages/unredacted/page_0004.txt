FAR EAST

3. Peiping presses India to concede that previous agreements on Tibet are invalid:
Peiping's position in its current talks with India on Tibet is that previous Indian agreements with China were "unequal treaties" and that negotiations should start on the basis that they are invalid.

NEAR EAST - AFRICA

4. Foreign powers allegedly implicated in Syrian crisis:
The king of Saudi Arabia told Ambassador Wadsworth on 3 February that reports from his ambassador and other sources in Syria indicated that British and Iraqi intrigue to achieve an Iraqi-Syrian union was responsible for the current Syrian crisis.
