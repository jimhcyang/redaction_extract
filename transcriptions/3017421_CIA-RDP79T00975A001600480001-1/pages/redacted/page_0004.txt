4. Israel may resume work on controversial Jordan River canal:

The American embassy in Tel Aviv believes Israel may soon resume work on the portion of its Jordan River diversionary canal which lies in the Israeli-Syrian demilitarized zone.

According to the Israeli minister in Washington, his government is under "strong pressures" at home to continue the project and Israel cannot refrain indefinitely from doing so.
