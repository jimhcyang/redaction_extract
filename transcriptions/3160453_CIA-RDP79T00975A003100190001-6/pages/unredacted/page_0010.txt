7. NEW OUTBREAKS THREATEN HAITI WITH ANARCHY

Comment on:

Pre-election tensions have erupted into
widespread violence, threatening Haiti
with anarchy. Strikers and demonstrators are demanding the resignation of
the executive council, Haiti's fourth government since last
December. The army appears to be the only remaining
stabilizing element, but popular pressures against military
rule forced army leaders to back down after attempting to assume control of the government earlier this month.

Current disturbances broke out after five
presidential candidates accused the two leading candidates who
control the executive council of attempting to rig the election, now scheduled for 16 June. The five candidates have announced that they will boycott the election, and are urging their
followers not to register.
