CENTRAL INTELLIGENCE BULLETIN
30 January 1958

DAILY BRIEF

II. ASIA-AFRICA

Egyptian-Syrian union: Nasir may be considering postponement of the announcement of Egyptian-Syrian union, but Syrian President Quwatli is still scheduled to arrive in Cairo on 1 February. Nasir is troubled by indications that the Soviet Union opposes the move, and he has second thoughts on the union's proposed parliamentary structure.

Syria: Syrian Communist Party leader Bakdash has clearly implied in a public statement that his party will not be dissolved voluntarily despite the fact that dissolution of all Syrian political parties is one of the conditions Nasir has set for Egyptian agreement to union with Syria.
