3. POLAND PARTICIPATING IN EGYPTIAN ARMS DEAL

Comment

the first indication that Polish experts, in addition to Czechs and Russians, will be sent to Egypt under the terms of the recent arms deal.

The five Polish experts are probably to be sent to Alexandria to arrange for receipt and maintenance of the nine motor torpedo boats offered by the Czechs on 31 August.

also suggests that arrangements are being made for Egyptian naval personnel to be trained in Poland just as Egyptian pilots are being trained in Czechoslovakia. (Prepared by ORR)
