2. NASR REPORTED SEEKING BRITISH LABORITES' AID TOWARD SUEZ COMPROMISE

Egypt has no intention of "building an empire" in the Near East and that Egypt would never "attack Israel. Nasr asserted, however, that the day of British "stooges" in the area has passed, and that it is to Britain's interest to co-operate with Arab nationalists in order to prevent Communist influence from replacing British;

Comment

Nasr is to have been in contact with Labor Party leaders in Britain for some time, and it is not unlikely that he has made an approach of this kind.
