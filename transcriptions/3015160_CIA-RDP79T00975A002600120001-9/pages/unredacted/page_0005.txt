The arrival of the guano gatherers on Robert apparently panicked the Vietnamese army unit on nearby Pattle Island, which still insists the move was a troop landing. The unit originally asked Saigon for permission to withdraw from Pattle.

According to late word from Taipei, the two Nationalist destroyer escorts now operating in the Spratly group, several hundred miles south, will not proceed to the Paracels as previously indicated.
