4. RIGHTIST MANEUVERINGS IN SYRIA

A rightist military group led by Colonel Nufuri, G-1 of the Syrian army, is apparently continuing its efforts to weaken leftist elements in the Syrian army.

Colonel Sarraj, the Syrian strong man, has been removed from his position as head of army intelligence and ordered to a post abroad. Other leftist-inclined officers have been transferred to less influential positions. Rightist officers are reputed to have taken command of the all-important armored brigades and to be converging on Damascus.

If these reports are true, this action would be a heavy blow to the leftists, and severe counteraction could be expected. Sarraj has previously been ordered to posts outside the country but remained in Damascus.
