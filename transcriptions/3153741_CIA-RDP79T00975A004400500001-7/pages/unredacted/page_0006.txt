*Somalia: Prime Minister Issa's action in accepting the resignation of Minister of Interior Bogor, an influential tribal leader, has precipitated a government crisis in the Italian Trust Territory of Somalia and threatens to cause a split along tribal lines in the governing party, the Somali Youth League (SYL). The issue of a party split is expected to come to a head at the SYL party congress, scheduled to begin on 16 May. Political instability resulting from a party split along tribal lines would provide an atmosphere conducive to further outside influence, especially from the UAR.
