Chou said the Chinese Communists were willing to continue indefinitely Sino-American talks at Geneva, but would not renounce force in the Taiwan area unless there was an American withdrawal. Insisting that Taiwan is Chinese territory, Chou declared there will be direct "negotiations" with Chiang Kai-shek for Taiwan’s liberation. 6. Chinese Communist propaganda to Taiwan has begun emphasizing charges that Soviet missile successes are weakening the US and Nationalist position on Taiwan. Nationalist officials, it is claimed, are "gradually losing confidence" in American strength, and prospects for Taiwan’s "liberation" are "increasing daily."
