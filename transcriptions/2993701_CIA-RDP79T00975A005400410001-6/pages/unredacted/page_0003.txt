17 DECEMBER 1960

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC
USSR tests another supersonic aerodynamic vehicle.
Vietnamese Communists to announce on 20 December united front throughout South Vietnam.

II. ASIA-AFRICA
Somali Republic to recognize Communist China.
Shah says he plans parliamentary elections; will choose candidates himself.
Situation in the Congo.
Ethiopia--Coup attempt has collapsed.

III. THE WEST
Italy--Political tension rising.
Ecuador to establish relations with USSR.

LATE ITEM
Situation in Laos.
