THE ARAB-ISRAELI SITUATION
(Information as of 1700, 3 September)

The UN truce supervisory organization in the Gaza area reports that Israeli forces have resumed close-in patrols of the border by units of platoon strength, according to the American army attache in Tel Aviv. Increased clashes with Egyptian forces are probable as a result of this type of activity, which in turn is probably the result of some renewal of Egyptian terrorist depredations.
