EASTERN EUROPE

8. Arrest of Polish cardinal reliably reported:

report the arrest on 25 September of Cardinal Wyszynski and Bishop Choromanski, secretary of the Polish episcopate. In the official Polish Communist daily newspaper of the same date, politburo member Ochab attacked the cardinal for violating the church-state agreement of 1950 and "sabotaging" the understanding with the government.

Comment: These arrests will evoke a strong reaction among the people. Despite relentless attacks by the regime over a long period of time, the Roman Catholic Church in Poland, with the adherence of over 90 percent of the people, has retained its organizational strength.
