4. SYRIAN DEFENSE MINISTER SEEKS INVITATION TO CAIRO

Comment. The antileftist army group in Syria presumably is worried about the possibility that Egypt might support a countercoup by leftist elements.

The antileftist group was not satisfied with the results of the visit to Cairo last week of General Nizam al-Din, new Syrian chief of staff. Raslan will probably try to convince Cairo that no change in Syrian-Egyptian military relations will occur as a result of the ouster of former chief of staff Shuqayr.

24 July 56 Current Intelligence Bulletin Page 6
