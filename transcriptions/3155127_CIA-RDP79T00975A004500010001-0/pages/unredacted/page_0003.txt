12 MAY 1959

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC

II. ASIA-AFRICA

Saudi Arabia - New crisis developing between King Saud and Crown Prince Faysal.

Qatar - Leaders of last week's five-day strike of oil workers still unknown; further unrest likely.

Turkey - Defense minister alarmed at government's repressive measures against opposition.

Algerian provisional government leaders hold important conference in Tunis.

Burma - Libel trial of TASS representative in Rangoon will give Soviet prestige further setback.

III. THE WEST

West German officials no longer oppose East German role as "checkpoint agents" of USSR.
