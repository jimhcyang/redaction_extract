5. French will not sign draft treaties satisfactory to Vietnamese:

Premier Buu Loc showed the American chargé in Saigon copies of the French-Vietnamese treaties, which are ready for signature and which meet all Vietnamese demands. He said the French are refusing to sign until certain auxiliary conventions are negotiated.

The chargé noted that articles in the treaties specifying that they would come into full force on the date of signature account for the French reluctance to sign.
