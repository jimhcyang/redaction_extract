3. South Korean police direct antiarmistice demonstrations:

Comment: On 23 April a mob of several thousand demonstrators attempted to penetrate the US Embassy compound, and the previous day the largest group of demonstrators ever seen in Pusan marched the streets carrying antiarmistice banners. Other reports have indicated police guidance in turning out the citizenry for the "spontaneous" demonstrations.

SOUTHEAST ASIA

4. Increased Viet Minh activity in Tonkin delta indicated:

Comment: There have been several sizable attacks in the delta in recent weeks, and further pressure on the French there may be expected.
