Khrushchev, replying on 6 June to questions put to him by the Hungarian press, again rejected any agreement which would have the effect of "perpetuating the occupation regime in West Berlin." On his arrival in Moscow on the same date, Khrushchev repeated earlier threats to establish missile bases in Albania and Bulgaria if Italy and Greece allow missiles to be based in their territory.
