2. KING SAUD SEEKS SYRIAN AND EGYPTIAN VIEWS ON
RECEPTION FOR RICHARDS MISSION

Comment

This appears to be a form of
pressure by Saud on Nasr, who probably
does not want to define his attitude toward the United States
at this time but does not want to antagonize Saud. Nasr may
well try to temporize further, possibly by asserting that such
definition should now await a settlement of the status of the
UNEF.
