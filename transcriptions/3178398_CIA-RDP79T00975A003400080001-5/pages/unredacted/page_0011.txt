7. SPAIN REINFORCES IFNI

Comment on:

Spain is rushing reinforcements to its enclave of Ifni on the Moroccan coast, presumably in an attempt to resist a challenge to its position in West Africa by the irregular Moroccan Army of Liberation. On 5 and 6 November, 200 soldiers were flown to Ifni from Madrid, and five Spanish warships, one of which carried 800 men, left Ceuta for Ifni on the night of 5 November. The Spanish government is further reported to have requisitioned a private vessel, which will leave Spain about 10 November, to ferry troops and equipment from the Canary Islands to Ifni.
