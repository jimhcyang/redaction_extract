1 APRIL 1960

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC

Peiping’s 1960 economic plan calls for continued development at "big leap" rate.

Mao Tse-tung described as "physically robust and mentally keen" by recent Nepalese visitors.

Mikoyan going to Baghdad on 8 April to open Soviet exhibition; he may offer Iraq new economic aid.

II. ASIA-AFRICA

Nehru intends to maintain his past position on border issue when talks with Chou begin, but he is prepared to make some concessions.

US Embassy comments on the situation in South Africa.

III. THE WEST

UAR minister in Panama active in pushing anti-US propaganda.
