It is not clear how far the bloc would permit Egypt to get into the production of Soviet-type aircraft. Czechoslovakia and Poland are the only countries outside the USSR which presently manufacture MIG's.

Neither is it clear whether Egypt is, at this time, seeking construction of an assembly plant, a major maintenance facility to service the aircraft already contracted for, or a complete airframe factory. In any case, Egypt would probably remain dependent on the bloc for jet engines.
