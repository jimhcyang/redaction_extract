3. Comment on signature of Suez base agreement:

The 19 October signature of the Suez base agreement may bring the contest between the Nasr regime and the extremist Moslem Brotherhood to a climax. The Brotherhood openly declared its opposition to the regime and to the terms of the base settlement following the July "Agreement on Principles" covering the British evacuation.
