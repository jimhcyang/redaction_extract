5 SEPTEMBER 1959

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC

Khrushchev to visit Peiping following trip to US.
Peiping accuses India of "aggression" along frontier; Nehru to issue white paper in refutation.
USSR may be planning space venture prior to or coincident with Khrushchev's visit to US.
Increase in Soviet military construction may relate to operational deployment of missiles.

II. ASIA-AFRICA

Laos appeals for UN intervention; asks South Vietnam for troops and materiel.
Sudan--Group of officers reported plotting coup against Abboud government.

III. THE WEST

Argentina--War secretary's resignation quells present threat of military revolt.
