New High-Performance Soviet Radar

Existence of a new Soviet early-warning radar with a detection capability considerably in excess of previously known Soviet equipment is revealed.

This radar apparently has been increasingly deployed for operational use in several sensitive peripheral areas of the USSR since mid-1959, when its signals were first intercepted.
