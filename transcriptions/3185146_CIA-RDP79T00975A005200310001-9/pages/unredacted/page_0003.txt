5 AUGUST 1960

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC

New Soviet early warning radar's detection capability considerably in excess of previous types.

Moscow offers arms and technical assistance to Lumumba.

USSR seeking to defeat US request for UN disarmament meeting.

Moscow and Peiping making new offers of aid to Yemen.

II. ASIA-AFRICA

Comment on Congo situation.

Syrian nationalist leader and former UAR vice president flees to Lebanon.

Indonesia--Sukarno planning to ban two major anti- Communist political parties despite army's opposition.

III. THE WEST

Fidel Castro's illness raises possibility of power struggle between brother Raul and Guevara.

LATE ITEM

Two officials of Soviet organization which deals with arms supplies to foreign countries have been in Havana.
