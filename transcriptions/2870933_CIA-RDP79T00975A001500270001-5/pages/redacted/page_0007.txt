LATIN AMERICA

7. Plot to assassinate Argentine president reported:

Approximately 160 active and inactive Argentine army and navy officers, including some senior officers, have been arrested since 5 April on charges of plotting to assassinate President Peron, Most of those arrested were navy officers, but also included were several from Peron's trusted army unit in Buenos Aires.

Comment: With few exceptions, the armed forces, totaling 184,000, are reported loyal to Peron and capable of controlling any opposition. Few elements opposed to the present regime are believed to have escaped the extensive purge of the military which followed the abortive revolt in 1951 led by the anti-American Menendez. Plotting since that time has largely involved inactive military personnel and exiles in Uruguay.
