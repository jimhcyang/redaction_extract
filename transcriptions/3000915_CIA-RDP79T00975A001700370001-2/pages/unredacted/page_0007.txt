WESTERN EUROPE

6. Italians ready to accept Trieste territorial compromise:

Comment: The agreement on territory will remove the last major obstacle to an overall Trieste settlement, though some minor details have still to be worked out. Premier Scelba will have an easier time winning over public and parliamentary opinion to accept the settlement if only a minimum of land inhabited by Italians comes under Yugoslav rule.
