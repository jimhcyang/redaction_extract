According to a late, unconfirmed report, government troops
at an outpost in the far northern province of Phong Saly were
dispersed with casualties after an attack on 29-30 July by an
enemy band allegedly North Vietnamese elements. Phong Saly,
along with Sam Neua Province, was a stronghold of the Commu-
nist Pathet Lao prior to the settlement reached with the govern-
ment in 1957.
