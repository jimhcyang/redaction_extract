12 SEPTEMBER 1960

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC
Ouster of Albanian politburo member
suggests Tirana regime may be coming
into line with Moscow in Sino-Soviet dis-
pute.

USSR grants $150,000,000 loan to Mongo-
lia.

II. ASIA-AFRICA
Opposing factions of South Korean Demo-
cratic party apparently near compromise
agreement on cabinet composition.

Jordanian forces remain in state of read-
iness for possible action in Syria; Cairo
orders Damascus to take some precau-
tions "unostentatiously," avoiding "provo-
cation of the Jordanian side."

Situation in the Congo.

III. THE WEST
Trujillo regime, reacting to OAS sanc-
tions, calls big public rally for 24 Sep-
tember; Dominican radio warns US
businessmen to defend Trujillo from US
attacks if they want to do business in
country.

Stability of Salvadoran regime under
serious strain despite some recent
abatement of tensions.

LATE ITEM
Situation in Laos.
