18 SEPTEMBER 1959

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC
Personnel changes in Peiping appear to strengthen Mao Tse-tung's position.
Poland's agricultural policies criticized by satellites.

II. ASIA-AFRICA
Jordanian King Husayn may meet with Nasir and King Saud.
Afghan request for US aid intended to offset expanded Soviet aid program.
UAR orders reduction of its military mission in Yemen.

III. THE WEST
Portugal--Any serious deterioration in Salazar's health might precipitate overt action by opposition.
De Gaulle's Algerian statement receives large measure of approval in French political circles.
