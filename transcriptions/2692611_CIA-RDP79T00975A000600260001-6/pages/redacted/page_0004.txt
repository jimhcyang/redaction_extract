NEAR EAST - AFRICA

4. Comment on King Farouk's dissolution of the Egyptian Parliament:

King Farouk's dissolution of the Egyptian Parliament on 23 March, his scheduling of general elections for 18 May, and the reconvening of Parliament for 31 May give Prime Minister Hilali approximately the maximum time available under the Constitution to work toward a settlement with Britain unhindered by parliamentary opposition.
