Argentina would probably like to line up support for such a conference because of growing resentment of what it regards as American "dumping" and trade barriers affecting such key Argentine exports as wheat, linseed oil and wool. Recent statements on these practices by Uruguay and other countries suggest that a proposal for an economic conference would probably find overwhelming support at Caracas.
