Indonesia: President Sukarno's choice of leaders for his new 610-member People's Congress, which he plans to convene on 10 November, advances his concept of "guided democracy" looking toward the amalgamation of all political elements. These leaders have been drawn from the major parties and include the Communist party secretary general as well as an army representative. The congress is expected to be another rubber-stamp body. Sukarno and the army will continue as the principal forces in the actual governing of the country.
