6. INDIA TO RAISE QUESTION OF CHINESE REPRESENTATION AT UN GENERAL ASSEMBLY

Prime Minister Nehru has decided to request inscription of the question of Chinese representation on the agenda of the forthcoming General Assembly. The request is to be given to the UN secretary general after the US election."

Comment
When the USSR made a similar formal request of the General Assembly in 1951, the issue was disposed of by the customary procedure of deferring discussion, thereby rejecting inclusion of the item.
