Bolivia: President Paz has told US Ambassador Strom that he is under increasing heavy political and economic pressure as a result of recent activity by extreme leftists and Communists, who are circulating the story that he is betraying the Bolivian revolution. Paz seemed genuinely disturbed, and Strom notes he had "never found him upset before." Paz said his position was being made particularly difficult by Communist bloc offers of a tin smelter and other economic aid at a time when the Bolivian treasury is empty.
