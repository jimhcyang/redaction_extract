7. SOVIET EXPERTS ARRIVING IN YEMEN

A Soviet ship en route to South Asia was scheduled to call in Yemen on 5 or 6 May with "experts to prepare the port for what has been requested."

These technicians may be involved in preparing the port to receive two shiploads of "heavy arms" which the Soviet bloc agreed in January to send following the reopening of the Suez Canal.

Soviet assistance to Yemen is intended to increase pressure on the British position in Aden. Moscow is presently urging a four-power declaration against arms shipments to, and foreign military bases in, the Middle East.
