South Vietnam: Growing discontent is reported among
middle- and junior-grade officers of the South Vietnamese
armed forces over the government's failure to check Commu-
nist guerrilla successes and to rally popular support; in addi-
tion, the officers are fearful that the Communists themselves
might initiate an ostensibly non-Communist coup.
