CENTRAL INTELLIGENCE BULLETIN

3. Congo: Tshombé considering attack on Manono. (Page i)

8. Tanganyika: Britain to be urged to announce early date for independence. (Page iii)

9. Communist China: Economic difficulties continue to limit Peiping's ability to pay for planned imports. (Page iv)

10. Albania: USSR and Tirana apparently continue cooperative naval activities despite political differences. (Page iv)
