Nepal: (After six months of personal rule by King Mahendra, conditions in Nepal continue to deteriorate, in terms of both economic stability and effectiveness of government. However, recent assessments by the American Embassy at Katmandu suggest that while there is valid cause for concern, the threat to the King's position is neither as great nor as immediate as exiled Nepali Congress party resistance leaders in India have recently portrayed
