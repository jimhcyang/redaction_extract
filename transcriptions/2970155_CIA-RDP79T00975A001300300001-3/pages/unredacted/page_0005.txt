5. Greece reportedly losing confidence in NATO:

Greece is losing confidence in NATO as the result of Anglo-American differences.

The Greek government allegedly believes that the United States will now promote further European defense arrangements outside NATO and free from British interference along the lines of the Spanish bases agreement.

6. Trieste decision may have caused differences among Yugoslav party leaders:

The American charge in Belgrade reports that there are credible rumors in Belgrade that the Trieste decision has precipitated quarrels in high party circles. He believes that these disagreements may be manifesting themselves in the current attempts to close the US information centers, which has always been a goal of the left wing of the party.
