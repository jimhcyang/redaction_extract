Caribbean: The Haitian Government has placed the armed forces on a full nationwide alert. This move was prompt ed by an explosion and fire at army headquarters in Port-au-Prince on 21 June and by rumors of imminent landings on the north coast. The landings might be made either by Dominican revolutionaries attempting to invade the Dominican Republic through Haiti or by Haitian exiles attempting to take advantage of the situation.

*Belgian Congo: Nationalist-inspired disorders may occur in Leopoldville within the next few days if the Belgians do not satisfy African demands for the early establishment of a regional "Republic of Central Kongo," The Africans are led by Joseph Kasavubu, who played an important role in the riots last January. the native city is tense and that "wholesale violence" against the 20,000-odd whites may result.
