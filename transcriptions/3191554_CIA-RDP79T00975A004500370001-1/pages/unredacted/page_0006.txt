Caribbean: The Haitian Government has placed the armed
forces on a full nationwide alert. This move was prompted by
an explosion and fire at army headquarters in Port-au-Prince
on 21 June and by rumors of imminent landings on the north
coast. The landings might be made either by Dominican rev-
olutionaries attempting to invade the Dominican Republic
through Haiti or by Haitian exiles attempting to take advantage
of the situation. (Page 5)
