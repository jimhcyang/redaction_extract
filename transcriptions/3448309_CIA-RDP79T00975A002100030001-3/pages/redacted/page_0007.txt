SOUTH ASIA

7. Indian military officers visit USSR:

three Indian army officers will soon visit the Soviet Union to examine tanks and artillery items.

This visit follows offers made by the USSR as early as December 1954 to sell tanks and jet aircraft to India. The offers have not yet been accepted by New Delhi.

Comment: Air Marshal Mukerjee and eight senior Indian air force officers were present at the air show in Moscow on 3 July.
