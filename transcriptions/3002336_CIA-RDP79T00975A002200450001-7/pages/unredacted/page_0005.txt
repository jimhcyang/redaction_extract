2. EGYPTIAN COMMANDO TROOPS REPORTED TO HAVE
ARRIVED IN DAMASCUS

Comment  Nasr's advance move prior to official
signature of the bilateral military pact
between Egypt and Syria would be in line with his drive to draw Syria into close alignment with Egypt.

The presence of this number of Egyptian troops in Syria would alarm Syria's neighbors, Turkey and Iraq, possibly provoking countermeasures. Israel would consider this action further preparation for an Arab assault.
