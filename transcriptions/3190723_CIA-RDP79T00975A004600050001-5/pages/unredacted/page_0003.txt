6 JULY 1959

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC
Chinese Nationalists claim five Communist MIGs destroyed in air engagement near Matsu.
Khrushchev cuts in half the price of bloc arms for Afghanistan.

II. ASIA-AFRICA
Iran--Conservative neutralist elements may be promoting move to appease Moscow in effort to end Soviet hostility.
Cairo considers resuming aid to Iraqi nationalists.
UAR agrees to restoration of diplomatic relations with Tunisia.
Sudan--Current trials of dissident military leaders adding to disaffection and instability of officer corps.
Ceylon--Domestic criticism and continued labor discord weakening Bandaranaike government; leftist labor leaders threaten new strikes.

III. THE WEST
Caribbean--OAS facing critical test in dealing with Dominican charges of foreign intervention.
