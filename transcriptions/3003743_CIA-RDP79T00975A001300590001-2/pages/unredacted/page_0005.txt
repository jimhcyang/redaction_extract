NEAR EAST - AFRICA

5. Prospective Libyan crisis threatens to delay US base negotiations:

American minister Villard in Tripoli expects a showdown between Prime Minister Muntasser and King Idriss in mid-December when the premier returns from medical treatment in Europe.

Muntasser, at a recent meeting with Villard in Frankfurt, stated that his position as prime minister will become untenable unless the king grants him more authority.

Villard believes that there is less than an even chance of Muntasser's remaining in office and that his departure would indefinitely delay negotiations on the American base agreement.
