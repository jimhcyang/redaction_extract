SOUTHEAST ASIA

4. Burma would prevent charges against US in UN appeal:

The Burmese Government, in its appeal to the UN on the Chinese Nationalist issue, will make every effort to prevent discussion of American involvement. The Burmese War Office had obtained enough evidence to prove Burma's case against Taipei.

Comment: A possible deterioration in American-Burmese relations is indicated, however. The Burmese did not advise the United States in advance of the date of its appeal and there have been reliable reports that the Burmese leadership has been criticized by its partisan supporters for being too friendly to the United States.
