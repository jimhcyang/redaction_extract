SOUTH ASIA

2. Pakistani prime minister out of favor with ruling clique:

Comment: Pakistan's ruling group is again considering removing Mohammad Ali as prime minister. Ghulam Mohammad wanted to remove Ali from his post at the time of the cabinet shake-up. If Ali is collaborating with the dissident groups, both Mirza and Defense Minister Ayub Khan are likely to agree with the governor general.

If the group does remove Ali, Mirza will probably succeed him. The group would be most unlikely to consider another "front" man after its recent experiences with Ali and would probably insist on holding key positions directly.
