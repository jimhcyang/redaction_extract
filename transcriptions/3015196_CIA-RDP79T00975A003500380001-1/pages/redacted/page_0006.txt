West German Foreign Ministry officials favor offering India new credits of $72,000,000 for economic development programs, besides postponing payments on the principal of India's $144,000,000 debt. For political reasons Bonn would like to encourage further private investment in India by German firms. Bonn states that German firms are short of funds, and urges joint Western financial aid.
