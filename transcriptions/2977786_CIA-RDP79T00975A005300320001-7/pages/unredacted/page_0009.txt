The activities of Soviet diplomatic personnel and cargo ships suggest that the USSR is marking time until the Congo situation clarifies. Diplomatic personnel evacuated from the Congo in mid-September are reported to be in Guinea "under wraps," although the former ambassador to the Congo is in Accra. Two cargo ships believed to be transporting equipment to the Congo have held their positions off the African coast since mid-September.
