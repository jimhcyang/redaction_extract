III. THE WEST

El Salvador: Two deputies in the National Legislative Assembly--a captain and an inactive army general--are trying to persuade several lieutenant colonels to take advantage of the recent increase in dissatisfaction among the military with President Lemus and lead a palace revolution in the second week of April. The anti-Communist officer corps, however, may hesitate to follow the advice of the two deputies, who recently introduced draft legislation that would have favored Communists and a pro-Castro faction in elections for the National Legislative Assembly and local offices on 24 April.
