4. Reports of organized resistance to Bulgarian regime not confirmed:

Interrogation of recent Bulgar refugees has provided no confirmation of any organized resistance activity in Bulgaria at the present time, but does indicate general unrest and sporadic cases of flights by peasants into the mountains to avoid arrest. Interrogations also reveal no evidence to confirm the frequent reports of organized plots to overthrow the Chervenkov regime, an insurrection in east-central Bulgaria, and clashes between Bulgarian troops and partisans in the northwestern area.
