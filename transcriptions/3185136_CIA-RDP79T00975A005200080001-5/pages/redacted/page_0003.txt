9 JULY 1960

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC

Khrushchev probably attempting to maneuver Bonn into foregoing West Berlin Bundestag session this fall under threat of new crisis.

Soviet support for East German efforts to assert control over Allied missions suggested by stiff reply to British protest.

II. ASIA-AFRICA

Yemen--Bloc personnel attempting to gain favor with Imam while undermining his regime; Imam's prestige lowered by internal difficulties.

LATE ITEM

Disturbances in Republic of Congo.
