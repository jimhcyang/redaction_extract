2. SOVIETS EXPEDITE NEW SHIPMENT OF LIGHT ARMS TO YEMEN

Comment on:
A 152-ton Soviet-bloc arms shipment for Yemen, consisting of 5,000 rifles, 150 machine guns and 3.1 million rounds of ammunition, was scheduled to arrive in Egypt on about 28 January, the Soviet ambassador in Cairo was "very pleased at fulfilling the imam's wishes with such speed!"

In addition to the Soviet-bloc arms shipment, a 30-ton consignment of Egyptian light arms for Yemen is being readied at Suez.

some of the arms would be flown from Egypt to Jidda in Saudi Arabia. When a "shipload" of arms had been accumulated in Jidda, a Yemeni ship would carry the arms down the coast to Yemen.

The imam was also advised that in the opinion of a Czech arms expert the cost of shipping "the anti-tank guns" from the bloc to Yemen by air "was very high!" The imam was advised that his minister in Cairo would request the "speedy shipment of the other items to Egypt by boat!"
