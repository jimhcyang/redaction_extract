2. French estimate Viet Minh capabilities:

A French official in Tonkin has informed US Consulate Hanoi that the Viet Minh can regroup and resupply themselves suffi-
ciently within three weeks to launch another offensive in the Red River Delta and, in such an event, probably would achieve better
coordination than in the January attack. The official added that there was, however, no indication that an imminent Viet Minh ac-
tion was planned.
