Finland: The failure of President Kekkonen last week to replace the minority Agrarian cabinet with an Agrarian-led majority coalition probably eliminates the prospects for any early Finnish affiliation with the European Free Trade Association (EFTA). The USSR had hinted to the Finns that it would agree to Finland's affiliating with the EFTA if, in return, the USSR could get a commitment from a broadly based Finnish government that would assure Finnish trade ties with the USSR for a longer period than the present five-year pact.
