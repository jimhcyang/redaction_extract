25 JULY 1959

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC
Bloc reaction to US proclamation of ‘Captive Nations Week.’
Peiping asks Rabat for three consulates in Morocco.

II. ASIA-AFRICA
Tunisian relations with Algerian rebels continue to deteriorate; Bourguiba publicly admonishes rebels in Tunisia.
Saudi Arabia may be considering curtailment of US military advisory activities; UAR training mission has resumed its functions.
Shah again showing dissatisfaction over extent of moral and material support for Iran.
Dalai Lama will press for UN consideration of Tibetan question.

III. THE WEST
Greece seeks association with European Common Market.

LATE ITEM
Valid countdown in process at Tyura Tam Missile Test Range; probably ICBM.
