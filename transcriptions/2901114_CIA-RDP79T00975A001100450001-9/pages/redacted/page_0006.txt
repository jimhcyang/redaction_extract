WESTERN EUROPE

7. West German populace seen standing firm against Moscow's unity overtures:

According to American officials in Bonn, West German public opinion would support an Allied rejection of any Moscow offer for a neutralized unified Germany. These American officials declare that in the past year the West German populace has become convinced of the importance of ignoring Soviet unity offers which would leave Germany neutralized without general European disarmament. Even the opposition Social Democrats now admit that Germany must be armed.
