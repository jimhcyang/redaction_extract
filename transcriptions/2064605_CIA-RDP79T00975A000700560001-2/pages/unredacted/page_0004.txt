STATIONS OF THE MAIN SOVIET AIR DEFENSE NET
(1) New Air Def Sector HQ  (2) Air Def Regional HQ  (3) Military District HQ
(4) Naval HQ  (5) Groups of Forces Outside USSR

Comment: Establishment of the smaller sectors and the elimination in some cases of one stage of the reporting sequence should increase the efficiency of the Soviet air defense system. The main air defense communications net now has 33 stations.

A concomitant development adding to Soviet capabilities for defense against air attack is the probable integration since last April of the air warning systems in the European Satellites with that of the Soviet Union.
