WESTERN EUROPE

8. Denmark refuses to permit US inspection of grounded MIG-15:
The Danish Defense Minister on 5 March refused the American Air Attache permission to inspect the Polish Air Force MIG-15 which landed on Bornholm that morning. Stating that the matter was to be kept exclusively Danish, he promised that the United States would nevertheless obtain full information from the Danish investigations.

The Department of State has instructed the American Embassy at Copenhagen to state that the United States Government considers the matter of "crucial military importance," and to seek a minimum assurance that the plane will not be returned promptly to Poland. Washington has suggested plans for examination of the plane by American technicians.
