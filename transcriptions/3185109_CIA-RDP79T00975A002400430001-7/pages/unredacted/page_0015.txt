The Soviet freighter General Chernakowsky,
which had declared Albania as its destination when it passed
Istanbul coming out of the Black Sea on 29 February, arrived
at Alexandria on 3 March. Its deck cargo, covered by tarpau-
lins, reportedly included 16 "light tanks" (probably T-34 medium
tanks). Its arrival brings to 16 the number of arms cargoes transported by Soviet freighters from Black Sea ports to Egypt since deliveries began in late October. The Karaganda, the last Soviet freighter to arrive at Alexandria prior to the Chernakowsky, reportedly delivered artillery as well as ammunition.
