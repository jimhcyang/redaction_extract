Souvanna's new government will receive the approval of the
National Assembly shortly. Aside from Souvanna, the most
noteworthy appointment is that of Quinim Pholsena as interior
minister. Quinim is the leftist, neutralist leader of the Santiphab
party, which was in parliamentary alliance with the pro-Communist
Neo Lao Hak Sat (NLHS) when both parties were represented in the
assembly. Otherwise, the cabinet is made up largely of unknowns.
The King must give his approval for the government to be legitimate.
