31 MARCH 1960

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC
Peiping’s announcement on urban communes suggests confidence in program; Mao makes first public appearance in Peiping in five months. (1)

II. ASIA-AFRICA
South Africa--Further serious disturbance probable. (2)
Outbreaks of racial violence in Rhodesia likely. (3)
UN group views South Korean election as "total fraud"; its report would further damage Seoul’s international standing. (4)
UAR officials increasingly critical of Soviet performance in regard to industrialization program. (5)

III. THE WEST
Breakdown of present round of Cyprus negotiations may be imminent. (6)
