3. Chinese Communist - North Korean air potential:

Comment: The increased number of Soviet-type jet fighter and light bomber aircraft operating within Manchuria, and the continued efforts to maintain air facilities in North Korea indicate Communist intentions to utilize his air force in the near future.

On 18 April the US Air Force, Washington, estimated that there are approximately 700 enemy aircraft now operating in the Manchuria-Korea area. Of these, approximately 115 are conventional-engined fighter and ground attack planes operated by North Korean pilots. Of the other 585 aircraft, about 220 are believed to be jet fighters; some of these are operated by Chinese and others by Soviet pilots (the number of each is not known).
