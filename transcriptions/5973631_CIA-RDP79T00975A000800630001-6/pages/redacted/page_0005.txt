WESTERN EUROPE

6. East Germans may announce "national armed forces" on 7 October:

Reports that "national armed forces" will be proclaimed on 7 October, the third anniversary of the East German Republic, have been given greater weight than usual by current developments, the date of announcement of an East German army is a favorite subject of speculation, and that in the past, plans of East German leaders have been vetoed by the Soviet Control Commission.

Comment: Since preparations for the creation of an East German armed force are well advanced, and since its development will not be materially affected by a formal proclamation, the timing of the announcement would appear to be governed by political considerations. It has been assumed hitherto that formal proclamation would not come before Bonn's ratification of the EDC treaty.
