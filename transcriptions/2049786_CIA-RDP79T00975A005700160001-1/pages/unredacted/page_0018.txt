Vela is also a friend of the President's, having held high positions while Velasco was in office from 1952 to 1956. On 9 May, Chiriboga strongly chastised Ambassador Falconi in Havana for hailing Castro as a "great leader." In his now public letter of resignation Chiriboga described the Castro regime as a "bloody Communist dictatorship" which had openly proclaimed its alignment with the Sino-Soviet bloc and is threatening "the foundations of the Organization of American States."
