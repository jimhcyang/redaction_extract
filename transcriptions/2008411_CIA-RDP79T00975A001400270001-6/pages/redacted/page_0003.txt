GENERAL

2. Soviet Union attempts large copra purchase from Indonesia:

the Soviet Union is attempting to purchase 50,000 tons of copra from Indonesia for which payment would be made in gold. price was no object to the Soviet Union and that immediate delivery was desired.

Comment: Indonesia has blamed the United States for reduced world prices for its principal products and is looking toward the Communist Orbit for an expanded market.

The Soviet Union is attempting to obtain increased amounts of fats and oils in non-Orbit countries. Substantial purchases from Indonesia will tend to increase the prestige of the Communists there.
