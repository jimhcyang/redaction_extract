31 DECEMBER 1959

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC
TASS statement on South Korean attack on Soviet ship apparently not intended to create major East-West issue.

II. ASIA-AFRICA
Laos--Premier Phoui and cabinet apparently ready to resign; this would pave way for redesignation of Phoui to head nonparliamentary cabinet including CDNI.
Truce supervision officials see danger of new incidents on Israeli-Syrian border.
Cameroun becomes independent on 1 January; internal problems pose threat to political stability.
Clashes between Ethiopians and tribesmen from British Somaliland may further exacerbate relations between London and Addis Ababa.

III. THE WEST
Austrians react strongly to Italian move in South Tirolean dispute.
