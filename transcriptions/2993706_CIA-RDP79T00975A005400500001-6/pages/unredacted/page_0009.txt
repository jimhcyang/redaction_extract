Both Soviet and North Vietnamese transports continued to be scheduled for flights into Laos through 26 December. Four Soviet IL-14s were scheduled on 26 December to fly two round trips each from Hanoi to Sam Neua. Seven flights by Soviet IL-14s were scheduled for the Vang Vieng drop zone north of Vientiane. At least one North Vietnamese transport was scheduled for a flight to the Vang Vieng area. The total of confirmed flights into Laos by Soviet IL-14s between 3 and 26 December is now 122.
