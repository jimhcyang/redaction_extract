Dominican Republic: A group of Dominican Army officers plans a coup in about two weeks to replace the Trujillo regime with a democratically oriented and pro-United States government.

The military leaders of the plotting group probably include the two army generals who were reported late last month to be confident of considerable military backing for their plans. These coup plans should be distinguished from the maneuvers Trujillo himself is reportedly considering for a "controlled" coup that would be no more than a change in Trujillo puppets.
