20 JULY 1959

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC
USSR launches 12th successful ICBM on 18 July.
Khrushchev reassures Poles on status quo in Eastern Europe.
Gromyko shows interest in proposal for small subcommittee of UN Disarmament Commission.

II. ASIA-AFRICA
Ethiopia receives offer of aid from Czechoslovakia.

III. THE WEST
French claim Algerian rebels using US arms.

LATE ITEMS
Iraq--Kirkuk disorders apparently suppressed; Qasim promises punishment for troublemakers.
Comment on situation in Cuba.
