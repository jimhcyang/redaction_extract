2. Prague trial seen as opening move to influence Arabs:

The Yugoslav Minister in Damascus is convinced that the injection of anti-Zionism into the recent Prague trial was the opening move in a Soviet campaign to influence and ultimately dominate the Arab states.

He believes that Czechoslovakia was selected to spearhead the campaign as the Satellite best prepared to satisfy Arab trading needs, particularly to replace West Germany, now in Arab disfavor as a result of the reparations agreement with Israel.

Comment: Available evidence indicates that the anti-Zionist charges in the Prague trial were intended to "prove" the collaboration of the defendants with an international movement acting in the interests of "American imperialism."
