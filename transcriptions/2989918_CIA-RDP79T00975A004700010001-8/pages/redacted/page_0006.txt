LATE ITEM

*India: The violent rioting which broke out in Calcutta on 31 August is the latest in a series of demonstrations begun on 20 August by the Communists and other leftist parties in West Bengal State in protest against high food prices. This campaign was planned as retaliation for the Nehru government's recent dismissal of the Communist government in Kerala State. A general strike has been called for 3 September in Calcutta. Its success may be jeopardized by widespread arrests of leftist leaders and by growing anti-Communist feeling in India following the recent border clashes between Indian and Chinese Communist forces.
