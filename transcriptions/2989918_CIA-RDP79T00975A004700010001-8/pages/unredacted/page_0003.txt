1 SEPTEMBER 1959

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC
Khrushchev says Adenauer letter produced "favorable impression."

II. ASIA-AFRICA
Nehru labels Chinese Communist border incursions as "aggression;" press reports of further incursions remain unconfirmed.
South Vietnam seeks to aid Laos with troops disguised as civilians.

III. THE WEST
Bolivian Government under leftist pressure to accept Soviet loan.
Cuba--More anti-Americanism expected as result of rift between inter-American labor organization and its Cuban affiliate.

LATE ITEM
Communists riot in Calcutta; call general strike for 3 September.
