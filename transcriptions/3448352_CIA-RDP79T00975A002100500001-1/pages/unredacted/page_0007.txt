4. SOUTH KOREA ORDERS NEW DEMONSTRATIONS AGAINST TRUCE TEAMS

Comment

Rhee has apparently chosen to ignore American warnings that constructive steps to terminate the truce inspection system cannot be taken in the face of Korean pressure.

200 American compound guards from the 24th Division were scheduled to return north to their unit. Renewal of pressure against the compounds may have been ordered on the assumption that their defenses had been weakened.
