THE ARAB-ISRAELI SITUATION
(Information as of 1700, 26 March)

The Israelis are planning to lower the level of Lake Huleh, above Banat Yacov, by one half, according to

He says such drainage would involve dynamiting in the demilitarized zone. The British speculate that this action might be an attempt by the Israelis to undermine the UN secretary general's proposed trip. There has been great bitterness between Syria and Israel over the Israeli plan to drain the Huleh swamps, on which work began in the spring of 1951. Despite a UN Security Council resolution of 18 May 1951, the dispute has never been settled. Any work done in the demilitarized zone by Israel will provoke strong Syrian protests.
