WESTERN EUROPE

9. French disclose negotiating position on Saar:

When the French-German negotiations on the Saar are resumed, Foreign Minister Schuman will suggest a transfer of French sovereignty and French controls over this area to the Coal-Steel Community for the present and to the European Political Community after it is created.

France will gradually dissolve the French-Saar Economic Union as agencies such as European customs and currency unions are established.

political concessions will be satisfactory to the Germans but the real stumbling block will be the coal and steel allocations.

Comment: This is the first report of a realistic French plan for "Europeanizing" the Saar. The French will probably insist on retaining their economic advantage. Adenauer is likely to be conciliatory on this point since he appears eager to settle the question while Schuman is still Foreign Minister.
