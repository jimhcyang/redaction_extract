3. Pacific Peace Conference in Peiping to open on 2 October:

The Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference will open in Peiping on 2 October, according to a 29 September announcement by the preparatory committee.

Comment: The Pacific Peace Conference, originally scheduled to begin on 26 September, was postponed to 10 October due to the non-arrival of many delegates. At least 250 delegates are now in Peiping.
