FAR EAST

1. Improvement of Chinese Communist airfield near Matsus possibly beginning:

The air attaché in Taipei believes the Chinese Communists are beginning to expand the Nantai airfield at Foochow, about 40 miles west of the Matsus.

Comment: The runway at the Foochow field is too short for use by jet planes, but it can be extended to the required 5,000 feet in less than two weeks.

Construction work at the five other airfields in the Fukien area on the southeast China coast has not yet been observed. Improvement of at least one of the Fukien fields would be expected prior to the launching of Chinese Communist assaults on the Nationalist-held offshore islands.
