Comment: Armitage is in London for consultation. Up to this time Britain has refused to consider self-determination for Cyprus.

A British statement favoring self-determination probably would represent the minimum concession acceptable to Cypriot Nationalists. The continuing high pitch of anti-British feeling on the island is reflected in recent rioting, which was occasioned by the opening of the trial on 16 March of Nationalists accused of smuggling arms into the colony.
