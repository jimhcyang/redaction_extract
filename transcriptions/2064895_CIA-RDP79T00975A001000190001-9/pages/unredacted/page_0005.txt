Meanwhile, the Jordanian member of the Commission has informed the American Consul General in Jerusalem that he will not sign another agreement with Israel because Tel Aviv has broken all previous ones. He stated that after 22 January, the termination date for the special May 1952 agreement, any Israeli soldier found crossing the demarcation line would be shot.

Comment: A top UN official on the Commission previously informed the Consul General that he believed Israel was fomenting border incidents in order to lend credence to its accusation that the stability of the Middle East was threatened by the sale of Western arms, including jet planes, to the Arab countries.
