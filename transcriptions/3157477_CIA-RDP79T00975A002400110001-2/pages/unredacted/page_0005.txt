2. MOSCOW AGREES TO HELP VIET MINH IN PROPAGANDIZING AGAINST ELECTIONS IN THE SOUTH

Comment

Although the Viet Minh has been attacking President Diem's plans to hold elections for an assembly on 4 March, Soviet propaganda treatment of the issue has been perfunctory.

Increased Communist propaganda attacks on the elections may be connected with support for Chou En-lai's demand that another Geneva conference on Indochina be convened. A Polish truce official in South Vietnam has reportedly hinted that in any new conference, the Communists would propose a postponement of unification elections in return for an agreement on economic co-operation between the two zones and neutralization.
