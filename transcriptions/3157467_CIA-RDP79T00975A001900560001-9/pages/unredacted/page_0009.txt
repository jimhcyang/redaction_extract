8. Major troop transport mission on Yangtze River indicated:

Comment: It was planned to complete in January the installation of public address systems on all landing craft now being employed by these river fleets as cargo carriers, suggesting that they were being readied for troop transport, possibly to move troops from central and western China down the Yangtze River to staging areas in the Yangtze delta.

The troop movement may already be under way, as one of the largest Yangtze River vessels was assigned on 29 January to an exclusively military transport mission.
