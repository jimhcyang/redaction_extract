Comment: In making this offer, the Soviet Government was probably concerned lest the locust plague, which has been unusually severe in Iran this year, spread into the USSR. In April, the Iranians accepted a Soviet offer of two men and some material to assist in combating the plague. The Soviet Government is doubtless aware of the intelligence and propaganda possibilities inherent in its offer.
