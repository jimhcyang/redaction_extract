15 APRIL 1958

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC

Czechoslovakia - Discontent within party and among workers over economic decentralization growing, but unrest is not likely to break into open.

II. ASIA-AFRICA

Indonesia - Central government landings near Padang believed imminent.

Japanese Government under severe pressure during pre-election period: from Peiping on flag clause in trade pact, from Moscow on fisheries issue.

Egypt -- Nasir retaliates against clandestine broadcast attacks; threatens UN action.

Moroccan Government protests Egyptian propaganda activity.

Greek political alignments for 11 May election leave Karamanlis party in leading position.

III. THE WEST

US Embassy in London sees drive for unilateral British nuclear disarmament declining, but Labor party to press for test suspension and European disengagement.

Uruguayan Government to buy Soviet crude oil on barter basis.
