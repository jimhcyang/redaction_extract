III. THE WEST

Britain: The American Embassy in London considers that public pressures for unilateral nuclear disarmament have abated in recent weeks despite the continuing efforts of various minority elements. The Labor party leadership, while pressing its relatively moderate demand for suspension of British nuclear tests pending a summit conference, may now campaign more actively for disengagement in central Europe.

Uruguay: The Uruguayan Government, for some months past the target of an intensive Soviet campaign for expanded trade relations, has authorized a trial purchase of Soviet crude oil on a barter basis. Uruguay is plagued with foreign exchange shortages derived from growing difficulties in maintaining exports of meat and wool products.
