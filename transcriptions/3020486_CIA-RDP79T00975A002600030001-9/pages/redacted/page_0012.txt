THE ARAB-ISRAELI SITUATION
(Information as of 1700, 31 May)

The Iraqi ambassador to Egypt told Ambassador Byroade on 29 May that he was almost certain that Egypt had made an additional arms deal with Czechoslovakia "within the last few days" and that it involved mostly aircraft. Byroade commented that this would fit with reports that Nasr has been "fretting" that the MIG-15's were not a match for the Mystere IVs sold to Israel and that he was getting MIG-17's. Byroade added that Nasr reportedly said that for every plane he heard of being provided Israel he would obtain four for Egypt.
