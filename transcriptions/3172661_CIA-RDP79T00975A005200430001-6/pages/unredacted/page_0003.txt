19 AUGUST 1960

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC

II. ASIA-AFRICA

Congo--US ambassador warns of possible new depredations by Force Publique and of precariousness of UN position.

Iranian security chief believes Iran headed for revolution.

Burmese Prime Minister U Nu highly gratified by border agreement with Peiping.

III. THE WEST

Mexico invites Supreme Soviet delegation in attempt to forestall Khrushchev visit.

Bonn believes Cuba may recognize East Germany.

West German foreign minister stresses need to equip NATO forces with medium-range nuclear missiles.

LATE ITEMS

New South Korean prime minister approved.

Situation in Laos.
