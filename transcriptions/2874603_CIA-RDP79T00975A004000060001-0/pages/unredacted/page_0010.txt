Advance reports state that a Nationalist LSM escorted by two sub chasers en route to Tungting, the small island about 16 miles south of Kinmen, were fired on by coast artillery on the morning of 18 September. One sub chaser was reported to have been hit and helpless, was being approached by enemy ships and was requesting US Navy assistance. Also, one Chinese Communist MIG fighter was reported to have strafed a Nationalist ship two to three miles south of Kinmen about the same time another convoy was scheduled to arrive there.
