18 SEPTEMBER 1958

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC

Taiwan Strait - Convoys under naval and air attack.

USSR may link agreement on preventing surprise attack with progress on disarmament.

II. ASIA-AFRICA

Lebanon - Chamoun will accept Karame as prime minister if cabinet is weighted with loyalist ministers.

Afghanistan - Prime Minister Daud may be planning some reorganization of government.

Three French Tropical African territories may declare independence.

Philippines - Mounting dissatisfaction with Garcia administration.

Ceylon accepts $10,000,000 in aid from Communist China.

III. THE WEST

USSR offers aid for Brazil's steel program.

LATE ITEM

Five high-ranking Lebanese officers arrested, possibly on coup charges.
