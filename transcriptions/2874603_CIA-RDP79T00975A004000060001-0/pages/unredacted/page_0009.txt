Peiping's official People's Daily on 18 September reacted strongly to President Eisenhower's call for a ceasefire in the Taiwan Straits, declaring that there would be no tension in the area if the US abandoned the use of force and armed intimidation. The Chinese Communists appear anxious to avoid the development of any impression in international opinion which might place the onus for the Taiwan situation on China. The editorial gives no indication that Peiping is prepared to renounce the use of force against the Chinese Nationalists at this time.
