2. Comment on air incidents in the Formosa Straits:

The two Chinese Communist air attacks on Nationalist aircraft near the Matsu Islands on 27 June were the first Communist attempts to intercept Nationalist planes in the Formosa Straits, although earlier attacks had been made north and south of the straits.
