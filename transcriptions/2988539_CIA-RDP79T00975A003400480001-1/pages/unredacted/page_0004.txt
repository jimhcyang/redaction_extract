1. US ANNOUNCES NEW SOVIET NUCLEAR TEST

Comment on:

The Joint Atomic Energy Intelligence Committee reports that the latest Soviet nuclear test occurred in the Semipalatinsk proving ground at 0720 GMT on 28 December. According to preliminary estimates, the device yielded from 10 to 100 kilotons. The test was the 43rd nuclear experiment detected by the US and the 15th known to have been conducted by the Soviet Union in 1957.
