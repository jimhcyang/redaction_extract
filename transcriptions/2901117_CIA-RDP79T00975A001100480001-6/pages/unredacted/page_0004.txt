3. Step-up of Soviet consumer goods production indicated:

Increased output is to be realized through the "broadest use" of local raw materials and scrap from heavy industry.

Comment: the new Soviet regime, following the amnesty decree and large price cuts, now intends to step up the production of consumer goods. Such action would be in line with the previous attempts of the new leaders to portray themselves as more liberal than Stalin.

With sufficient allocations of raw and scrap materials, local industries would be able to overcome many long-standing shortages of consumer goods.
