WESTERN EUROPE

7. Rome voices conditional support of Balkan pact:

High officials of the Italian Foreign Ministry told the American embassy on 22 July that Italy will not oppose signing of the Balkan alliance, provided (1) that it receives assurances from SHAPE that military planning aspects of that pact will harmonize with those of NATO, (2) that commitments under the pact are no stronger than the consultative type of commitment of NATO, and (3) that Greece's and Turkey's commitments are reciprocated by Yugoslavia.
