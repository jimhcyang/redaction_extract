1 JULY 1959

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC.

Soviet commandant in Berlin declares Allied military personnel traveling via autobahn to Berlin are under East German jurisdiction.

Khrushchev offers Iran "more aid" if Iran takes steps toward friendship; refuses to end propaganda campaign until such steps taken.

II. ASIA-AFRICA

Israel--Comment on Ben-Gurion's cabinet "crisis."

UAR and Israel opposed to compromise solutions of canal impasse; Hammarskjold arrives in Cairo.

III. THE WEST

Bolivian military official says government faces serious challenge from dissidents in eastern area.

IV.

Conclusions of special USIB committee on Berlin situation.
