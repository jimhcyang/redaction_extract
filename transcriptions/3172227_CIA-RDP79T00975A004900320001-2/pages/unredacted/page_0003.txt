9 FEBRUARY 1960

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC

II. ASIA-AFRICA
Algerian rebels request Libya to "facilitate" movement of Libyan volunteers to join rebel forces in Algeria.
India--Khrushchev may offer to assist India in constructing nuclear power station

III. THE WEST
French nuclear test shot to take place at end of this week; arrangements made to handle publicity, particularly in African capitals.
