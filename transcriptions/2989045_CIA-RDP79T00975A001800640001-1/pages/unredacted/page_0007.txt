Comment: General Orbit propaganda threatening that the Soviet bloc will take measures to strengthen its defenses against a rearmed West Germany has markedly decreased since the French assembly vote. This has probably resulted from an attempt to gain a greater propaganda impact from a renewed campaign against ratification just prior to consideration of the agreements by the French Council of the Republic in February.

Rumors of extensive Orbit military measures, which have been reported during the past month in Bucharest, Prague, and Warsaw, have not been substantiated and apparently resulted from the Orbit's propaganda campaign against the Paris agreements.
