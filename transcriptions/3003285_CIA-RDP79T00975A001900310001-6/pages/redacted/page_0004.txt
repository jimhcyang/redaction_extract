NEAR EAST - AFRICA

3. Shah now reported willing to continue support of Zahedi:

The Shah has decided to continue his support of Prime Minister Zahedi but intends to make several cabinet changes,

Comment: These reported actions of the Shah may have been designed to stop the intensive Iranian political maneuvering until his return later this week. In view of the long-standing disagreements between the Shah and Zahedi, any new collaboration between them will probably be short-lived.
