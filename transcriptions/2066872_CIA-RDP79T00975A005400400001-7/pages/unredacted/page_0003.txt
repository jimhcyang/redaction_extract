16 DECEMBER 1960

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC
North Vietnam tells free world diplomats that Hanoi has decided not to remain inactive in Laos.

II. ASIA-AFRICA
Nepalese King's take-over of government control appears motivated by domestic rather than foreign policy considerations.
Congo--Armed clashes between Congo Army and pro-Lumumba militia from Orientale Province may take place at any time.
The situation in Laos.
Ethiopia--Action of Imperial Bodyguard against Emperor Haile Selassie confined to capital, Addis Ababa; important army and air force officers appear loyal to Emperor.

III. THE WEST
Peronistas may make another effort to unseat Argentine President Frondizi.
Guatemala--New effort to oust Ydigoras regime may be near.
