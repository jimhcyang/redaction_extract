THE ARAB-ISRAELI SITUATION
(Information as of 1700, 28 December)

The Israeli army is known to have been drawing up plans for an action to break the Egyptian blockade of entry to the Gulf of Aqaba. It appears unlikely that Israel would mount such an operation in the immediate future, not only because of logistic problems, but because Tel Aviv now seems to be making a considerable effort to convince Western opinion of its peaceful intentions.
