FAR EAST

2. Ogata seen replacing Hatoyama by July:

Democratic Party secretary general Kishi is confident that plans to replace Prime Minister Hatoyama by Taketora Ogata, former deputy prime minister and president of the opposition Liberal Party, will be carried out following passage of the budget in late June or July. Kishi told the American embassy that he has been entrusted with negotiations calling for at least a partial merger of the conservative parties, with new elections held in abeyance until the election laws have been amended to undercut the left.
