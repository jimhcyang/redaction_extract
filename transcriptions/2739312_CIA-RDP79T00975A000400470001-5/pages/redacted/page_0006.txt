Comment: The Tunisian delegation, including the Prime Minister and Minister of Justice, has been in Paris for almost a month in connection with this petition. The natives generally are dissatisfied with the ineffectual French reform program initiated last spring, and are increasingly insistent that they share in the country's administration.

Vested French interests in Tunisia, however, strongly oppose even the present reforms, and are exerting pressure upon the French Government not to alter the status quo. French failure to make any compromise might cause the resignation of the Tunisian cabinet members and would set off anti-French demonstrations and disorders.
