22 AUGUST 1960

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC
Soviet recovery of space capsule.
USSR asks Nepal to propose admission of Communist China to UN.

II. ASIA-AFRICA
Mali Federation--Senegalese secession may bring violence.
Laos--Souvanna Phouma and General Phoumi continue to seek negotiations while Phoumi moves troops toward Vientiane.
North Vietnam increases number of illicit radio stations in South Vietnam.
The Congo situation.
