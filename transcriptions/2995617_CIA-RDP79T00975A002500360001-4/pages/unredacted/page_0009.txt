6. PATHET LAO VIEWS BUDDHIST CLERGY AS KEY
TO SUBVERSION OF LAOS

Pathet Lao strategy in subverting Laos relies primarily on winning over the Buddhist priesthood.

On the basic assumption that "whoever is master of the pagoda is master of Laos," the first objective is to exploit the Buddhist clergy's ignorance of political matters so that "they may appear as friends of peace and fraternity" and become unwitting propaganda agents. Some methods of penetration are gifts to pagodas, repairs to buildings, and the infiltration of the priesthood by Pathet Lao sympathizers.
