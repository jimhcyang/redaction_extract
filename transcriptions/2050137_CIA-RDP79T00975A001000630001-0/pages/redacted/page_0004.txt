NEAR EAST - AFRICA

3. Plans for redeployment of British Middle East troops hit snag:

The British War Office reports that for the present it cannot plan to transfer the 25th Armored Brigade from the Suez Canal to Jordan. The Jordanian Prime Minister has informed the British that as he will not be a member of the new government to be formed in two months when King Hussein ascends the throne he does not wish to make any commitments.

Comment: Since the Anglo-Egyptian defense talks are expected to start soon, Britain must have firm plans for evacuating its forces from the Suez Canal zone.
