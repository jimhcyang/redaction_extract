5. Peiping developing trade with Egypt:

Comment: The official Peiping trading company has not previously been known to act as middleman between two non-Communist countries. In the past year Peiping has made itself an important buyer in Egypt's cotton market by purchasing more than it needs and selling the remainder to the European Satellites.

These purchases, which do not appear to be economically profitable to Peiping, may be directed at persuading Egypt to become the first Arab country to recognize Communist China.
