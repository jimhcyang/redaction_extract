LATE ITEM

*USSR: Two valid eight-hour countdowns were conducted on 25 and 27 July, culminating in attempted launchings of what appear to have been ICBM test vehicles. In neither case was there confirmation of the launching.

Although initial assessment of COMINT evidence indicated the missiles left the pad, further examination of the evidence injects uncertainty as to what actually happened.

The closeness of the two operations and their similarity suggest that the launch attempt on 25 July was not successful and that the missile did not leave the pad. The numerous delays in the 27 July operation likewise suggest that the technical troubles continued and that the missile still has not been launched.

While some doubt remains as to whether a missile left the pad in either operation it is definite that in neither case was the missile successful in reaching its intended impact area.
