27 MAY 1959

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC
F. R. Kozlov, one of top Soviet leaders, to open Soviet exhibit in New York in June.
USSR may return ambassador to Iran in order to improve relations.
Communist China and India unable to agree on long-term trade pact.

II. ASIA-AFRICA
South African police expect imminent African uprisings.
Laos--Pathet battalion may again elude army forces.

III. THE WEST
West Germany--Power struggle expected at 28 May Socialist party convention.

IV.
Conclusions of the special USIB Committee on the Berlin Situation.
