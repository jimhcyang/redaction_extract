II. ASIA-AFRICA

Union of South Africa: The police are taking seriously nationwide rumors of imminent African uprisings, and plan to take determined action should violence break out. The dates most frequently mentioned are 31 May and 26 June, African Freedom Day. The mass burning by natives of their identification passes, expected by the police on 26 June, could result in violence and mass arrests.

Laos: The rebellious Pathet Lao battalion is now loosely encircled by Laotian Army units.
