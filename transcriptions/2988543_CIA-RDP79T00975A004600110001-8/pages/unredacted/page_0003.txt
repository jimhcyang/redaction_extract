18 JULY 1959

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC
Soviet official further defines USSR's position on time period that West may remain in Berlin.
USSR makes economic loan to Ethiopia.

II. ASIA-AFRICA
Iraq--Anti-Communist elements encouraged as 14 July celebrations approach.

III. THE WEST
Bolivia reported to have received $100,000,000 joint aid offer from Czechoslovakia and USSR; offer probably intended primarily for propaganda.
Attempted coup in Honduras fails.
