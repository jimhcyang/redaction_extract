Three Soviet Submarines Probably En Route to Egypt

Three Soviet W-class long-range submarines, escorted by a minesweeper, turned south after entering the North Sea from the Skagerrak on 19 March and are probably destined for Egypt, where they could arrive about 4 April.

The Egyptian Navy already has a short-range M-class submarine and two W-class submarines which arrived in Alexandria last June.
