WESTERN EUROPE

10. Italy to maintain its claims to entire Trieste territory:
Comment: Italian leaders have privately indicated that they would accept a solution giving Zone A to Italy, but public admission of this is apparently not feasible at this time.
Improvement in Italo-American relations has already been reflected in the attitude of the Foreign Office, which on 9 October expressed "warm optimism" that the changed Trieste picture would result in the speedy conclusion of the stalled bilateral negotiations for US-manned NATO base facilities.
