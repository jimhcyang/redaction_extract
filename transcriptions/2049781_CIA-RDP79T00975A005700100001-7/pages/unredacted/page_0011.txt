Note: Henceforth, airlift statistics reported in the Central Intelligence Bulletin will be computed on the following basis: 100 percent of all Soviet and North Vietnamese flights scheduled into Laos less known cancellations, plus 50 percent of all Soviet and North Vietnamese transports scheduled for flights within North Vietnam. The latter category is considered to be associated with the airlift. Airlift tonnages, when reported, will be computed on the basis of 2.25 tons per IL-14 sortie and 1.5 tons per LI-2 sortie.

On 4 May, 15 transports were involved in airlift operations--12 Soviet and 3 North Vietnamese. Of this number, 4 Soviet LI-2s were reflected flying round-trip from Hanoi to Xieng Khouang.
