II. ASIA-AFRICA

Indonesia:
an amphibious attack is planned on the Padang area of the west coast of Central Sumatra for 17 March and will be concentrated near the town of Pariaman, 30 miles north of Padang.
(Map)

Watch Committee conclusion--Indonesia: There is no evidence of Sino-Soviet intention to become militarily involved in the Indonesian situation. The Djakarta government is proceeding with military measures to subdue the dissidents, but an early resolution of the issue is unlikely. The general situation continues to favor the Communist position on Java and exploitation by the Sino-Soviet bloc.
