14 APRIL 1958

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC  
USSR continues to urge Iran to accept economic assistance.

II. ASIA-AFRICA  
Indonesia - Dissidents report Djakarta naval force off Padang beaches; Djakarta says two battalions have deserted from dissident forces.  
Ghana - Pan-African Conference this week will not be "African summit talks".

III. THE WEST  
British will urge Adenauer to overcome French objections to free trade area.  
French rightists in reconvening Assembly will demand Gaillard take Tunisian border problem to UN Security Council.  
Cuba - Rebels continue hit-and-run sabotage tactics.

IV. ANNEX  
The Indonesian military situation - a consolidated study.
