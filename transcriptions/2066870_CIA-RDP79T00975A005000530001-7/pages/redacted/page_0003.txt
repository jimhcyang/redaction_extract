CENTRAL INTELLIGENCE BULLETIN
30 April 1960

DAILY BRIEF

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC

Communist China - Nepal: Chou En-lai left Katmandu on 29 April after a three-day visit during which he took every opportunity--including denial of any claim to the southern slopes of Everest--to increase Peking's influence in Nepal and to make India appear intransigent in the border negotiations. Despite his efforts, the resultant treaty of peace and friendship with Nepal omits any prohibition on defense arrangements with third countries.
