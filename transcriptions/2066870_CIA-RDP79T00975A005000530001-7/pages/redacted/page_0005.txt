III. THE WEST

Cuba: The massive May Day rally in Havana seems certain to be primarily a concentrated attack on the US and on what remains of private enterprise in Cuba. Developments which the American Embassy considers likely include announcements of the nationalization of public services and of the reorganization of the labor movement under strict government control, statements that there must be some change in the status of the US Naval Base at Guantanamo Bay, and calls for Puerto Rican freedom and Panamanian control of the Panama Canal.
