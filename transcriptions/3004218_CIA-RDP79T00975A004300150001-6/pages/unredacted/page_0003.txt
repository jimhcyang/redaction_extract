25 February 1959

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC

First Soviet ambassador to arrive
in Ghana in early March.

II. ASIA-AFRICA

South Vietnam marines seize 70
Chinese Communists in disputed
Paracel Island group.

Iraq - Qasim continues to rely on
Communist support.

King Saud maneuvering to get
Prince Faysal's resignation as
premier.

Riots in Nyasaland add to pressure
for breakup of Rhodesia-Nyasaland
federation.

LATE ITEM

Khrushchev disparages conference
of foreign ministers and calls for
summit meeting.
