3. ESTABLISHMENT OF SOVIET-LIBYAN DIPLOMATIC RELATIONS MAY BE IMMINENT

the establishment of diplomatic relations between Libya and the Soviet Union may be imminent. An announcement to this effect may be made on 25 September.

Comment

Soviet approaches to Libya for the establishment of diplomatic relations were reported early this month. If Libya has in fact agreed to open diplomatic relations, the USSR has probably agreed to support Libya's candidacy for UN membership. This support, however, would probably be limited to the inclusion of Libya in a "package deal" including several of the Soviet Satellites.
