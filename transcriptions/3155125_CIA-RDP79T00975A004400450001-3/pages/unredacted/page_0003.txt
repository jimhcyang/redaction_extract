5 May 1959

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC

Chinese Communists and Indians continue acrimonious exchanges about Tibetan situation. (1)

II. ASIA-AFRICA

UAR press, under orders from Nasir, attacks Chinese Communist role in Asian affairs. (2)

Iraqi Army's ability to stem power of Communist-controlled Popular Resistance Forces further weakened. (3)

Turkish Government may take further repressive measures against principal opposition party as aftermath of recent demonstrations. (4)
