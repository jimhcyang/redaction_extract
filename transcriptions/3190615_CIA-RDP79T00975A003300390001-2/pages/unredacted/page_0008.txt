5. NEW TENSION IN TURKISH-SYRIAN SITUATION

Turkish forces on the Syrian frontier now number 50,000 rather than the 37,000 reported earlier.

On the Syrian side, no military build-up at the frontier has been noted. However, Damascus' formal requests for UN discussion of the Turkish threat, and the proclamation of the state of emergency in the Syrian army on 16 October, suggest that Syria is becoming increasingly concerned over possible Turkish action. According to press reports from Damascus and Cairo, Damascus in declaring a state of emergency has also begun extensive distribution of arms to popular resistance groups, which are largely leftist dominated.
