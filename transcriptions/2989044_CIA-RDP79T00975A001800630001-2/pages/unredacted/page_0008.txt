LATIN AMERICA

8. Antigovernment plotting in Guatemala may involve army:

Comment: Unrest is growing in Guatemala, both in the army and among the civilian groups backing Castillo. There are numerous rumors of an imminent attempt against the government, but no evidence that a coup is near.

There is no other information on a plan to kill Monzon. He has been in quiet retirement since leaving the government in September, and is believed still loyal to Castillo. His death, however, might quickly be blamed on Castillo and could lead to a violent reaction in the army, where he still has a considerable following.
