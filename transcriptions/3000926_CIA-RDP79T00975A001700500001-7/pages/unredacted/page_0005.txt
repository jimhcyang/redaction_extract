WESTERN EUROPE
4. Adenauer informs France of his conditions on modifying EDC:

at the Brussels EDC ministerial meeting on 19 August, Adenauer will not accept any French proposal which would compel the Bundestag to re-examine EDC, would result in any delay in the treaty's implementation, or would limit the supranational powers established in the treaty.

The French minister to the EDC Interim Committee has expressed the belief that Premier Mendes-France will take these requirements into consideration. The French government has also indicated that prior to the Brussels meeting, it will inform the participating governments of its proposals for modifying the treaty.

Comment: While Adenauer will steadfastly hold to his position on not resubmitting the treaty to the Bundestag for changes, he may be willing to commit himself to a modification of the treaty's supranational powers after it has been adopted.
