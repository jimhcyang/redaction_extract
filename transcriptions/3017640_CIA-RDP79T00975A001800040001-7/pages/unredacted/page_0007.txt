6. Viet Minh asks Soviet and Chinese help in combating famine:

Comment: Rice production in North Vietnam is barely sufficient for local needs, even in good years. This year, both the spring and fall crops have been far below normal.

Communist China could easily make 10,000 tons of rice available; it is exporting this much to Ceylon from South China every two weeks. This amount, however, would probably cover only a small part of the Viet Minh deficit.
