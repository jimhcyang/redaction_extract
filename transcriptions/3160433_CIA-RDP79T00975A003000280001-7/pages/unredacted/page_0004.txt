1. THE SITUATION IN SYRIA

Comment on:

While some of the proposed transfers of Syrian army officers have been carried out, the army chief of staff, as a result of a compromise among army factions, is reported to have agreed to retain leftist Lieutenant Colonel Sarraj as G-2, for the present.

The Egyptian embassy apparently is supporting the Sarraj-leftist group.

The situation remains unsettled. Leftist moves to involve "street" support are suggested by reports that Damascus youth were issued Czech submachine guns on 20 March in preparation for a "parade" on 24 or 25 March. Several thousand members of the leftist paramilitary "Futuwa" organization are believed similarly armed.
