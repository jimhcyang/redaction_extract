4. FRENCH CHARGE IN LAOS ADVISING PATHET LAO

Comment on:

French chargé Soulié has joined with the Indian truce commissioner in recommending the urgent return to Vientiane of Pathet chief Souphannouvong in order to head off growing cabinet criticism of the 28 December agreement with the Pathet Lao.

Soulié's lack of sympathy with US-UK efforts to block the formation of a coalition government with the Pathet Lao. It is now apparent that he is actually promoting the coalition.

By such tactics Soulié apparently hopes to increase French influence in Laos. The degree of his success in assuring Souvanna Phouma of French co-operation was made apparent by Souvanna on 27 January when he publicly thanked Soulié for his government's support, and observed that France as a fellow member of the French Union was the only government which had the right to advise and criticize Laos on foreign policy matters.
