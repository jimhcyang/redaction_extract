SOUTHEAST ASIA

4. White men killed in Burma reportedly were French Foreign Legion deserters:

Two of the three white men recently killed in a Burmese Army encounter with Chinese Nationalist forces have been identified as individuals formerly residing in Bangkok. Their names are the same as those of two Germans who deserted from the French Foreign Legion in Vietnam in 1951.

Comment: There is a small group of deserters from the French Foreign Legion in Bangkok. General Li Mi has asserted that he had several German technicians at his base in Burma.
