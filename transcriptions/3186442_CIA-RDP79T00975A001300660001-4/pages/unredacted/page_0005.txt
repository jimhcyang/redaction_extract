4. Construction of airfield in Burmese insurgent-held territory confirmed:

The American air attache in Bangkok has confirmed the presence of an airfield, possibly adequate for C-47's, in Burmese territory just west of the Thai border and east of the port of Moulmein. When the attache's plane flew over, workmen on the field dispersed.
