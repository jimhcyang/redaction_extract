THE ARAB-ISRAELI SITUATION
(Information as of 1700, 7 May)

Israel has charged that Egyptian troops have violated the cease-fire agreements by opening fire on Israeli settlers at Nirim, near the Gaza strip, and by crossing the demarcation line into Israel in the same area. No casualties were reported.
