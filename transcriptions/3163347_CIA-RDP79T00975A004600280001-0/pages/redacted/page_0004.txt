Somalia-Italy: Somalia, the Italian-administered UN trust territory scheduled for independence next year, has recently asked Italy for $5,600,000 worth of arms to be used for internal security. Rome has not yet reached a decision, but Italian officials have speculated they might provide funds to permit Mogadiscio to buy some equipment. Such action could lead to an aggravation of existing tensions between Ethiopia and Somalia. Each state fears territorial expansion on the part of the other; arms assistance to Somalia would probably prompt Ethiopia to seek more arms aid.
