III. THE WEST

Brazil: Brazil's foreign exchange crisis is expected to result shortly in defaults on some foreign obligations. In that event, the Kubitschek administration may publicly blame the United States for its predicament. A Communist-backed campaign that "American imperialism" is responsible for Brazil's financial plight will probably receive new impetus from the dramatic reappearance on 26 March of Communist party chief Luiz Carlos Prestes, who has just emerged from ten years in hiding.
