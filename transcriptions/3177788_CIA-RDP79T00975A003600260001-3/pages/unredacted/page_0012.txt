In North Sumatra, the deputy military commander has asserted that dissident forces in the neutral area of Tapanuli will be attacked in a drive to annihilate all pockets of resistance. The simultaneous arrest of 60 members of the Masjumi party and ten senior police officers in Medan on 28 March may place new strains on political relationships in Djakarta.

According to a Peiping radiobroadcast, the central committee of the Indonesian Communist party (PKI) will hold a four-day meeting beginning 31 March.
