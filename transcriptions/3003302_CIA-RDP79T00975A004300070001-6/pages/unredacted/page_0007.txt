France-Algeria: France is preparing an intensified effort against the Algerian rebels including diplomatic as well as political and military measures, according to a Foreign Ministry official. De Gaulle's recent demand that his NATO allies back French policy toward North Africa may stem partly from the failure thus far of reported secret contacts between the French and rebels designed to produce a cease-fire which the French Army could claim as a victory.
