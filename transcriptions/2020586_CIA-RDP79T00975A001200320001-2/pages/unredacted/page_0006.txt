NEAR EAST - AFRICA  

7. Egyptian foreign minister to contact British delegates informally:  

British charge Hankey and General Robertson, military delegate for the Anglo-Egyptian talks, will meet Egyptian foreign minister Fawzi soon, according to Ambassador Caffery.  

Fawzi also said that the Pakistani charge in Cairo had invited him to dinner with Hankey and Robertson on 30 July.  

Comment: These two proposed meetings should offer the necessary opportunity to arrange a resumption of the Anglo-Egyptian talks, which have been suspended since 6 May.
