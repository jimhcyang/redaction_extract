PATHET LAO ATTACK IN NORTHERN LAOS
JULY 1955

Muong Peun remains a potentially explosive situation, and hostilities there could spread throughout both Phong Saly and Sam Neua provinces. Neither side at present appears capable of gaining complete military control over the northern provinces. The Pathet Lao, however, aided by both materiel and personnel support from the Viet Minh in contravention of the Geneva agreement, is gradually increasing its military capabilities.
