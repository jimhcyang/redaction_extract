2. DIEM ANTICIPATES INCREASED VIET MINH
TERRORISM IN SOUTH

The Viet Minh plans a considerable
increase of terrorism in South Vietnam,

The Viet Minh effort is expected to be directed at
preventing further extension of government control and at
interfering with the training program of the Vietnamese
army.

The remnant forces of Hoa Hao rebel
Ba Cut in southwest Vietnam, which appear to have been
taken over by the Viet Minh, are showing signs of activity.
This activity follows the transfer to training centers of
army units which had been deployed against the Ba Cut reb-
els.

Comment    To date the Viet Minh has been cautious
in promoting terrorism in the south,
probably because of a need to develop increased capabili-
ties.

Vietnamese army operations have greatly
reduced the capabilities of the rebel sect forces, but the rem-
nants of these forces are probably now more willing to co-
operate with the Viet Minh.
