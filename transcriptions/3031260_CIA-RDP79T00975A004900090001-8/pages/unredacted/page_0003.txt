13 JANUARY 1960

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC

II. ASIA-AFRICA

Plotting in Iran for overthrow of Shah again reported; Sources allege national intelligence chief Gen. Bakhtiar intends to "take over."

Creation of National Front in Indonesia seen as further step in "guided democracy"; President Sukarno asserts right to dissolve any party whose principles or actions conflict with those of state.

African developments improve prospects for moves toward Kenyan autonomy; African nationalists in Kenya "supremely confident" of getting major concessions in London talks this month.

III. THE WEST

Cuban contacts with Communist China increase; Castro regime may recognize Peiping sometime this year.

Monday's rioting in Venezuelan capital followed series of terrorist bombings; regime has situation under control at present, but more unrest might lead some military elements to attempt government take-over.
