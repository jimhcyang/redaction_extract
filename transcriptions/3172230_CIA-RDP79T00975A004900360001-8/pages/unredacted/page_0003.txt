13 FEBRUARY 1960

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC

II. ASIA-AFRICA

New Indian note to Peiping does not advance prospect of negotiations on border dispute.
Israel asks US for military aid.
Iranian foreign minister dissatisfied with lack of progress in improving Iranian-Soviet relations and concerned over continuing Soviet radio attacks on Shah.
Tunisia recalling its ambassadors to NATO countries, presumably for discussion of Bizerte issue.
French forces in Cameroun, in response to prime minister's request for aid, planning military action against dissidents in southwest on 15 February.

III. THE WEST

Panama--New demonstrations against US Canal Zone policies planned.
