2. Embassy Moscow's views on Molotov and Zhukov visit to Warsaw:

The US Embassy in Moscow considers that the appearance of Molotov and Zhukov at the recent Polish holiday celebration indicates the Kremlin has reassessed the international situation. According to the Embassy, the setback to Communist aims in Korea can hardly fail to have repercussions inside the Soviet Politburo, and it is possible that new policies will be adopted in consequence.
