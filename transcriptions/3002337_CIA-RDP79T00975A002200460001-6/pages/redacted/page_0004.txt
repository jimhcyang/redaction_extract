1. SOVIET UNION AND YEMEN MAY ESTABLISH DIPLOMATIC RELATIONS

A Soviet delegation is expected in Taiz soon to discuss the establishment of a diplomatic mission in Yemen, according to the Yemeni representative in Aden. He said the Imam of Yemen, motivated by anti-British feeling and inspired by the Egyptian example, probably will seek arms from the USSR.

The American consul in Aden comments that Yemeni permission for the opening of a Soviet mission would be in marked contrast to the government's attitude toward Italian and French requests for permission to open legations, which have been ignored for months.
