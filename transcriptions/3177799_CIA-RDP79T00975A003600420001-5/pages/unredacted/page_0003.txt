18 APRIL 1958

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC
East Germany introducing broad program of military training.
Student unrest probable, and refugee flow may increase.

II. ASIA-AFRICA
Indonesia - Fighting for control of Padang apparently still continuing; government air units have joined in attack. Government airfield in Borneo bombed.
Saudi troops in Jordan ordered to prepare to return home.
Forthcoming general election in Southern Rhodesia will probably result in gains for segregationists.

III. THE WEST
France - New premier unlikely to be named before next week. Pressure for return of De Gaulle not significant at this time.
Nuclear issue still looms large in West German politics; Adenauer parties claim Socialists making little impact.
Fall of Icelandic Government on anti-inflation measures possible.
