2. Britain presses sale of TV transmitters to USSR:

The American embassy in London reports that British officials have twice recently stressed the importance their government attaches to the proposed sale of over $500,000 worth of television transmitting equipment to the Soviet Union. The embassy states that the government's concern apparently arises from the increasing pressure on it to authorize the sale, and from the extended period of disagreement with the United States over the policy and technical considerations involved.
