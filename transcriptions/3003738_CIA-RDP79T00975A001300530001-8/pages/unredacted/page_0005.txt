SECURITY INFORMATION

Comment: The expansion of production indicates that the USSR has adopted the Gorki model as the principal offensive weapon in building up a modern submarine fleet. This program ends the relative inactivity in construction of new long-range submarines since the war and will add significantly to Soviet offensive capabilities.

Since late 1949, 14 to 20 submarines have been built at Gorki. These four yards together could by 1956 produce an estimated minimum of 16 to 20 per year.
