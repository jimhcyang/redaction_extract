THE ARAB-ISRAELI SITUATION
(Information as of 1700, 22 December)

No significant military activity has been reported.

On 30 November the secretary general of the Italian Foreign Ministry told the American embassy that it would become increasingly difficult to resist domestic pressure for the sales of arms to the Middle East as a result of delays in the Washington talks and reports that the French were continuing to sell arms to that area.
