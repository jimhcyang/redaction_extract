CENTRAL INTELLIGENCE BULLETIN

8 June 1961

DAILY BRIEF

*Laos: Enemy forces penetrated Pa Dong's defenses late in the afternoon of 6 June, and following fighting which continued into the night, government forces began withdrawing to alternate positions several miles to the south and west. The attack was preceded by a heavy artillery barrage. The extent of government losses is not yet known.

General Phoumi left Vientiane as scheduled on 6 June for Nice, where he is expected to join Premier Boun Oum for talks sponsored by Prince Sihanouk with Souvanna Phouma and Souphannouvong. The latter two arrived in Moscow on 6 June en route to the Geneva Conference.

Bloc airlift operations into Laos were scheduled through 7 June.
