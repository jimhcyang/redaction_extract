4. FURTHER UNREST IN SYRIA

Comment
Incidents in Syria might be triggered by the "treason" trials now drawing to a close. Unrest exists among some rightist groups as well as in certain army quarters. The Populist Party and the Syrian Social Nationalist Party are reported secretly organizing resistance in Aleppo with the aim of stirring up the public against the government and demanding an end to the state of martial law.

19 Feb 57 Current Intelligence Bulletin Page 6
