20 DECEMBER 1960

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC
Germany--Interzonal trade talks nearing climax.

II. ASIA-AFRICA
Laos--Pathet Lao skirmishing around Luang Prabang and in Xieng Khouang Province.
Indonesia--High-level delegation leaving for naval and air force assistance talks in Moscow.
Nepal--King has now detained almost all key politicians; intends to rule directly for some time.
Congo--Morocco delays withdrawal of UN contingent; USSR threatens aid to Stanleyville dissidents.

III. THE WEST
Cuba--West German ambassador tells Cubans his government neutral in "Cuban-US quarrel."
