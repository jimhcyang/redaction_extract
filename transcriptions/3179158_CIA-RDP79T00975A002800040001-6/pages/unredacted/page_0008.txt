5. TURKEY AND IRAN STUDYING JOINT OIL PIPELINE PROPOSAL

Turkey and Iran are in direct communication regarding joint construction of an oil pipeline from Iran through eastern Turkey to the Mediterranean coast, according to Secretary General Birgi of the Turkish Foreign Ministry.

the negotiations have made significant progress. The construction will probably follow the right of way of the railroad under construction from southwestern Turkey to northwestern Iran, terminating at Iskenderun (Alexandretta) on the Mediterranean coast.

Foreign financial and technical assistance will be sought to handle the actual construction of the pipeline which, according to current planning, will tap only the new Iranian oil field at Qum near Tehran.
