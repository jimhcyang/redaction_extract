The West German Government will seek parliamentary approval of its defense proposals when it submits the Defense Community treaty for ratification.

Comment: Allied observers have felt that the German delegation would be inclined to overestimate both the speed with which German units could be formed and the costs of these units.

Germany has agreed to provide Allied logistical support to the amount of 1.6 billion dollars. This sum, added to the estimated cost of the German force, exceeds the country's 2.7 billion dollar defense contribution by 1.7 billion.
