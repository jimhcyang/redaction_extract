1. MOSCOW TERMS US CONTROL OF PANAMA CANAL ILLEGAL

A long Izvestia article on 7 September quoting the authoritative Soviet publication International Affairs argues that the Republic of Panama is the proper owner of the Panama Canal and states that American "pretensions" that the canal is an American waterway are "illegal." The article refuses to accept the distinctions made by Secretary Dulles on 28 August, between Panama and Suez nationalization, and compares the Panama Canal with American bases in Britain. Moscow broadcast a summary of the article to Latin America.

Before the London conference, the USSR in commenting on Suez on several occasions referred to the Panama Canal and other international waterways as proper subjects for internationalization, but during and since the conference has made few references to other waterways.

11 Sept 56 Current Intelligence Bulletin Page 3
