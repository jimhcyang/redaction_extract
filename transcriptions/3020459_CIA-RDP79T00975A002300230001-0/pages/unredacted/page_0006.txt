3. DESTROYERS BEING CONSTRUCTED IN SHANGHAI

The type of ship in question is possibly
the 1,500-ton Soviet-designed Riga-class escort destroyer,
first built in 1952 at Kaliningrad. It would be the largest
warship of postwar design to appear in China.

Some 90 Soviet shipbuilders, many with
experience in building the Riga-class destroyer, "W-class"
long-range submarine and small patrol vessels, are in Shang-
hai.
