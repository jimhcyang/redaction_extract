4. Yugoslav military moves in Trieste area:

The Yugoslavs have sent additional armored battalion to Zone B and have reinforced the areas bordering the Free Territory and Italy with one infantry division and elements of one tank division. The infantry division and some armored elements already in the border area have allegedly been moved closer to the Free Territory.

The Yugoslav assistant military attache in Rome has told an American official that three additional divisions, one naval and one air unit have been ordered to positions along the Yugoslav zonal boundary.
