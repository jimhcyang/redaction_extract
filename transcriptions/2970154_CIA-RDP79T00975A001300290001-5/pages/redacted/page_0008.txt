The Foreign Ministry believes that such a public offer to the USSR would emphasize the West's peaceful intentions and have a salutary affect on world opinion.

Comment: This new French approach apparently supplants Maurice Schumann's 25 September speech before the UN calling for bilateral negotiations on security guarantees between the European community and Russia. The proposed West German guarantee would ease current French fears that West Germany would use EDC in an attempt to regain territories in the East.

West Germany, however, would probably refuse to support any declaration which, in its view, would jeopardize its claims to "the lost territories."
