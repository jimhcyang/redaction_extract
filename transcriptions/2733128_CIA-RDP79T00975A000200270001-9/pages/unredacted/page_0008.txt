9. Franco may be planning move to forestall Madrid strike:

Meanwhile, the US Embassy in Madrid remarks that the series of editorials in the 17 May government-controlled newspapers warning of the "dire consequences" of protest strikes appears to be a deliberate attempt to intimidate the Madrid populace.

Comment: There has been an unusual crop of rumors regarding contemplated cabinet changes during the past several months. Franco could well consider this the appropriate moment to make some dramatic pronouncement designed to assuage momentarily the current wave of popular discontent.
