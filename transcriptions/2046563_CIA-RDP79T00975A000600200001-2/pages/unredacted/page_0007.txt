7. Pro-Italian extremists threaten disorders in Trieste:

Police authorities in Trieste have warned that "the most serious threat to public order for the past three years" will occur on 20 March, the date set by pro-Italian elements for demonstrations marking the anniversary of the 1948 tripartite declaration advocating the return of the entire Free Territory to Italy.

There are indications that the neo-Fascist Italian Social Movement will try to whip up anti-Yugoslav feeling and provoke incidents serious enough "to focus the attention of the world on the Trieste problem."
