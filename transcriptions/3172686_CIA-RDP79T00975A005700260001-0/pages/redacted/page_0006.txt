African Labor Conference: Labor leaders from most African countries are meeting in Casablanca, starting on 25 May, to set up an independent All-African Trade Union Federation (AATUF). Several African regimes--notably those of Ghana and Guinea--have promoted the projected organization as a vehicle for advancing their militant and anti-Western brand of pan-Africanism. Representatives from these countries can be expected to press vigorously for an early severance of ties
