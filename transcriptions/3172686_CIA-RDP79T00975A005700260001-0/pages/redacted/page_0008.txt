Dominican Republic: Trujillo is planning to overthrow his puppet President Balaguer, according to information given separately to the American Consulate on 23 May by the British ambassador and the Brazilian consul. Both link these reports to rumors that the dictator is planning a trip abroad and feel that he may be about to stage a maneuver designed to end in his being recalled to "save the country." The American consul notes that Balaguer is under increasingly heavy fire from Trujillo-controlled propaganda media and that such a maneuver would be consistent with previous Trujillo actions when he was hard pressed.
