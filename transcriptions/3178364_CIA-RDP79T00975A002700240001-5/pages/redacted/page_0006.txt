3. SOVIET BLOC MILITARY LEAVES REPORTED CANCELED

Reports of Communist origin circulating in East Berlin assert that military leaves in the Soviet bloc have been canceled because of the Suez crisis, according to the press.

The action was said to have been taken since Britain and France started their current military build-up on Cyprus.

Comment
If true, such an action would indicate an increase in Soviet concern over the Suez dispute. These reports may be designed to impress on the British and French the possibility of direct Soviet intervention. There are no other indications of Soviet offers to Egypt beyond "volunteers" and economic and military aid.
