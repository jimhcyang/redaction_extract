1. EDEN RECALLS PARLIAMENT

Prime Minister Eden has called Parliament into special session on 12 September to seek renewed support in the Suez crisis in view of heavy criticism of the government's handling of the situation. Labor leader Gaitskell's demand of 31 August for a special session was rejected by Eden, drawing criticism even from the London Times, which alone had been consistent in supporting the government's actions on the Suez issue.

The cabinet presumably believes that Nasr will reject the London conference proposals and that the Cairo talks will be over by the time Parliament convenes.
