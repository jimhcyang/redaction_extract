WESTERN EUROPE

6. French propose broader scope of revision of Italian Peace Treaty:

A French Foreign Office announcement on 22 August stated that consideration of revision of the Italian Peace Treaty would extend not only to the military clauses but also to certain political provisions in order to give Italy "moral satisfaction."

According to a Foreign Office spokesman, the Foreign Minister is personally interested in having France on the record at this time, and the Foreign Office has submitted a draft declaration in this matter to the US. However, no immediate formal action toward revision is planned.
