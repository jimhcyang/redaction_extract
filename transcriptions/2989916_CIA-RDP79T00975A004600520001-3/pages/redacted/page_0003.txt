CENTRAL INTELLIGENCE BULLETIN
29 August 1959

DAILY BRIEF

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC

Communist China - India: The border clashes between Chinese Communist and Indian troops publicized by Nehru in the Indian Parliament on 28 August occurred in areas of northeast Kashmir and Assam which Peiping claims are Chinese Communist territory. Nehru, who is publicly committed to defense of the frontier areas involved, has turned the Assam problem over to military commanders.
