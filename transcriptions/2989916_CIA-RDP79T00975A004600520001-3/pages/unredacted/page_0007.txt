I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC

Significant Developments Related to Special Watch Committee Conclusion on Taiwan Strait

Significant developments relating to this conclusion include:

b. Chinese Communist bomber activity has been at a very low level since mid-July; other Chinese Communist flight activity remains normal. There was no Chinese Communist shelling of the offshore islands from 17 until 27 August when firing resumed at a very low level. During the same period vehicular and troop sightings on the mainland can be considered normal.

c. There has been a lack of Chinese Communist propaganda as contrasted with the heated build-up prior to hostilities in August 1958.
