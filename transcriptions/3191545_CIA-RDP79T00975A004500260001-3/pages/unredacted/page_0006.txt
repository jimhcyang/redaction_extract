III. THE WEST
West Germany: Economics Minister Erhard's strong statement after his return to Bonn has disrupted efforts of the Christian Democratic Union (CDU) to close ranks in the face of an anticipated strong opposition attack in the Bundestag--in the 11 June budget debate--on Adenauer's decision to remain as chancellor. Although Erhard has ruled out any possibility of accepting support from the opposition parties in challenging Adenauer, he will apparently demand a clear expression of confidence from Adenauer and the party. It is not yet clear, however, whether he would go so far as to resign or force a full showdown over the chancellorship and control of the CDU.
