10 JUNE 1959

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC
Chinese Communists refuse to offer any concessions for return of Dalai Lama.

II. ASIA-AFRICA
Iran prepares "white paper" denying Soviet rights of military intervention under old treaties.
Libyan oil production may reach 100,000 barrels daily by 1961.
Laotian cabinet decides to release pro-Communist leaders from house arrest.
Indonesia--President Sukarno is expected to put 1945 constitution into effect by decree upon his return in late June.
Indian economic mission returns from Moscow with new aid agreements.
Ceylon cabinet reshuffled.

III. THE WEST
West Germany--Strong Erhard statement keeps CDU controversy alive.

IV.
Conclusions of the special USIB committee on the Berlin situation.

LATE ITEM
Probable ICBM launched from Tyura Tam.
