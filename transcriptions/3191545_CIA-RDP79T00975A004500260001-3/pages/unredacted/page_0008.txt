LATE ITEM

USSR: Preliminary analysis indicates that a probable ICBM test vehicle was launched from the Tyura Tam Missile Test Range at about 2030 GMT (1630 EDT) on 9 June. The operation and its objectives were similar to those of the 30 May ICBM flight which traveled an extended distance estimated at 4,000 to 5,500 nautical miles.

It has been estimated that the USSR might launch a Venus probe attempt from Tyura Tam during the week of 8 June. The 9 June launching does not rule out the possibility of such an attempt in the near future.
