SOUTH ASIA

4. Comment on the new Chief Minister of the Pakistani Punjab:

The resignation of Punjab Chief Minister Daultana on 23 March, reportedly at the request of the Pakistani Prime Minister, apparently was brought about chiefly by his inability to control religious rioting aimed at ousting Foreign Minister Zafrullah Khan.

His successor, Malik Feroz Khan Noon of the Punjab Moslem League Assembly party, has been Governor of East Pakistan. A personable official, who generally delegates his authority, he may nevertheless be expected to follow the orders of the Moslem League high command and to bring a measure of stability to the Punjab.
