In effect, this would subordinate the ICC to a domestic body probably dominated by the Pathet Lao. The Pathet Lao commentary also voiced opposition to the dispatch to Laos of any military observers or troops of the ICC member nations to help supervise the cease-fire. The entry of foreign troops, it said, "would not be helpful and, as in the Congo, would even worsen the situation."
