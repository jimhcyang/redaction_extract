10. AFGHAN KING ACCEPTS INVITATION TO VISIT MOSCOW

Comment on:
King Zahir Shah of Afghanistan has accepted an invitation to visit Moscow extended by Kliment Voroshilov, president of the Presidium of the USSR Supreme Soviet, according to a Soviet radio broadcast of 28 May. No date was mentioned in the announcement.

Moscow presumably waited until King Zahir had indicated his acceptance before it made this announcement. The king's other engagements make it unlikely that he plans to visit the USSR before next fall, however, and he may prefer to wait until next year.
