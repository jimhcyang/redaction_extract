WESTERN EUROPE
4. Early West German action on Bonn and Paris treaties seen possible:
High West German officials indicate that Chancellor Adenauer, in the event of a Constitutional Court refusal to accept the government's petition for an opinion on the legality of the Bonn and Paris treaties, is determined to press for final Bundestag approval of the treaties between 10 and 14 March. The court's attitude is expected to be known by 25 February.

If the government's petition is accepted, Adenauer will postpone Bundestag action until April, in the expectation that the court's opinion on the treaties' legality would be favorable.

Comment: The Chancellor recently stated that the French protocol demands might cause further delay. His coalition supporters are now urging rapid ratification without any treaty changes. They suggest that the protocols might later be negotiated and approved by executive action.
