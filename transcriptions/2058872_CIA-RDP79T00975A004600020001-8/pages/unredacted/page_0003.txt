2 JULY 1959

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC
Bulgarian party attacks critics of "leap forward"; may foreshadow a high-level purge.

II. ASIA-AFRICA
Chinese Nationalists complete withdrawal of 15,000 troops from Chinmen complex, leaving effective troop strength at 65,000.
Indian Praja Socialists seek to bring Tibet case before UN; New Delhi says it will not recognize a Tibetan exile government.

III. THE WEST
Continued efforts by Adenauer to eliminate Erhard as a possible successor are anticipated.
Argentina--Armed forces remain split over appointment of new top military commanders.
