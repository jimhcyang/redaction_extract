WESTERN EUROPE

6. Adenauer presses for reply on Allied occupation costs:
Chancellor Adenauer has warned that without
information on the amount of anticipated Allied
occupation costs, parliament will not act on
the Israel restitution agreement, and that he
might therefore have to postpone his trip to Washington until the resti-
tution agreement is finally ratified.
