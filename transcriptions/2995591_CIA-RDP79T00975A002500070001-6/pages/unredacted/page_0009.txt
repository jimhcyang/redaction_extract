(Information as of 1700, - 1 April)

Egypt has called for an urgent meeting of the Egyptian-Israeli Mixed Armistice Commission over a charge that an Israeli patrol killed two Arabs and captured an Arab child in the Gaza area. Israel claims that Jordanian infiltrators ambushed a police car and wounded a civilian. (Press)
