21 MAY 1958

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC
Communist meetings in Moscow probably dealing with broad issues affecting bloc unity.

II. ASIA-AFRICA
Terrorism in Lebanon may now take more anti-American direction.
Indonesia - Djakarta claims recapture of Morotai, presaging further attacks aimed at Menado.
Jordanian officials taking precautions against possible attempt on King Husayn's life on 25 May.
Laos - Communists and allies have potential for blocking formation of new government.
Pakistan plans to reject Soviet charges about bases.
Algeria-Tunisia - French Army in Algeria preoccupied with administrative problems; Algerian rebels in Tunisia anxious to avoid action.

III. THE WEST
French military insist on return of De Gaulle; mount war of nerves against Pflimlin government.
