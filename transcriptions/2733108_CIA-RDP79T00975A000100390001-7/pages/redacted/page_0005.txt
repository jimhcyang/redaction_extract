6. Egyptian Government concerned over Iranian developments:

According to US Ambassador Caffery in Cairo, top political circles in Egypt are increasingly concerned over developments in Iran. Caffery reports that, while government officials are inclined to blame the British oil company, they and opposition leaders are genuinely alarmed at the possibility that the Moslem Brotherhood will revive assassination-type politics, which, under the guidance of the Communists, it would direct against Egyptian leaders. Caffery adds that Egyptian political leaders do not know how to deal with this situation.
