8. Comment on Chou En-lai's visit to Rangoon:

The joint statement issued on 29 June by Chou En-lai and Burmese prime minister U Nu paralleled the Chou-Nehru declaration only in pledging nonaggression and noninterference in each other's affairs. The lack of any reference to the desirability of "unified" states in Indochina suggests that Chou was unable to persuade Burmese leaders that Viet Minh control over all Vietnam would be in Burma's interest.
