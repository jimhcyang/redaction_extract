USSR

1. Collective farms buying milk in order to meet deliveries under the plan:

Comment: Collective farms deliver their produce to the Ministry of Procurement, which in turn allots it to consumers, including cooperative stores. In this case, the farms would have had to buy more than twice the amount of milk they produce and then turn it over to the State, in order to meet deliveries under the plan. This practice (which may be an isolated instance) produces plan fulfillment on paper, despite inadequate supplies.
