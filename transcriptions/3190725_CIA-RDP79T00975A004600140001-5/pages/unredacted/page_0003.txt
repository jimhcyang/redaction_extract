16 JULY 1959

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC

Soviet source suggests possible device
Gromyko may propose for getting Western agreement to an all-German committee.

II. ASIA-AFRICA

Iraqi political groups may see Qasim's remarks on 14 July as signal to start new maneuvering.

Ethiopia planning strong protest to UK over press attacks from British Somaliland.

III. THE WEST

West Germany soliciting support for French position in UN debate on Algeria, possibly in return for French support on reunification issue.
