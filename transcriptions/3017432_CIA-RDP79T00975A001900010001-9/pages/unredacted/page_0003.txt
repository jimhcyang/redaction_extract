GENERAL

1. Peiping avoids suggesting agenda for Afro-Asian conference:

Comment: By refusing to contribute to the provisional agenda for the Afro-Asian conference, Peiping probably hopes to create the impression that it does not intend to influence the proceedings of the conference unduly. Provisional agenda items already suggested by other participants, however, will allow Chinese Communist representatives ample opportunity to present Peiping's views.

Propaganda has already indicated that Peiping's major efforts at the conference will be directed toward anticolonialism, gaining support for its claim to Formosa, prevention of military alliances among Asian nations, and the encouragement of "neutrality."
