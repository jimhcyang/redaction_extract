SOUTHEAST ASIA

4. Viet Minh working for stronger bonds with India:

Comment: The Viet Minh has already favorably impressed the Indian as well as the Canadian members of the international commission in Indochina with its orderly assumption of authority since the truce. The Indians have made a deliberate effort to view the Viet Minh in the best possible light. The Indian government, however, is unlikely to be so swayed by reports from its commission members as to abandon its position of neutrality in Indochina matters.
