Meanwhile, Diem has replied evasively to a hint from Bao Dai that he resign. He is now taking steps to reconstitute his cabinet which, as a result of recent resignations, now consists of little more than Diem himself.

7. French and British urge Laotian ultimatum to Pathets:

French and British officials in Vientiane plan to urge Premier Katay to make a last effort to obtain a reasonable settlement and, if the Pathets fail to agree within a short time, to break off negotiations. Katay will be advised to appeal then to the International Control Commission for a decision on the restoration of royal authority in the two northern provinces. If the commission fails to act effectively, Katay is to appeal to the Geneva signatories.
