18 March 1958

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC

USSR apparently engaged in construction activity, possibly missile-associated, in northern Urals.

II. ASIA-AFRICA

Indonesia - Government forces again control Medan.

Okinawa - Leftists win only 5 of 29 seats in Ryukyuan legislature.

Chiang Kai-shek threatens to break relations with Japan if Peiping trade mission is permitted to fly flag in Tokyo.
