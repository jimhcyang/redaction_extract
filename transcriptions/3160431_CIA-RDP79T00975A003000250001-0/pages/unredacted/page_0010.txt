8. CHOU EN-LAI DECIDES TO MEET WITH BURMESE PREMIER IN KUNMING

Comment on:

Premier Chou En-lai, contrary to an earlier decision, will make an effort to meet with Burmese premier U Nu on the border question when the latter visits Yunnan Province the end of this week. Originally, Vice Premier Ho Lung had been scheduled to do the honors.

Chou's decision is in line with Peiping's effort to maintain the appearance of reasonableness in regard to a border settlement and thus to forestall renewal of Rangoon's propaganda campaign on the problem.
