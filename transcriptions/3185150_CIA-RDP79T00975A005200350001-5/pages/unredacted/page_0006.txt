Neutralist former Premier Souvanna Phouma may have been behind the coup and he may soon be named premier.

Four members of the ousted Premier Somsanith's government, including Defense Minister General Phoumi, have reportedly arrived in northeast Thailand requesting political asylum. However, Somsanith and some of his ministers apparently remain in Luang Prabang. They claim the continued support of certain army units, but there is no confirmation of press reports that loyalist forces are moving toward Vientiane from Luang Prabang.
