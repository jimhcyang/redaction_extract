8. BULGANIN PREDICTS US PLANS FOR MIDDLE EAST WILL FAIL AFTER "SEVERE STRUGGLE"

Premier Bulganin sees the "wisdom" of Egypt's present position on American Middle East plans and that while he expects the plans to fail in the end, "a severe struggle will be needed."

Khrushchev considers the proposed American policies "a plan of thieves to plunder the Middle East!"

Comment
The USSR's main effort to counter the proposed American plans has been directed at persuading middle Eastern states to reject them. Moscow has also indicated that it is prepared to extend economic aid and to continue its military assistance to those states in the area that reject the American plans.
