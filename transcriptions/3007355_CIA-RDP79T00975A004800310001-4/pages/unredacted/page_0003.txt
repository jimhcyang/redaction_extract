7 DECEMBER 1959

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC
USSR plans to follow up present economic talks with Brazil in Moscow with high-level trade delegation to Brazil.

II. ASIA-AFRICA
Pakistan may have asked Peiping for negotiations on Kashmir border demarcation.
Iran--Shah says he will not go beyond his original offer to Moscow to guarantee that Iran will permit no foreign missile bases.

III. THE WEST
Belgium planning to reduce its NATO defense expenditures.
De Gaulle-Adenauer talks reached agreement on summit agenda, Berlin, and disarmament, but French views on NATO unchanged.
Promotion of controversial Portuguese Army officer may cause discontent in military leadership.
