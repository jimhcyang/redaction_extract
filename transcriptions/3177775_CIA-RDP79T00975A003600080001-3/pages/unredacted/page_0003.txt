10 MARCH 1958

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC
Moscow propaganda on SEATO conference extends nuclear-free zone proposal to Southeast Asia.
USSR continues to negotiate for additional arms shipments to Near East.
Belgrade breaks ideological truce with Moscow by attacking Marshal Konev.

II. ASIA- AFRICA
Indonesian Government asks for immediate evacuation of Americans from certain areas in Central Sumatra as military campaign apparently gets under way.
Egypt and Syria continue to emphasize charges that King Saud plotted assassination of Nasir and overthrow of UAR. Saud offers to meet with Nasir to refute charges.

III. THE WEST
French Embassy in Rabat fears Spain may take steps that would lead to conflict with Royal Moroccan Army, and that in any case Morocco may take issue to UN.
Cuba — Batista accepts offer by church-sponsored "Committee of National Harmony" to mediate between government and opposition.
