Comment: Current Israeli propaganda

While the Nasr regime has given full
support to the Arab line on Israel, it has from the beginning
emphasized a concept of national unity which avoids any sem-
blance of discrimination against minority groups.

Minority elements in Egypt have fre-

6. Comment on Popular Front victories in Greek municipal
elections:

Preliminary reports of Greek munic-
ipal elections on 21 November show that
the Communist-supported Popular Front
achieved notable successes over the Greek
Rally of Prime Minister Papagos. The
Front elected mayors in such important
places as Athens, Piraeus, Volos,
Kavalla, Larissa and Lamia.
