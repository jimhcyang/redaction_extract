25 MAY 1959

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC
Defense Minister Malinovsky to accompany Khrushchev on Albanian trip. (1)
Soviet diplomat stresses favorable prospects for agreements at Geneva. (2)
Peiping admits 1959 harvest may fall short of goals. (3)
USSR to give further aid to Indian oil industry. (4)

II. ASIA-AFRICA
UAR seizes cargo from Israeli chartered ship. (5)
Coup attempt in Sudan collapses. (6)
Violence possible in Uganda. (7)
Laos--Dissident Pathet battalion still at large. (8)
Singapore--PAP leader says he will refuse to form government unless six pro-Communist leaders are released. (9)

III. THE WEST
Growing nationalist trend undermining Coal-Steel Community. (10)
