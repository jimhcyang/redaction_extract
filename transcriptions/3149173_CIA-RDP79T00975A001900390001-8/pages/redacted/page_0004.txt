Ambassador Peurifoy warns that fighting might break out between retreating Chinese Nationalists and Thai police forces unless the Chinese surrender promptly on crossing the border. Should there be a large number of internees, Thailand may request reactivation of the joint military committee which last year handled the evacuation of 7,000 Chinese Nationalists to Formosa.

There are estimated to be 6,000 Chinese Nationalists, concentrated largely along the Thai border, remaining in Burma. While they are no match for the Burmese army, the terrain is admirably suited for evasive tactics, which the Chinese have been using successfully in the last two years.
