9. New French government threatens withdrawal from Indochina:

Following a conversation with Premier Laniel and his deputy Paul Reynaud, Ambassador Dillon in Paris warns that if a substantial increase in American assistance is not forthcoming immediately, the French government may feel obliged to negotiate with Ho Chi Minh looking to withdrawal from Indochina.
