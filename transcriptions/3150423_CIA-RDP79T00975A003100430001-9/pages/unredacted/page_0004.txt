1. SYRIAN PRESIDENT WEIGHS RESIGNATION

Comment
President Quwatli has previously threatened to resign. Since his position has become increasingly untenable, he may now actually do so unless he receives strong backing from either Nasr or King Saud. It is likely that Nasr will do little more than urge Quwatli to make accommodations with the left.

There are indications meanwhile that Quwatli has been in contact with Saud and that the king will support him and antileftist forces in Syria. Such support would forestall Quwatli's resignation, as well as induce Syrian moderates to force a showdown with the leftists. Rumors of a showdown are again appearing.

14 June 57 Current Intelligence Bulletin Page 3
