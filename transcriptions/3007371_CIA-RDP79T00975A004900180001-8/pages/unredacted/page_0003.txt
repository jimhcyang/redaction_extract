23 JANUARY 1960

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC
UAR general going to Moscow to discuss future procurement.

II. ASIA-AFRICA
Libya asks for new round of negotiations on US air base and aid.
Indonesian dissidents continue as disruptive force.

III. THE WEST
Latest French plans for nuclear testing.
De Gaulle believed ready to press for Algerian settlement despite army opposition.
Cypriot negotiators seen ready to accept indefinite postponement of independence rather than agree to present British demands.

Dominican Republic--Widespread arrests believed to have forestalled anti-regime plotting.
Venezuela--Demonstrations may take place on today's anniversary of Perez Jimenez' overthrow.
