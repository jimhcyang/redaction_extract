6 JUNE 1960

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC

Probable ICBM launched from Tyura Tam to usual Kamchatka impact area.

Chinese Communist artillery fires on Nationalist vessels in Chinmen area on 4 June; Peiping steps up propaganda charges of US-created "tension" in Taiwan Strait.

II. ASIA-AFRICA

Ghana's Nkrumah publicly calls South African Government "alien" and "temporary"; he is working to form a South African government-in-exile" in Accra.

III. THE WEST

Cuba likely to seize three American-owned refineries when firms announce, as they now plan, refusal to process Soviet crude.

Cuba cancels long-planned underdeveloped nations conference; move seen as public acknowledgment of plan's failure to attract much support either in Latin America or among Afro-Asian nations.
