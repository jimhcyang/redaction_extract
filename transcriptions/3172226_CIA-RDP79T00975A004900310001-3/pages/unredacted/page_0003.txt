8 FEBRUARY 1960

I. THE COMMUNIST BLOC

Chinese Communists send ships to bring back Overseas Chinese desiring to leave Indonesia. (1)

Chinese Communist observer's speech at Warsaw Pact conference reflects Peiping's continuing reservations about Khrushchev's policy of detente with the West. (2)

II. ASIA-AFRICA

III. THE WEST

Venezuela requests 8 February meeting of Organization of American States to consider Dominican activities in Caribbean. (3)

Latin American Communist leaders agree to avoid aggressive acts during President Eisenhower's visit but to make President feel "unwelcome" through other means. (4)
